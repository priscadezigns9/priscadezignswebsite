Google Unveils AI Health Coach to Challenge Apple Watch Dominance

Google has officially entered the 2026 AI health race with the launch of its native AI Health Coach. Integrated into the Google Health ecosystem, the assistant uses multi-modal data from Pixel Watch and Oura Ring integrations to provide predictive health interventions.

The move directly challenges Apple, which recently leaked its M4-tier requirements for the upcoming Apple Watch Pro AI Agents. As wearables shift from data logging to proactive coaching, the tech giants are locked in a high-stakes battle for the 'Personal Wellness OS'.
