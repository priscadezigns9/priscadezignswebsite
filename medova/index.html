The Problem

Caribbean private clinics are buried in admin work.

Too much time spent on paperwork means less time for patients. These four problems cost Caribbean clinics revenue, reputation, and staff sanity every single week.

⏳

Long Waiting Times

Paper-based check-ins, manual scheduling, and disorganised patient flow mean patients wait hours. They leave frustrated — and don't come back.

🚶

Missed Appointments

Without reminders or automated follow-ups, no-shows are rampant. Each missed appointment is lost revenue and a wasted slot that another patient needed.

📁

Paperwork Overload

Doctors spend hours writing notes, finding old records, and filling forms instead of treating patients. Administrative burden leads to burnout and errors.

💰

Billing Chaos

Manual invoicing, insurance errors, and lost receipts cost clinics thousands monthly. No visibility into what's been paid, what's pending, or what's overdue.

AI Intelligence

AI that helps doctors, not just administrators.

Medova's AI is designed to reduce the cognitive load on doctors and staff — so your team can focus on what actually matters: patient care.

📊
NO-SHOW PREDICTION

AI No-Show Prediction

Based on booking patterns, appointment history, and patient behaviour, Medova's AI flags which patients are likely to miss their appointment — giving your team time to follow up, fill the slot, or adjust the schedule before the day is wasted.

🚨
AUTO-TRIAGE

Auto-Triage System

When patients book, they describe their symptoms. Medova's AI analyses the intake form and flags urgent cases that need immediate attention — automatically moving critical patients to the top of the queue before they even arrive at your clinic.

📝
PATIENT SUMMARY

Patient History Summary

Before every appointment, doctors receive a 3-line AI summary of the patient's relevant history, recent visits, and active conditions — so they walk into the room informed, prepared, and able to spend less time reading and more time treating.
