Our Story

Made with
slow hands
and intention.

Neh Neh was born in Trinidad, out of a love for fabric, colour, and the stories women carry in what they wear. Every piece is hand-cut, hand-sewn, and finished with care — no mass production, no shortcuts.

Locally Sourced
Fabrics and materials chosen from Caribbean suppliers wherever possible.
Made to Last
Garments designed to outlast trends. Quality over quantity, always.
Wearable Identity
Caribbean femininity expressed through colour, pattern, and silhouette.
