Our story

Made in
Trinidad.

Neh Neh is a one-of-a-kind creative studio based in Fyzabad, Trinidad. We create original acrylic paintings that celebrate Caribbean life — and we dress the people who live it.

From carnival costumes to bridal gowns, custom swimwear to school uniforms — every piece is made by hand with intention, pride, and a deep love for this island.
