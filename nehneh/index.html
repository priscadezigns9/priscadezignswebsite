Our Story

Art You Can Wear.

NehNeh is a handcrafted fashion and art brand born in Trinidad & Tobago. Every piece is made with intention — from the fabric chosen to the final stitch. This is not fast fashion. This is wearable culture.

We create custom clothing, wearable art, and original commissions for people who refuse to blend in. Each piece is one of a kind, made to tell your story.

Caribbean roots. Global vision. Built for those who understand the value of something truly handmade.
