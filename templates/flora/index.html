I.
S E R V I C E S

Flora Studio is a floral practice working primarily with local ingredients. Based in our city, our work operates in the space between urban and wild.

Our work in floristry includes recurrent commercial accounts, design for specialty events, corporate gifting and styling for photoshoots. We study every stem, branch and bloom that we collect, contemplating the potential of its form — aiming to reflect people's perspectives on the miraculous abundance that is constantly underfoot and overhead.

Flora Studio was founded with the belief that arranging structures of sticks, mud and wildflowers is an obsession that has intensified after a decade as a florist. We are bound and fascinated by the natural forces of the world and believe our imperative, as practitioners who use what the earth has to make and survive, must be one that works in alignment with the ecosystem.

W H A T W E D O
01.
Weddings & Celebrations

Full-service floral design for ceremonies and receptions. From intimate gatherings to large-scale installations — every arrangement is composed with intention and grown from primarily local, seasonal material.

02.
Corporate & Events

Recurring commercial accounts, product launches, gallery openings, and hospitality design. We build long-term relationships with brands and venues who share our commitment to considered, high-fidelity spaces.

03.
Editorial & Photoshoots

Floristry direction for fashion, lifestyle, and commercial photography. We collaborate with photographers, art directors, and stylists to build living compositions that move — not just sit — within an image.

04.
Studio Subscriptions

Weekly or bi-weekly arrangements delivered to your home, office, or studio. Each subscription is composed from whatever the season has to offer — no two arrangements are ever the same.

05.
Installations

Large-scale botanical installations for architecture, retail, and hospitality. We work from first sketches through build day — structural, botanical, or somewhere in between.

06.
Private Workshops

Intimate hands-on workshops at our studio or your venue. We teach composition, sourcing, and the structural logic behind arrangements that feel effortless. Limited to 8 participants per session.

A deliberate path,
and our one true promise.

There are some projects that deeply align us — an obsession that has only grown in depth and direction. Whether or not it's the result of any collaboration, we care about the quality of what we are a part of, which means that we are proudly stand behind everything we produce and communicate.
