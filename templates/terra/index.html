The Terra Story

Born on the trail. Built for the world.

Terra began on a hiking trail in 2019 when our founder couldn't find footwear that was both high-performance and genuinely sustainable. Four years later, we've proven that you don't have to choose between comfort, style, and conscience.

We partner directly with certified suppliers across Portugal and Colombia — paying fair wages, eliminating middlemen, and keeping our footprint as light as possible.
