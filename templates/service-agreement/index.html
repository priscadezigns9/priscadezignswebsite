1. Scope of Work

Prisca Dezigns agrees to deliver the following services based on the package selected by the Client:

Template Website ($149.99 setup + $19.99/mo)

  • One (1) pre-designed template from the Prisca Dezigns catalogue, customised with Client-supplied content

  • Ongoing hosting for the duration of the active subscription

Micro Store ($249.99 setup + $34.99/mo)

  • All items included in the Template Website package
  • Product upload and configuration for up to 12 products
  • WhatsApp order button integration per product
  • Post-launch product additions billed at $9.99 per product

Premium 3D Template ($299.99 setup + $19.99/mo)

  • All items included in the Template Website package
  • High-fidelity 3D or WebGL-powered template with advanced animations
  • One (1) round of revisions included

Add-Ons (if selected)

  • Copywriting Add-On ($49.99): Prisca Dezigns writes all website copy (About, Services, and supporting text). Post-launch copy updates billed at $4.99 per request.
  • AI Chatbot Add-On ($349.99 setup + $49.99/mo): A custom AI chatbot trained on your business FAQs (hours, services, location, contact). Scope is strictly limited to your business information.

The Client agrees to provide the following within 48 hours of payment confirmation to ensure timely delivery:

  • Written content (About section, Services/Products, contact details) — unless the Copywriting Add-On was selected

Note: The 24-hour build guarantee begins only upon receipt of all required materials. Delays in material submission will delay delivery accordingly and do not constitute a breach by Prisca Dezigns.
