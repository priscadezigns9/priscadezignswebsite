Brand Voice Architect

Branding
"Create a brand voice document for [BRAND_NAME]. Include tone guide (2-3 sentences on personality), core vocabulary (15-20 key terms), communication style (formal/casual ratio), and 5 example statements. Target audience: [DEMOGRAPHIC]. Industry: [INDUSTRY]."

Builds a consistent voice framework for any brand from scratch.

Use Cases: Agency work, personal branding, product launches, rebranding projects.

Content Pillar Generator

Content Strategy
"Generate 7 content pillars for [BRAND/NICHE]. For each pillar: name, purpose (1 sentence), 3 sub-topics, and 2 content formats (blog/video/email). Target: [AUDIENCE]. Goal: [BUSINESS_OBJECTIVE]."

Maps an entire content strategy framework in minutes.

Use Cases: Content calendars, marketing audits, editorial strategy, social media planning.

SEO-Optimized Brief Builder

SEO & Writing
"Write a 500-word article brief optimized for '[KEYWORD]'. Include: H1 (with keyword), meta description (155 chars), 3-5 subheadings, keyword density targets (1.5-2%), internal linking opportunities, and 5 LSI keywords. Tone: [TONE]."

Creates briefs that writers and AI can execute flawlessly.

Use Cases: Blog post planning, technical writing, pillar content, SEO audits.

Launch Strategy Mapper

Strategy
"Create a 30-day launch timeline for [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. Include: Week 1 (awareness), Week 2 (engagement), Week 3 (conversion), Week 4 (retention). For each phase: channels, messaging, KPIs (3 per phase). Budget: [AMOUNT]. Audience size: [NUMBER]."

Converts abstract launches into executable roadmaps.

Use Cases: Product launches, campaign planning, event marketing, seasonal promotions.

Email Campaign Sequence

Email Marketing
"Design a 7-email nurture sequence for [AUDIENCE]. Email 1: Hook (subject line + opening), Email 2-4: Value (each with unique benefit), Email 5-6: Social proof (case study/testimonial), Email 7: CTA (offer). Goal: [CONVERSION_GOAL]. Tone: [TONE]."

Builds high-converting email sequences with proven frameworks.

Use Cases: Lead nurturing, customer onboarding, product education, re-engagement campaigns.

Social Media Content Matrix

Generates platform-specific social strategies with exact formatting.

Use Cases: Instagram/TikTok/LinkedIn planning, influencer content, seasonal campaigns, engagement growth.

Visualizes every interaction point in the customer lifecycle.
