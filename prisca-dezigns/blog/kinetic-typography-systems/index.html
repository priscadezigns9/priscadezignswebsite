Kinetic Typography: The Fluidity of Communication

PUBLISHED: 2026.05.26 // REF: CANVA NEWSROOM

Text is no longer static. In 2026, Kinetic Typography has evolved from a motion graphic technique into a fundamental brand language. As spatial interfaces (AR/VR) become more prevalent, type must breathe and move with the user's environment.

This "Liquid Type" adapts its weight and slant based on the context of the user—responding to movement, sound, or data inputs. It’s an evolution from variable fonts to reactive ones.

At Prisca Dezigns, we are integrating kinetic systems into the core identity of our partner brands. By moving beyond the fixed baseline, we allow brands to express urgency, calm, or complexity through the rhythmic movement of their own characters.
