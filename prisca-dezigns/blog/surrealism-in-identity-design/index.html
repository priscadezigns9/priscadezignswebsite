The Surrealism Pivot: Dreaming in Brand Identity

PUBLISHED: 2026.05.24 // REF: BRANDING JOURNAL

In a world of hyper-rational AI generation, the unexpected is the new premium. We are seeing a significant pivot toward "Branding Surrealism"—a movement that values dream-logic, impossible compositions, and absurd textures over photographic realism.

This shift is a direct response to the "uncanny valley" of early AI imagery. Instead of trying to look perfectly real, brands are choosing to look intentionally impossible. Textures like "liquid metal," "nebula fabric," and "synaptic glass" are becoming common in the visual vocabulary of 2026 boutique brands.

At Prisca Dezigns, we use these surrealist elements to create a sense of wonder and exclusivity, signaling to the audience that they are entering a space that transcends the ordinary.
