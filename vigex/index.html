The Problem

Caribbean security companies run on
WhatsApp and spreadsheets.

It's costing you clients, incidents, and credibility. Here's what's really happening on the ground.

📡
No Real-Time Visibility
You have no idea where your officers are or if they're actually doing their rounds. Clients know it too.
📋
Manual Scheduling Chaos
Spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and phone calls to cover shifts. One resignation breaks everything.
📝
Incident Reports Lost or Faked
Paper reports get lost, altered, or simply never filed. When something goes wrong, there's no audit trail.
📍
No GPS Patrol Verification
Officers say they did the rounds. Did they? Without GPS data, you're trusting their word — and so is your client.
📊
Zero Performance Analytics
No data on guard performance, incident frequency, or patrol compliance. You can't improve what you can't measure.
