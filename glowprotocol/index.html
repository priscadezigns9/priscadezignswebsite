THE FOUNDATION

Know Your Skin.
Change Everything.

Most people use the wrong products not because they don't care — but because they never learned what their skin actually is. Oily, dry, combination, sensitive, dehydrated — each type responds differently to the same ingredients.

💧 Dry Skin

Needs: ceramides, hyaluronic acid, thick moisturisers. Avoid: alcohol-heavy toners.

✨ Oily Skin

Needs: niacinamide, salicylic acid, gel moisturisers. Skip: heavy oils, comedogenic ingredients.

🌸 Combination

Needs: zone-specific care. Lightweight hydration overall, targeted treatment on T-zone.

🔴 Sensitive

Needs: fragrance-free, minimal actives, barrier-supporting ingredients like centella.

THE SCIENCE

The Ingredients That Actually Work

Skincare is full of buzzwords. These are the ones backed by real science.

Vitamin C
Brightening + Collagen

Look for: L-ascorbic acid 10–20%. Pairs beautifully with Vitamin E and ferulic acid for enhanced stability and results.

Pro tip: Avoid layering with AHAs in the same step — alternating times is smarter.

Retinol
Cell Turnover + Anti-Aging

Start at 0.025–0.1% and build slowly. Night use only — it degrades in sunlight. Purging (3–6 weeks) is normal.

Pro tip: Always pair with SPF the next morning. Non-negotiable.

Niacinamide
Pores + Oil Control + Barrier

5–10% is the sweet spot. Minimises pores, fades dark spots, regulates sebum. One of the most versatile actives in skincare.

Pro tip: Safe with almost everything. A true team player ingredient.

Hyaluronic Acid
Deep Hydration

Apply to damp skin for best absorption. Always seal with a moisturiser — HA draws water but needs help holding it in.

Pro tip: Multiple molecular weights = better penetration through all skin layers.

Salicylic Acid
Acne + Blackheads

0.5–2% concentration. Oil-soluble BHA that exfoliates inside the pore — unlike AHAs which only work on the surface.

Pro tip: Best for oily and acne-prone skin. Start 2–3x per week maximum.

SPF
Your Non-Negotiable

UVA + UVB (broad spectrum). Reapply every 2 hours in sunlight. The single most effective anti-aging product that exists — full stop.

Pro tip: SPF 30 blocks 97% of UVB. SPF 50 blocks 98%. Every day, not just summer.

HAIR SCIENCE

Your Hair Has a pH.
Are You Respecting It?

Healthy hair sits at pH 4.5–5.5. Most shampoos are alkaline — they open the cuticle, cause frizz, and weaken the strand over time. Knowing this one fact changes everything about how you shop.

1
Protein vs Moisture Balance
Over-proteined hair feels brittle and snaps. Over-moisturised hair feels mushy and stretches without bouncing back. You need both — in the right ratio for your porosity.
2
The LOC Method
For natural and curly hair: Liquid → Oil → Cream. Each layer seals in the moisture from the one before. This single method transformed millions of natural hair journeys.
3
Scalp Health First
Treat your scalp like your face. It needs exfoliation, hydration, and a clean environment. A healthy scalp = healthy hair growth. Everything else is secondary.

BODY CARE

Skin Below the Neck
Matters Too.

Your face gets all the attention — but your body has 2 square metres of skin that deserves a proper routine. Here's what actually works.

🪮
Dry Brush Before Your Shower

Boosts circulation, sloughs dead skin cells, and preps your skin to absorb products better. Use long strokes toward the heart.

🧴
Moisturise Within 3 Minutes

The 3-minute rule: apply body lotion immediately after showering while skin is still damp. You'll lock in 70% more moisture this way.

⚗️
Body Acids — Yes, Really

AHAs on the body (like lactic acid) tackle keratosis pilaris, strawberry legs, and uneven tone. Use 2× per week for best results.

☀️
SPF on Arms & Chest

The chest and arms are among the first places to show sun damage. If you spend time outdoors, this is non-negotiable — same as your face.
