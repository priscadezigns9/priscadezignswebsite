What is the best automatic watch under $100?

The OLEVS Skeleton Automatic ($89) is the most-recommended entry-level automatic in 2026 — genuine self-winding movement, sapphire-coated crystal, and a visible gear train that rivals watches 3x the price.

Are skeleton watches a good investment?

Skeleton watches from established movement houses hold value better than sealed-dial equivalents. The mechanical movement is the asset — visible movements make it easier to verify authenticity.
