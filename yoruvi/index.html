How It Works

From solo to squad
in three easy steps.

No awkward bios. No endless swiping. Just find your event, find your people, show up together.

🎟️

Pick your event or activity

Browse concerts, festivals, brunches, gallery nights, hikes, and more in your city. Or add your own — YORUVI discovers what's happening and what people want to do.

🤝

Match with people going too

YORUVI shows you real, verified people who are also going (or want to go) to that same event. Match one-to-one or form a whole group — you choose.

🎉

Show up together. No awkwardness.

Chat, plan, coordinate. Then go experience the thing you love — with people who love it too. It's not dating. It's just life, shared.
