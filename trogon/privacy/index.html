Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Trogon, we may collect:

  • Account information: name, email address, password
  • Social media tokens: OAuth tokens for connected platforms (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, etc.)
  • Content data: posts, captions, images, and schedules you create in Trogon
  • Usage data: how you interact with the Service, features used, and time spent
  • Device data: browser type, IP address, and device identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Trogon platform
  • Schedule and publish content to your connected social media accounts
  • Send service-related notifications and updates
  • Provide customer support
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. TikTok Data

When you connect your TikTok account, Trogon accesses your TikTok data solely to provide the scheduling and publishing features you request. We do not sell your TikTok data to third parties. We store only the access tokens necessary to perform actions on your behalf. You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time from your Trogon settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: third-party services that help us operate Trogon (hosting, analytics, payments)
  • Social media platforms: when publishing content on your behalf
  • Legal authorities: when required by law

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data including encryption in transit and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Disconnect any connected social media account

8. Cookies

Trogon uses cookies to maintain your session and improve your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Trogon is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through the Service.

11. Contact Us
