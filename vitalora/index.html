AI Intelligence

AI that makes your
clinic run smarter.

Three AI systems embedded in every Vitalora clinic — working quietly in the background to reduce admin, predict problems, and help doctors deliver better care.

📊
AI Module
AI No-Show Prediction
Vitalora AI analyzes booking patterns, patient history, and appointment type to predict which patients are likely to no-show. Automated reminders are sent based on risk level — SMS, WhatsApp, or email — reducing empty slots and lost revenue before they happen.
🩺
AI Module
Auto-Triage
When patients book online, AI asks structured intake questions and automatically categorizes urgency. Urgent cases are flagged for same-day slots. Routine checkups are routed appropriately. Your reception staff spend less time triaging and more time with patients.
📋
AI Module
Patient History Summary
Before every appointment, Vitalora AI generates a concise summary of the patient's last 5 visits, current medications, outstanding tests, and key risk flags. Doctors start every consultation fully briefed — in seconds, not by scrolling through pages of old notes.
