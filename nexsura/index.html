AI Intelligence Layer

AI that works before you do.

Three AI modules that operate in the background — detecting fraud, extracting data, and routing claims — so your team focuses on decisions, not admin.

AI Fraud Detection
Cross-references every claim against known Caribbean fraud patterns, historical claim data, and behavioural signals. Flags suspicious submissions with a risk score before any human reviews them — giving adjusters the information they need before they even open the file.
