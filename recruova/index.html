The Problem

Bad hires are expensive. Slow hiring is costly.
Caribbean companies face both.

💸

Bad Hires Are Costly

The wrong person in the role costs months of salary, management time, and team morale — plus the time and cost to replace them. One bad hire can set a small business back six months.

📂

Application Overload

200 CVs arrive for one role. There's no way to review them all properly. Qualified candidates get missed. The wrong people get shortlisted. Hiring managers are overwhelmed.

⏳

Slow Hiring Process

Great candidates drop out while waiting for responses. Competitors move faster. The best talent accepts another offer while your approval process is still in progress.

🗂️

Onboarding Chaos

New hires arrive on day one confused, under-equipped, and unsupported. No structured process means a slow start, low confidence, and higher early attrition.
