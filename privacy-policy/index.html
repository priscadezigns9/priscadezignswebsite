Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 17, 2025

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Prisca Dezigns ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our website and internal tools, including any integrations with third-party platforms such as Pinterest.

2. App Purpose

Our Pinterest API integration is developed and used exclusively for personal and organisational purposes by Prisca Dezigns. It is used to manage, schedule, and publish content to Pinterest boards owned by Prisca Dezigns. This application is not distributed publicly and does not collect data from third-party Pinterest users.

3. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from visitors to our website. Our internal tools may access the following data solely for operational purposes:

  • Pinterest account credentials and page tokens (stored securely, never shared)
  • Board and pin metadata from our own Pinterest account
  • Website analytics (page views, traffic sources) via standard tools

4. How We Use Information

Any data accessed through third-party APIs (including Pinterest) is used solely to:

  • Publish and manage pins on our own Pinterest boards
  • Automate content scheduling for our own account
  • Monitor performance of our own pins and boards

We do not sell, share, or distribute any data to third parties.

5. Data Storage & Security

All API tokens and credentials are stored securely and are accessible only to authorised personnel within Prisca Dezigns. We follow industry-standard security practices to prevent unauthorised access.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website and tools may interact with the following third-party platforms:

  • Pinterest (content publishing)
  • Facebook / Instagram (content publishing)
  • Amazon Associates (affiliate links)
  • GitHub (website hosting and version control)

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We are not responsible for their data practices.

7. Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies for analytics purposes. No personally identifiable information is collected through cookies.

8. Your Rights

As this application is for internal organisational use only, there is no personal data collected from external users. If you have any questions, please contact us directly.
