AI HR Assistant

An intelligent HR layer that handles incident reports, employee queries, and compliance documentation — automatically, accurately, and around the clock.

Overview

The AI HR Assistant takes over the most time-consuming parts of HR administration. It guides employees through structured incident report forms via chat, categorises severity, flags urgent cases, and stores OSH Act-ready documentation directly to your system — all without HR staff having to manually process a single report.

It's not a generic form. It's trained on your HR policies and compliance requirements, so every interaction is consistent, confidential, and legally sound.

What It Does

Guide employees through incident and grievance reports via chat
Categorise reports by severity and type automatically
Flag urgent cases and escalate to HR management instantly
Store OSH Act-compliant documentation to your database
Answer common HR policy questions 24/7
Handle onboarding FAQs for new employees
Maintain a complete, searchable log of all interactions
Operate across website, WhatsApp, or internal portals

Incident Reporting: Workplace injuries, near-misses, and safety violations — captured, classified, and stored automatically.
Grievance Filing: Employees submit formal complaints through a guided, confidential chat flow.
