AI Lead Qualifier

A conversational sales agent that engages every enquiry, separates serious buyers from browsers, and delivers hot prospects directly to your team — ready to close.

Overview

The AI Lead Qualifier transforms your chatbot into a high-performance sales front line. The moment a prospect reaches out — via your website, WhatsApp, or email — the AI engages them in a natural conversation, walks through your offer, identifies their budget and intent, handles objections, and scores them as hot, warm, or cold.

Only the high-intent, qualified prospects get escalated to your team. You stop wasting hours on tyre-kickers and start every sales conversation already knowing the lead is serious.

What It Does

Engage every incoming enquiry instantly — no delay, no missed messages
Walk prospects through your offer with a natural conversation flow
Ask qualifying questions — budget, timeline, decision-making authority
Handle common objections and rebuttals automatically
Score leads as hot, warm, or cold based on your criteria
Escalate hot leads to your sales team with a full summary
Book discovery calls or demos directly into your calendar
Nurture warm leads with follow-up sequences

Key Features

Custom qualification criteria built around your ideal client profile
Multi-channel deployment — website, WhatsApp, and email
Real-time lead scoring and CRM push
Objection-handling scripts trained on your sales process
