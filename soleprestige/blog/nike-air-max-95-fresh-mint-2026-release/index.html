Nike Air Max 95 "Fresh Mint" 2026

Sole Prestige | June 1, 2026 Release

The legend is back. On June 1st, 2026, Nike is officially resurrecting one of the most beloved colorways in the Air Max archive: the "Fresh Mint" Air Max 95. For collectors and casual enthusiasts alike, this drop marks a return to the golden era of Sergio Lozano's human anatomy-inspired design.

The Anatomy of a Classic

The 2026 iteration stays true to the original's layered aesthetic. The signature grey-to-white gradient on the side panels is executed with surgical precision, while the "Fresh Mint" accents on the eyelets, tongue logo, and Air units provide that iconic pop of neon-adjacent energy.

Why It Matters

In a sneaker market currently dominated by minimalists and "dad shoes," the Air Max 95 remains a bold statement. The Fresh Mint colorway, in particular, is celebrated for its versatility—blending effortlessly with tech-wear or luxury street styles.
