Welcome to Prisca Dezigns. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you maintain absolute sovereignty over your data. 2. Data Sovereignty

Our core principle is: "You own the data." Unlike traditional platforms, Prisca Dezigns utilizes non-custodial architecture and decentralized identity solutions. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Information We Collect

4. Use of Information

We use your information strictly to:

  • Provide and maintain our AI-powered services.

5. Data Storage and Security

Most of your data is stored on-chain or within end-to-end encrypted neural links. We utilize industry-standard 15px Focus Blur security protocols and Ivory-theme-compliant encryption layers to protect your information.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and export your data at any time.
