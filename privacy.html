Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 20, 2026  ·  Jurisdiction: Republic of Trinidad and Tobago

Prisca Dezigns is committed to protecting the privacy, security, and dignity of every individual whose data passes through our platform. This policy governs how we and our AI employees collect, process, store, and protect personal information in full compliance with the Trinidad and Tobago Data Protection Act, Chap. 22:02, the Electronic Transactions Act, Chap. 22:03, and the Cybercrime Act, 2015.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal and professional information that is voluntarily provided through our communication channels, including AI-driven interfaces (WhatsApp, website chat, email), consultation forms, and onboarding workflows. This may include:

  • Full name and contact details (phone number, email address)
  • Business name, industry, and service requirements
  • Conversation content submitted through AI chat interfaces
  • Appointment and scheduling data
  • Workplace incident information (where HSE services are deployed)
  • Lead qualification and scoring data generated by our AI systems
Basis for collection: By engaging with any Prisca Dezigns AI interface — including Sierra (AI Receptionist), Sunil (AI IT Support), Jamal (AI Finance), Alice (AI HR), and Dean (AI Marketing) — you acknowledge that your interaction data may be stored and processed in accordance with this policy. This constitutes informed consent under the TT Data Protection Act.

2. How Our AI Employees Process Data

Our AI employees operate under a strict Autonomy Boundary Rule. Every action they perform is fully automatable, ethical, and legally compliant. They do not make unilateral decisions on tasks that require human, legal, or regulatory sign-off. Specifically:

Sierra — AI Receptionist & Channel Manager

Sierra processes inbound messages, qualifies leads, captures contact details, books appointments, and sends follow-up communications. All data captured by Sierra is stored within your designated CRM or Prisca Dezigns' secure database. Sierra does not share contact data with third parties outside of your authorised CRM integration.

Sunil — AI IT Support Officer

Sunil logs IT support tickets, routes requests, and coordinates user onboarding workflows. Sunil does not autonomously grant or revoke system access — all access-related actions are routed to your designated IT administrator for approval.

Jamal — AI Finance Administrator

Jamal processes invoice data, tracks AR/AP records, and generates financial reports. Jamal does not file, submit, or certify any financial document on behalf of a regulated body. All outputs are prepared for human review and submission.

Alice — AI HR Assistant

Alice handles recruitment screening, onboarding coordination, and HR documentation. Alice does not make employment decisions or handle legally binding HR actions without human approval.

Dean — AI Marketing Data Administrator

Dean manages marketing contact lists, lead scoring, and campaign performance data. All audience data is processed in accordance with this privacy policy and is never sold or transferred to third parties.

3. Follow-Up Communications

Sierra may send automated follow-up messages to contacts who have previously engaged with your business through an AI-managed channel. Follow-up sequences are only triggered for contacts who have initiated a conversation — we do not send unsolicited messages to cold contacts.

This practice is compliant with the TT Electronic Transactions Act, Chap. 22:03, which permits electronic communications where a prior business relationship or inquiry has been established.

Opt-out: Any contact may request to stop receiving follow-up communications at any time by replying "STOP" or by contacting the business directly. All opt-out requests are processed immediately.

4. Workplace & HSE Data

Where our AI HSE employee is deployed, workplace incident data — including employee names, incident descriptions, severity classifications, and timestamps — is captured and formatted into structured reports.

Important: Our AI prepares and structures OSH Act-compliant incident documentation. However, the submission of these reports to the Occupational Safety and Health Authority (OSHA TT) or any statutory body is the sole responsibility of the authorised human operator (e.g. your Safety Officer or HR Manager). Our AI does not file, certify, or submit reports on your behalf to any regulatory authority.

Workplace incident data is treated as sensitive personal information and is stored with restricted access within your designated database only.

5. AI Sales Interactions

Our AI sales interfaces (including Sierra's Sales Logic add-on) are programmed to present factual, accurate information about a business's products and services. Our AI:

  • Does not use psychological pressure tactics, manufactured urgency, or deceptive framing
  • Does not make promises or guarantees that have not been authorised by the client business
  • Escalates all high-intent or complex sales conversations to a human representative for closing
  • Identifies itself as an AI assistant when directly and sincerely asked

This is in line with our ethical mandate and the consumer protection principles embedded in TT law.

6. Third-Party CRM & Integrations

Where our AI employees are integrated with a third-party CRM (e.g. HubSpot, GoHighLevel, Zoho), data is transferred to that platform under the terms of that platform's own privacy and data processing agreements. Prisca Dezigns acts as a data processor on behalf of the client business, which remains the data controller.

We do not integrate with platforms that do not maintain their own published data protection policies.

7. Data Retention

Personal data collected through our AI interfaces is retained for as long as is necessary to fulfil the service agreement with the client business, or as required by applicable law. Upon termination of a service agreement, client data is deleted or returned within 30 days upon written request.

Conversation logs and lead data within our systems are retained for a maximum of 24 months unless a shorter period is agreed upon during onboarding.

8. Your Rights

Under the TT Data Protection Act, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request that inaccurate data be corrected
  • Deletion — request that your personal data be erased where it is no longer necessary
  • Objection — object to the processing of your data for marketing or follow-up purposes
  • Portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@priscadezigns.org. We will respond within 14 business days.

9. Data Sovereignty

You maintain full ownership of your data at all times. Prisca Dezigns does not sell, trade, license, or transfer your personally identifiable information to any third party outside of the integrations you explicitly authorise during onboarding.

All data processed by Prisca Dezigns AI systems remains within platforms that operate under internationally recognised data security standards (SOC 2, ISO 27001, or equivalent).
