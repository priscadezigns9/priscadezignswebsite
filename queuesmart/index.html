Features

Everything you need to manage your queue

Simple tools that actually work for small businesses, salons, clinics, and government offices.

📱
Join by phone or QR
Customers scan a QR code or text a number to join. No app required — works on any phone.
🔔
SMS notifications
Customers get a text when it's almost their turn, so they can wait anywhere — not in your lobby.
📊
Live dashboard
See your full queue in real time. Call the next customer, add notes, and track wait times.
📅
Appointment booking
Let customers book specific time slots so you can plan your day in advance.
⭐
After-visit reviews
Automatically send a review request after each visit. Build your reputation on autopilot.
📈
Analytics & reports
Track peak hours, average wait times, and busiest days to staff your business smarter.
How it works

Up and running in minutes

No tech skills needed. If you can send a WhatsApp, you can run QueueSmart.

1
Create your queue
Sign up and set up your business profile. Paste your QR code at the door or share your link online.
2
Customers join
They scan, enter their name and number, and they're in. They get a confirmation text instantly.
3
You call them in
When ready, tap "Next" on your dashboard. They get a text alert and walk in — no shouting required.
