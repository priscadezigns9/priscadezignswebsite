Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 8, 2026

1. Chat Logs

Your conversations with Hartaly are stored with end-to-end encryption. They are private and never sold to third parties.

2. Mood Data

Mood tracking data is used to provide you with insights. This data remains anonymous and is never used for advertising.

3. Data Deletion

You have the absolute right to delete your entire history at any time through the application settings.

4. HIPAA Compliance

While Hartaly is not a clinical tool, we maintain HIPAA-adjacent security standards for data privacy and encryption.
