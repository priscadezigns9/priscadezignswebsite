Features

Your money, finally
making sense.

Built around how people in Trinidad & Tobago actually spend, earn, and save.

📊
Spending tracker
Every transaction categorized automatically — groceries, transport, entertainment, bills. See where your money actually goes.
💡
Smart budgets
Set monthly budgets for each category. Get alerts before you overspend — not after it's too late.
🎯
Savings goals
Set a goal — car, vacation, emergency fund — and Dollo maps out exactly how to get there from your income.
📱
Bank sync
Connect your TT bank accounts and cards. Transactions import automatically — no manual entry needed.

Financial insights
Weekly and monthly reports showing your trends, biggest expenses, and how this month compares to last.

Bill reminders
TSTT, WASA, T&TEC, insurance — never miss a payment again. Reminders sent before due dates.
