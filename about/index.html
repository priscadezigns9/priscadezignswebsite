About Prisca Dezigns

Prisca Dezigns is a global visual identity and AI architecture firm headquartered in Trinidad and Tobago, serving clients worldwide. We build premium niche brands and deliver world-class digital experiences across fashion, lifestyle, tech, gaming, beauty, and culture.

What We Do

We design, build, and grow digital brands. From full-stack web development and brand identity to AI-driven content systems and social media infrastructure, Prisca Dezigns handles the complete digital lifecycle of a brand.

Our Empire

Beyond client services, we operate a portfolio of 15+ independent niche brands — each a premium editorial destination in its category. From Dreaming Anime to Essence Elite to The Autodrome, our brands reach audiences across fashion, fragrance, gaming, fitness, travel, and more.
