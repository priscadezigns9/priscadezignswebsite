Our Story

South TT's Own

redd's. has been serving San Fernando's best fried chicken right out of 39 Cocoyea Village, Naparima Mayaro Road. Not a franchise. Not a copy. The real thing — built in the community, for the community.

Two flavours. Spicy and regular. Hand-seasoned, fresh-fried daily. The kind of food that keeps people coming back every single week — and driving in from Penal, Marabella, and beyond just to get a piece.

Featured on Foodie Nation. Loved by South TT. Open every Sunday from 10:30 AM — call ahead for weekday hours.
