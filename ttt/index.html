Opportunity for Digital Transformation

Issue 01
Opportunity for Instant Digital Engagement

Issue 02
Opportunity to Capture Every Advertising Enquiry
Advertising enquiries can arrive at any time. AI helps ensure that every enquiry is acknowledged immediately, qualified, and routed appropriately, allowing the sales team to follow up with full context.
Issue 03
Free Staff for Higher-Value Work
Routine enquiries such as programming schedules, advertising information, and general questions can be handled automatically, allowing TTT's team to focus on strategic initiatives and complex customer interactions.
Issue 04
Opportunity to Automate Repetitive Data Processes
Where repetitive administrative processes exist, AI can assist with structured data processing, validation, and reporting—reducing manual effort while improving consistency and accuracy.

The Real Business Cost
Two roles. Over TT$100,000 per year. And they both go offline at 5pm.
