Executive Summary

Helping TTT Communicate Faster, Work Smarter, and Serve Better

TTT is one of Trinidad and Tobago's most recognized media organizations. As customer expectations continue to grow, audiences and advertisers expect quick, accurate, and consistent communication across every channel.

Prisca Dezigns proposes implementing an AI-powered communication and business automation solution that helps TTT respond faster, reduce repetitive administrative work, and improve operational efficiency while allowing employees to focus on higher-value responsibilities.

Rather than replacing staff, the proposed solution is designed to act as a "Digital Workforce" that handles the volume, so your team can focus on creativity and strategy.

The Challenge

The Bottleneck of Modern Media

In a high-speed media environment, manual processes create friction. When advertisers have to wait for enquiry responses or when administrative tasks slow down production, the entire network's agility is compromised.

Enquiry Volume

High volumes of repetitive enquiries via WhatsApp, Email, and Phone slow down response times.

Manual Data Entry

Routine administrative work pulls skilled employees away from high-value media tasks.

24/7 Availability

Media never sleeps, but traditional customer service is limited by office hours.

The Strategy

The Three AI Solutions

1. AI Customer Communication

Immediate responses to common enquiries across digital channels, ensuring every advertiser and viewer is acknowledged instantly.

  • Website AI
  • WhatsApp AI
  • Email AI
  • 24/7 Availability
2. AI Workforce

Human-grade virtual employees that handle routine voice conversations and administrative role-play.

  • AI Receptionist
  • AI Sales Representative
  • AI Appointment Scheduler
  • AI Accounts Receivable Agent
3. Intelligent Business Operations

Automates repetitive office processes by processing documents and generating intelligence reports.

  • AI Data Entry
  • AI Data Processing
  • AI Reporting
