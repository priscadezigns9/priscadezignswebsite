Section 03

The Problem

TTT's digital audience has grown significantly. Its ability to respond to that audience has not scaled with it.

Issue 01
No automated viewer response
Viewers who contact TTT through the website or WhatsApp receive no immediate acknowledgement. Questions about programming, schedules, and content go unanswered until a staff member is available — often the next business day.
Issue 02
Advertiser leads go cold
Potential advertisers who reach out after hours or during peak periods may wait days for a response. In that window, they often move to another media outlet. Each delayed response is a potential revenue loss with no recovery mechanism.
Issue 03
Staff time spent on first-contact tasks
Answering routine inquiries — rates, programme schedules, ad package information — consumes significant staff bandwidth. These are tasks an AI system can handle instantly, freeing the sales and operations team for higher-value work.
Issue 04
550K followers with no automated engagement path
TTT's Facebook and YouTube audiences are substantial. But there is no automated system to convert a viewer's curiosity into a website visit, a lead form submission, or an advertiser inquiry. The connection between audience and revenue is manual.
The Business Cost
Every unresponded inquiry is a missed conversion.
Whether it is an advertiser looking to spend TT$15,000 on a campaign or a viewer asking about a programme — the first response sets the standard for TTT's professionalism.

Tier 1
Website Chatbot
An AI assistant embedded directly on TTT's website. It handles viewer and advertiser inquiries, qualifies leads, and captures contact details — automatically, around the clock.

+ TT$1,050 / month
What's Included
  • AI chatbot built and deployed on TTT's website
  • Custom conversation flows for viewers and advertisers

  • Lead forwarding to the correct TTT team member
  • 24/7 availability — no staff required after hours
  • Brand-matched tone and voice
  • Go-live support — all issues corrected at no charge

A viewer lands on ttt.live with a question about a programme. Instead of finding a general contact email and waiting, they interact with the AI assistant — which answers immediately, 24 hours a day.

An advertiser visits and wants to know about ad packages. The chatbot qualifies their interest, captures their details, and forwards a structured lead to TTT's sales team — without a single staff member involved.

Result: TTT is available to viewers and advertisers around the clock at no additional staffing cost.

Best for: Testing AI automation with minimal commitment
