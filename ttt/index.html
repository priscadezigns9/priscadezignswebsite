Section 03

Opportunity for Digital Transformation

TTT's digital audience has grown significantly. Its ability to respond at scale has not.

Issue 01
Opportunity for Instant Digital Engagement
Outside of normal business hours, viewers may not receive an immediate response to their enquiries. AI ensures every enquiry is acknowledged instantly and directed appropriately, regardless of the time of day.
Issue 02
Opportunity to Capture Every Advertising Enquiry
Advertising enquiries can arrive at any time. AI helps ensure that every enquiry is acknowledged immediately, qualified, and routed appropriately, allowing the sales team to follow up with full context.
Issue 03
Free Staff for Higher-Value Work
Routine enquiries such as programming schedules, advertising information, and general questions can be handled automatically, allowing TTT's team to focus on strategic initiatives and complex customer interactions.
Issue 04
Opportunity to Automate Repetitive Data Processes
Where repetitive administrative processes exist, AI can assist with structured data processing, validation, and reporting—reducing manual effort while improving consistency and accuracy.
Hidden Cost — Customer Service
The Cost of Repetitive Manual Processes
Based on typical customer service salaries in Trinidad & Tobago, employing a dedicated representative can cost approximately TT$4,000–TT$5,000 per month, before employer contributions and other employment-related costs. Including the mandatory 12% NIS contribution, the estimated annual employment cost ranges from approximately TT$53,760 to TT$67,200.
Hidden Cost — Data Entry
The Business Value of Intelligent Automation
Based on current Trinidad & Tobago market rates, a dedicated data entry role typically ranges between TT$4,000 and TT$4,500 per month before employer contributions. AI data automation offers organisations an opportunity to reduce repetitive administrative work while maintaining consistent processing around the clock.
The Real Business Cost
Two roles. Over TT$100,000 per year. Business hours are limited. Customer expectations are not.
As communication volumes grow, AI provides TTT with an opportunity to scale customer engagement efficiently while allowing staff to focus on work that requires human expertise and decision-making.
