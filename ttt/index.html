Executive Summary

The Opportunity in Brief
TTT is Trinidad & Tobago's most trusted media institution. Its communication infrastructure has not kept pace with its reach.

TTT Limited — with 550,000+ Facebook followers, 265,000 YouTube subscribers, and decades of broadcast authority — commands a digital audience that continues to grow. Yet viewers and advertisers who attempt to engage through TTT's website, WhatsApp, email, or phone often experience delays, or no response at all. This is not a staffing problem. It is a systems problem.

Prisca Dezigns proposes to solve it permanently. This proposal outlines a tiered AI communication system that handles viewer inquiries, qualifies advertiser leads, manages email traffic, answers inbound calls, and processes data — all automatically, without additional headcount. TTT's team continues to do what they do best. The AI handles every first interaction, 24 hours a day.

Four engagement tiers are presented — from a focused website chatbot to a fully autonomous communication and data operations layer. A fifth tier, featuring AI deal-closing and invoice generation, is currently in development and will be available to early adopters first.

Section 03

The Problem

TTT's digital audience has grown significantly. Its ability to respond to that audience at scale has not.

Issue 01
No automated viewer response
Viewers who contact TTT through the website or WhatsApp receive no immediate acknowledgement. Questions about programming, schedules, and content wait until a staff member is available — often the next business day.
Issue 02
Advertiser leads go cold
Potential advertisers who reach out after hours or during busy periods may wait days for a response. In that window, they often move to another outlet. Each delayed response is revenue with no recovery mechanism.
Issue 03
Staff time spent on first-contact work
Answering routine inquiries — rates, schedules, ad package information — consumes significant staff time every day. These are tasks an AI system handles instantly, freeing the team for higher-value decisions.
Issue 04
Data entry handled manually
Advertiser bookings, ad logs, scheduling records, and operational data are likely entered and maintained by hand. This creates room for error, delays reporting, and consumes hours that could be redirected to revenue-generating work.
The Business Cost
Every unresponded inquiry is a missed conversion. Every manual data entry is a preventable cost.
The solution is not to hire more people. It is to deploy a system that never sleeps, never misses a message, and processes data automatically — every night.

Implementation Process

From first meeting to go-live — a clear, structured process designed to require minimal time from TTT's team at every stage.

01
Step 1 · Before Any Commitment
Proposal Review & Initial Meeting
TTT reviews this proposal. A follow-up meeting is arranged to walk through any questions — by phone, video, or in person. No financial commitment is made at this stage.
02
Step 2 · Before Any Payment
Full Audit of TTT's Communication Channels
Prisca Dezigns conducts a comprehensive audit of TTT's current inquiry volume — website traffic, WhatsApp message frequency, email load, call volume, and data entry workload. The final monthly retainer is determined from this audit. TTT knows the exact cost before signing anything.
03
Step 3
Contract Signing & 50% Deposit
Formal service agreement issued and signed by both parties. The 50% commencement deposit is paid via Scotiabank transfer. Work begins within 24 hours of deposit confirmation.
04
Week 1
TTT Sends All Relevant Information
TTT provides all required assets and access: brand guidelines, website credentials, WhatsApp Business account, email access (if Tier 3+), call system details (if Tier 4), FAQs, rate cards, and any existing data formats. Prisca Dezigns handles all technical setup from this point independently.
05
Weeks 2–(3, 5, or 7 depending on tier)
Build & Internal Testing
The AI system is configured with TTT's tone, FAQ database, lead flows, escalation rules, and data processing pipelines. Full internal testing is completed before TTT sees anything — no half-finished work is ever presented.
06
Final Week Before Go-Live
TTT Review & Adjustments
TTT's team reviews the system in a live demo. Feedback is collected and all adjustments are made within 48 hours. Up to two rounds of revisions are included in the setup fee.
07
Go-Live
Deployment · Training · Final Payment · 30-Day Support
System goes live. Staff training session completed (1 hour). Full documentation provided. Final 50% payment is due at this stage, before credential handover. A 30-day support window begins — all issues resolved within 24 hours at no additional charge. Monthly retainer billing starts on day 31.
