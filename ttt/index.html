Institution Background

The Opportunity

As a central pillar of the Caribbean media landscape, TTT Limited manages a high volume of viewer interactions and administrative data. Modernising these workflows represents a significant operational advantage.

The Communications Challenge
Manual handling of viewer inquiries, advertiser leads, and institutional support creates significant operational friction. A single customer service representative in Trinidad costs between TT$4,000–$5,000/month in salary alone — add NIS contributions and overhead, and the true cost exceeds TT$65,000 per year. AI serves as a high-performance support layer that eliminates these delays.
