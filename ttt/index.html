The Opportunity in Brief
TTT is Trinidad & Tobago's most trusted media institution. Its communication infrastructure has not kept pace with its reach.

Section 03

The Problem

TTT's digital audience has grown significantly. Its ability to respond at scale has not.

Issue 01
No automated viewer response
Viewers who contact TTT through the website or WhatsApp receive no immediate acknowledgement. Questions about programming and schedules wait until a staff member is available — often the next business day.
Issue 02
Advertiser leads go cold
Potential advertisers who reach out after hours may wait days for a response. In that window, they often move to another outlet. Each delayed response is revenue with no recovery mechanism.
Issue 03
Staff time lost on first-contact work
Answering routine inquiries — rates, schedules, ad package information — consumes significant staff time every day. These are tasks an AI system handles instantly, freeing the team for higher-value decisions.
Issue 04
Data entry handled manually
Advertiser bookings, ad logs, scheduling records, and operational data are entered by hand — creating room for error, delaying reporting, and consuming hours that could go toward revenue-generating work.
Hidden Cost — Customer Service
What a customer service rep actually costs TTT
A salaried customer service rep at a media organisation like TTT is realistically TT$4,000–$5,000/month. Add the mandatory 12% NIS employer contribution and the true annual cost sits between TT$53,760 and TT$67,200/year — before sick days, vacation leave, or gratuity. And critically: that person goes offline at 5pm. The WhatsApp does not.
Hidden Cost — Data Entry
What manual data entry actually costs TTT
The legal minimum wage is TT$20.50/hr (Ministry of Labour, 2024). At 173 hrs/month plus 12% NIS, the floor cost is TT$47,724/year. In practice, a data entry role at TTT's scale pays closer to TT$4,000–$4,500/month — bringing the true annual cost to TT$53,760–$60,480. The AI Data module handles the same workload automatically, every night, at a fixed predictable cost.
The Real Business Cost
Two roles. Over TT$100,000 per year. And they both go offline at 5pm.
The solution is not to hire more people. It is to deploy a system that never sleeps, never misses a message, never calls in sick, and processes data automatically — every single night.

Implementation Process

From first meeting to go-live — designed to require minimal time from TTT's team at every stage.

01
Step 1 · Before Any Commitment
Proposal Review & Initial Meeting
TTT reviews this proposal. A follow-up meeting is arranged to walk through any questions — by phone, video, or in person. No financial commitment is made at this stage.
02
Step 2 · Before Any Payment
Full Audit of TTT's Communication Channels
Prisca Dezigns audits TTT's current inquiry volume — website traffic, WhatsApp frequency, email load, call volume, and data entry workload. The final monthly retainer is determined from this audit. TTT knows the exact cost before signing anything.
03
Step 3
Contract Signing & 50% Deposit
Formal service agreement issued and signed. The 50% commencement deposit is paid via Scotiabank transfer. Work begins within 24 hours of deposit confirmation.
04
Week 1
TTT Sends All Relevant Information
TTT provides brand guidelines, website credentials, WhatsApp Business account, email access (Tier 3+), call system details (Tier 4), FAQs, rate cards, and data formats. Prisca Dezigns handles all technical setup independently from this point.
05
Weeks 2–(3, 5, or 7 depending on tier)
Build & Internal Testing
The AI system is configured with TTT's tone, FAQ database, lead flows, escalation rules, and data pipelines. Full internal testing is completed before TTT sees anything.
06
Final Week Before Go-Live
TTT Review & Adjustments
TTT's team reviews the system in a live demo. All adjustments made within 48 hours. Up to two rounds of revisions included in the setup fee.
07
Go-Live
Deployment · Go-Live Briefing · Final Payment
System goes live. Final 50% payment due before credential handover. Prisca Dezigns conducts a 1-hour go-live briefing with TTT's team covering escalations, handoffs, and human override procedures. The monthly retainer begins the following billing cycle.
