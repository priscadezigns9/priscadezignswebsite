Part One — Prisca Dezigns

AI Customer Service Automation for TTT

Prisca Dezigns builds AI systems that handle the routine, repetitive layer of customer communication — so TTT's team can focus on real work. The offering is structured in four tiers, each building on the last. TTT can start at any tier.

Tier 1
Website Chatbot
An AI assistant embedded directly on TTT's website. It handles viewer and advertiser inquiries, qualifies leads, and captures contact details — automatically, around the clock.
$1,500 USD · one-time setup
What's included
  • AI chatbot built and deployed on TTT's website
  • Custom conversation flows for viewer inquiries
  • Advertiser inquiry capture and qualification
  • Lead forwarding to the right TTT team member
  • 24/7 availability — no staff required after hours
  • Brand-matched tone and voice
  • 30-day post-launch support
How it works for TTT

A viewer lands on the TTT website and has a question about a programme. Instead of finding a general contact email and waiting days for a reply, they interact with the AI assistant — which answers immediately.

An advertiser visits and wants to know about ad packages. The chatbot asks the right questions, captures their details, and forwards a qualified lead to TTT's sales team — without a single staff member involved.

The result: TTT is available to viewers and advertisers 24 hours a day without any additional staffing cost.

Tier 2 — Recommended
Website Chatbot + WhatsApp Automation
Everything in Tier 1, plus AI handling of TTT's WhatsApp Business number. Viewers and advertisers who message on WhatsApp get the same instant, intelligent response as on the website.
$2,800 USD · one-time + ~$30/mo API
What's included
  • Everything in Tier 1
  • WhatsApp Business API integration via 360dialog
  • AI handling incoming WhatsApp messages
  • Voice note transcription and response
  • Advertiser lead capture via WhatsApp

Why WhatsApp matters for TTT

In Trinidad and Tobago, WhatsApp is the primary communication channel. Viewers do not send emails — they send WhatsApp messages. Advertisers follow up via WhatsApp. If TTT's WhatsApp is not automated, inquiries are being missed every single day.

This tier ensures that every message — whether it arrives at 9am or 11pm — gets an intelligent, branded reply immediately. Urgent matters are escalated to a staff member with a notification.

The result: TTT never misses a lead or a viewer inquiry regardless of time of day or staffing levels.

Website + WhatsApp + Email Automation
The full front-line communication layer — website, WhatsApp, and email inbox — all handled by AI. TTT's team only touches conversations that require a human decision.

  • Everything in Tier 2

TTT receives dozens of emails daily — press inquiries, advertiser questions, partnership requests, complaints, and general queries. Currently, a staff member manually reads and responds to each one.

With Tier 3, the AI reads every incoming email, decides the category, responds to routine inquiries automatically, and only flags the ones that need a human. Staff wake up to an inbox that is already managed.

The result: TTT's email channel operates at full capacity without full-time email management staff.

A caller rings TTT's main number. The AI voice agent answers professionally, identifies the purpose of the call, and handles it — whether that is giving programme schedule information, taking a complaint, or routing an advertiser to the sales team.

Why Electric — The Business Case

What this means for TTT

Transitioning even a portion of TTT's fleet to electric delivers immediate and compounding financial savings, while sending a visible public signal that TTT is leading — not following — on sustainability.

💰
Fuel Cost Eliminated
A petrol vehicle in daily operational use costs hundreds of TT dollars per month in fuel. An equivalent EV costs approximately TT$20 for a full charge — the same range. Across a fleet of even five vehicles, the annual savings are substantial and immediate.
🔧
Near-Zero Maintenance
EVs have no oil to change, no transmission to service, no exhaust system to replace, and no alternator to fail. The mechanical complexity that drives fleet maintenance costs simply does not exist. TTT's fleet budget shifts from ongoing repair to planned depreciation.
🌧️
Built for Trinidad
Every Evolve Mobility vehicle carries an IP68 battery protection rating — meaning the battery is sealed against water ingress. Trinidad's rainy season and flood-prone roads are not a risk. These vehicles are already deployed across the Caribbean region and proven in the local climate.
🏆
Safety You Can Broadcast
BYD, GAC, Leapmotor, Denza, and Dongfeng all carry 5-star global safety ratings. These are not experimental vehicles — they are among the most tested and validated cars in the world, with billions of kilometres of real-world data behind them.
📡
A Story Worth Telling
As a national broadcaster, TTT choosing to electrify its fleet is a public statement of leadership. It is a story TTT can broadcast, post, and reference — reinforcing the organisation's relevance and authority in the national conversation.
🔋
Overnight Charging at Compound
Fleet vehicles charge overnight at TTT's compound and are fully ready every morning. No detours to a filling station. No fuel tracking. No petrol receipts. The operational simplicity of an EV fleet is a real administrative advantage.
