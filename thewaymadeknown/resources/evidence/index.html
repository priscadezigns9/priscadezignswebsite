The Cohesion of Truth

Inspired by the legacy of Ravi Zacharias, we believe that Truth must cohere. It must be logically consistent, empirically adequate, and experientially relevant.

Historical

Examining the manuscript evidence, archaeological findings, and the historical reality of the empty tomb. We believe the land of Israel itself speaks to the reliability of the Word.

Scientific

Exploring the precision of the universe, the complexity of life, and the scientific evidence that points to a Creator. We do not fear science; we see it as a proclamation of glory.

Experiential

The living evidence of transformed lives. From our founder's testimony to the experiences of thousands, the peace and joy of Christ are experientially undeniable.

The Three-Fold Test

"A worldview must provide answers to four necessary questions: origin, meaning, morality, and destiny. These answers must then pass three tests: logical consistency, empirical adequacy, and experiential relevance. If they do not cohere, the foundation cannot hold."
— The Ravi Zacharias Legacy
