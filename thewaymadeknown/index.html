The Unknown Proclaimed

In the heart of Athens, amidst the philosophers and the seekers, the Apostle Paul stood at the Areopagus and observed an altar with the inscription: TO THE UNKNOWN GOD.

He didn't come to bring a new philosophy, but to reveal a Person. This is the heartbeat of The Way Made Known—the very foundation upon which Prisca Dezigns is built.

We believe that in every pursuit of excellence, there is a deep-seated worship for something greater. Our mission is to bridge that gap—sharing the Grace, Love, and Truth of Jesus Christ through active service to the poor and the unreached.
