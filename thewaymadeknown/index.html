Our Heart

The Way Made Known is not just an organization; it is a commitment to sharing the transformative Grace, Love, and Truth of Jesus Christ through action. We believe that spiritual growth and physical empowerment go hand in hand.

Founded by Prisca Dezigns, our mission is to illuminate the path for those in darkness—whether through the warmth of a home, the power of education, or the strength of a supportive community.

Our Intentions

We focus on reaching the most vulnerable, providing both immediate relief and long-term tools for flourishing.

Church Evangelism

Supporting local churches with tools and resources to spread the Gospel effectively within their communities.

Education for All

Providing scholarships, digital literacy, and fundamental education to children in poverty-stricken areas.

Crisis Homes

Funding and building safe havens for those displaced by hardship, in partnership with Prime Land.

Youth Mentorship

Empowering the next generation through guidance, skill-building, and spiritual foundation.
