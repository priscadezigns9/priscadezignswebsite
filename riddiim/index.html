What It Does

Everything music.
One app.

Riddiim collapses the entire music experience into a single, seamless product.

01 🎵

Instant Song Detection

Hear something you like? Hold up your phone. Riddiim identifies any song in under 3 seconds — title, artist, album, lyrics, and where to stream it.

02 🎧

Seamless Streaming

Access 100M+ tracks without switching apps. Curated playlists, mood-based radio, and a library that actually learns your taste over time.

03 🤖

AI Music Creation

Generate beats, melodies, and full tracks with AI. Describe a vibe, upload a reference, or start from scratch — Riddiim makes creation frictionless.

04 🌍

Artist Community

Follow artists, share your creations, discover emerging talent, and collaborate globally. Music is a conversation — Riddiim makes it social.
