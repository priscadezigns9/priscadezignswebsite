# Riddiim - Your Artists. Your Music. Direct.

Riddiim is a direct-to-fan music platform designed to strip away the middlemen and connect artists directly with their audience. It captures the energy of a Caribbean concert—loud, intimate, and real.

## Brand Identity
- **Design Language**: Concert energy meets vinyl culture. Dark, warm, alive.
- **Color Palette**:
  - Background: `#0A0007` (Near black, purple undertone)
  - Accent: `#FF2D6B` (Hot pink/magenta)
  - Secondary: `#FFB800` (Amber gold)
  - Surface: `#150010`
  - Border: `#2A0020`

## Core Features
- **Direct Subscriptions**: Fans pay artists directly ($4.99–$14.99/mo).
- **The Wave**: A signature discovery UI with pulsing waveforms and live indicators.
- **Exclusive Content**: Unreleased tracks, raw voice notes, and behind-the-scenes access.
- **Listening Parties**: Real-time live sessions for album drops and community hangouts.
- **Voice DMs**: High-fidelity voice messaging between artists and fans.

## Architecture
- **Frontend**: Vanilla HTML5, CSS3 (CSS Variables, Flexbox/Grid), and JavaScript.
- **Animations**: Pure CSS keyframe animations for waveforms and live indicators.
- **Monetization**: 85/15 revenue split (Artist takes 85%).

## File Structure
- `landing/index.html`: Marketing and onboarding.
- `fan/dashboard.html`: Subscription feed and discovery.
- `artist/dashboard.html`: Content studio and analytics.
- `shared/style.css`: Core design system.
