Overview

The AI Sales Representative acts as an intelligent sales assistant, engaging with potential customers through natural voice conversations. It can answer questions about your products or services, collect prospect information, qualify leads based on your business criteria, and schedule appointments with your sales team.

Designed to support—not replace—your sales professionals, the AI Sales Representative ensures every lead receives immediate attention while allowing your team to focus on closing deals.
