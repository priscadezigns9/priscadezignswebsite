Overview

The AI Accounts Receivable Agent serves as an intelligent finance assistant, helping businesses manage accounts receivable communications through natural voice conversations. It can remind customers of upcoming or overdue invoices, provide invoice and payment information, answer common billing enquiries, and record customer responses for follow-up by your finance team.

Designed to support—not replace—your finance professionals, the AI Accounts Receivable Agent helps improve communication consistency, reduce administrative workload, and maintain positive customer relationships while encouraging timely payments.
