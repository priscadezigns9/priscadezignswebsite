Overview

The AI Receptionist is the first voice your callers hear — and it makes an instant impression. It greets every caller professionally, understands why they're calling, and routes them to the right person or department without delay. No voicemail black holes. No unanswered phones. No missed business.

Unlike a Customer Service Rep, the Receptionist doesn't resolve issues — it qualifies and directs. Think of it as the smartest front desk you've ever hired, working 24 hours a day without lunch breaks.

What It Does

Greets every caller with a custom, branded welcome
Identifies the caller's name and reason for calling
Routes callers to the correct department or person
Answers basic FAQs (hours, location, services)
Takes detailed messages when staff are unavailable
Books callbacks or transfers to a human on request
Notifies you via WhatsApp when a message is left
Operates 24/7 including weekends and holidays

Common Use Cases

Medical / Dental Clinics: Route patients to the correct doctor, nurse, or billing desk without tying up staff.
Law Firms: Greet clients professionally, capture reason for call, route to the correct attorney or clerk.
Real Estate: Route buyer vs. seller enquiries, capture contact info, notify the agent instantly.
Retail & E-Commerce: Answer store hours, location, and return policy questions before routing to staff.
Car Dealerships: Separate sales vs. service calls and route accordingly — no customer left hanging.
Any SME: One phone number, multiple departments — all handled without a human operator.
