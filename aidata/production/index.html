A Day With Chan

What Chan handles today

A snapshot of Chan's daily workflow — from shift start to end-of-day report.

6:00 AM

Shift Start Log

Chan opens the production log for the new shift, records operator names, machine assignments, and production targets for the day.

8:30 AM

Inventory Check

Chan scans raw material levels against the day's production schedule. Flags any shortfalls and alerts the procurement team via WhatsApp.

12:00 PM

Mid-Shift Output Snapshot

Chan calculates units produced vs. target at the halfway mark. Sends a mid-shift efficiency score to the floor supervisor.

2:00 PM

Maintenance Reminder

Chan checks the equipment schedule and sends a service reminder for Machine #3's weekly lubrication — logged and dispatched before the end of the shift.

4:30 PM

End-of-Day Report

Chan compiles total output, downtime incidents, material usage, and any non-conformances into a structured PDF and delivers it to management's inbox.

6:00 PM

Weekly Trend Analysis

On Fridays, Chan generates a 7-day production trend report — efficiency curves, bottleneck patterns, and a recommended action item for next week.
