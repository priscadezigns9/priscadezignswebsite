A Day with Hera

What Hera does while you work

A typical day for your AI HR employee — running quietly in the background so nothing falls through the cracks.

7:00 AM
Leave Request Received
An employee messages via WhatsApp requesting 3 days annual leave. Hera checks their balance, approves, and logs it instantly.
8:30 AM
New Hire Onboarding Triggered
HR confirms a new hire. Hera sends a welcome email, onboarding checklist, and policy documents — all automated.
10:00 AM
CV Screening Batch
12 applications arrive for an open position. Hera screens all 12 against your criteria and delivers a ranked shortlist of 3.
12:00 PM
Payroll Cycle Processed
Timesheets submitted. Hera calculates NIS, PAYE, and Health Surcharge for every employee and flags one discrepancy for review.
2:30 PM
Contract Renewal Alert
Two employee contracts expire in 30 days. Hera sends the manager an alert and prepares the renewal documents for review.
5:00 PM
HR Summary Delivered
End-of-day report: 1 leave approved, 1 hire onboarded, 3 candidates shortlisted, payroll reviewed. Manager signs off in 5 minutes.
