How It Works

📢
1. You Post
Client posts the job on JobsTT, Caribbean Jobs, or social media with the AI application link
📋
2. They Apply
Candidates fill the AI intake form and upload their CV or résumé directly
🤖
3. AI Scores
AI reads every CV, scores against your job criteria, and filters out unqualified applicants
✅
4. You Receive
HR staff receives only approved candidates via email with scores, summaries, and contact details

What The AI Evaluates

Years of relevant experience
Required qualifications & certifications
Skills match against job description
Dealbreaker detection (auto-disqualify)
Availability & start date alignment
Overall suitability score (0–100)
