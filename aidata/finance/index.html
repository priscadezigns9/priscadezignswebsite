A Day with Vera

What Vera does while you run your business

A typical day for your AI Finance employee — processing, matching, and reporting so your books are always clean.

7:00 AM
Bank Feed Reconciled
Overnight transactions are pulled and matched against your ledger. By the time you open your laptop, everything from yesterday is already reconciled.
8:15 AM
Discrepancy Alert Sent
One unmatched debit detected — a duplicate vendor payment. Vera flags it with the full context and sends you a review request before the issue compounds.
10:30 AM
Expense Report Processed
Team submits weekly expense claims. Vera categorises each line, validates against your approval rules, and logs the approved total to the correct accounts.
12:00 PM
Weekly Income Summary
Vera generates your mid-week revenue summary — broken down by product line or channel — and delivers it clean to your inbox. No manual collating.
3:00 PM
VAT Deadline Reminder
VAT return is due in 5 days. Vera compiles the full quarter's taxable transactions and delivers a structured summary to your accountant for final sign-off.
5:00 PM
Daily Finance Close Report
End-of-day: all transactions reconciled, 1 discrepancy flagged and resolved, 0 outstanding entries. Your books close clean — every single day.
Month End
Statements Ready
P&L, Cash Flow, and Balance Sheet — generated, formatted, and delivered before your first meeting of the new month. No last-minute scramble.
