A Day with Dean

What he handles while you work

From first lead of the day to end-of-day campaign report, Dean keeps your marketing running without a single manual intervention.

7:45 AM

New Staff Onboarded

A new lead comes in via the website form. Dean captures it, cleans the data, tags the source, and adds it to the audience list automatically.

8:30 AM

Password Reset Resolved

Lead scoring runs. Dean evaluates 12 new leads, assigns scores, and fires a WhatsApp alert to the sales team for the top 3 — highest priority first.

10:00 AM

Audience Data Audit

Dean scans the contact list — flags 8 duplicate entries, 5 missing email fields, and 2 outdated audience tags. Clean data, ready to use.

11:30 AM

Campaign Performance Report

Dean pulls the week's Meta Ads data — cost per lead is up 18%. He flags it, compares against the previous week, and surfaces which ad set is underperforming.

2:15 PM

System Alert Triggered

A campaign goes live. Dean begins tracking impressions, clicks, and conversions in real time — ready to report at end of day with a full breakdown.

5:00 PM

End-of-Day Marketing Report

Dean sends a daily digest — new leads captured, scores assigned, campaign spend vs. conversion, and priority follow-ups for tomorrow.
