A Day with Boyo

What he handles while you work

From morning briefing to end-of-shift sign-off, Boyo keeps your safety programme running with zero manual input.

6:30 AM

Morning Safety Briefing

Boyo sends the day's toolbox talk and PPE reminder to all active workers via WhatsApp.

8:00 AM

Hazard Report Received

A worker flags a wet floor. Boyo logs it, assigns severity, and notifies the site supervisor immediately.

10:15 AM

Near-Miss Logged

Worker reports a near-miss via WhatsApp. Boyo captures the full incident form and stores it with timestamp.

12:00 PM

Compliance Scan

Boyo runs an automated check against your OSH obligations and flags any documentation gaps.

3:30 PM

Incident Report Closed

The morning hazard is resolved. Boyo updates the log with corrective action taken and notifies management.

5:00 PM

End-of-Day Summary

Boyo sends a daily safety digest — incidents logged, hazards cleared, open items, and tomorrow's toolbox topic.
