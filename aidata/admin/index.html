A Day in the Life

Your AI Admin employee — Monday to Sunday

While you lead, your AI handles the logistics. Here's what that looks like in real time.

7:02 AM
Inbox Management

Overnight emails — triaged before you wake up

34 emails came in overnight. AI categorised them by priority, drafted responses for routine enquiries, and flagged two urgent items needing your attention. Your inbox is structured before your first coffee.

9:15 AM
Calendar Coordination

Meeting request handled — confirmed in 2 minutes

A supplier requests a meeting. AI checks availability, proposes three slots, receives confirmation, creates the calendar event, and sends a brief with agenda — without you touching a single email.

11:40 AM
Document Processing

Contract received — filed and acknowledged automatically

A signed contract arrives via email. AI extracts the key details, files it in the correct folder, logs the vendor and expiry date, and sends a receipt confirmation — full audit trail created.

2:30 PM
Follow-up Automation

Pending action from Monday — follow-up sent

A proposal sent Monday had no response. AI sends a professional follow-up, logs the outreach, and sets a 48-hour reminder if still no reply. Nothing falls through the cracks.

5:00 PM
Daily Summary

End-of-day report — delivered to your inbox

AI compiles everything actioned during the day: emails handled, meetings confirmed, documents filed, follow-ups sent. You get a clean summary so you know exactly where things stand.
