A Day with Ada

What Ada does while you work

A typical day for your AI Administration employee — running quietly in the background so nothing falls through the cracks.

7:00 AM
Morning Brief Delivered
Ada compiles all pending tasks, scheduled meetings, and open threads into a clean morning brief — in your inbox before you start.
8:30 AM
Inbox Triaged
80 emails sorted. Urgent items flagged, routine replies drafted, noise filtered. You open your inbox to a prioritised action list — not chaos.
10:00 AM
Meeting Confirmed
A client requests a call. Ada checks your calendar, books the slot, sends the invite, and preps a one-page agenda — without you lifting a finger.
12:00 PM
Documents Processed
Three contracts arrive. Ada names, classifies, routes each one for signature, and logs them in your records system. Nothing sits in an email thread.
2:30 PM
Follow-Ups Sent
Two proposals sent Monday haven't had a response. Ada sends a professional follow-up to both — on schedule, on tone, without prompting.
5:00 PM
Daily Summary Delivered
End-of-day report: 42 emails handled, 2 meetings booked, 3 documents filed, 2 follow-ups sent. Your day's admin, accounted for in full.
