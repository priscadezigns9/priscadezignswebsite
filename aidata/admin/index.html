A Day with Alana

What Alana does while you work

A typical day for your AI Administration Coordinator — connecting departments, chasing deadlines, and keeping the business moving so you don't have to.

7:00 AM
Unified Morning Brief
Alana pulls reports from all connected departments — Chan's overnight shift log, Zara's open NCs, Luna's pending HSE actions — and delivers one clean brief to the owner before the day starts.
8:30 AM
Inbox Triaged
WhatsApp and email sorted by priority. A supplier in Chaguanas is asking about a PO. A client wants to reschedule. Both handled — routed, replied, and logged — before you sit down.
10:00 AM
Regulatory Alert Fired
Alana flags that your business licence renewal is due in 9 days. She logs the deadline, drafts the renewal reminder message, and escalates to you for action — nothing slips through.
12:00 PM
Documents Processed
Three contracts arrive. Alana names, classifies, routes each one for approval, and logs them in your records system. Zero email threads used as filing cabinets.
2:30 PM
Vendor Follow-Up Sent
A delivery confirmation from your supplier in Port of Spain is overdue. Alana sends a professional follow-up via WhatsApp — on schedule, on tone, without prompting.
5:00 PM
End-of-Day Report
Department outputs compiled: 42 messages handled, 1 regulatory alert actioned, 3 documents filed, 2 follow-ups sent, 1 meeting booked. Your entire operation — accounted for.

The Process

How we set Alana up for you

From audit to activation — your AI Administration Coordinator is fully configured and live within weeks.

01

Business Process Audit

We map your current admin workflows — inbox volume, scheduling patterns, document types, follow-up frequency — and identify exactly where Alana should take over.

02

Department Selection

You choose which departments Alana connects to — one at Starter, up to four at Growth, full house at Enterprise. Each connection is configured to route the right outputs to the right people.

03

AI Configuration & Activation

We train Alana on your business tone, document templates, escalation rules, and Caribbean regulatory calendar. Everything is live-tested before handoff — you don't touch the backend.

04

Ongoing Management

Monthly review of Alana's performance across all connected departments. We handle adjustments, retraining, and workflow updates — included in your monthly plan.
