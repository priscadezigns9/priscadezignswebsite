A Day With Drew

From first message to closed deal — on autopilot.

Here's how Drew moves through a real business day — with zero input from you.

7:00 AM
Morning Pipeline Review
Drew scans overnight enquiries, tags leads by temperature (hot/warm/cold), and queues follow-up actions for the day.
8:30 AM
Inbound Lead Response
A new enquiry comes in via WhatsApp. Drew responds in under 60 seconds — qualifying budget, urgency, and product interest before any human is involved.
10:15 AM
Product Walkthrough
A prospect asks for a full breakdown of your service tiers. Drew delivers a detailed, accurate, personalized response — no guesswork, no delays.
12:00 PM
Objection — Price Too High
"That's more than I expected." Drew doesn't panic — he acknowledges the concern, reframes the value, and offers the next best option to keep the deal alive.
2:30 PM
Follow-Up Sequence Fires
A lead from 3 days ago goes cold. Drew sends a tailored follow-up — referencing their original enquiry — to re-engage the conversation automatically.
4:45 PM
Deal Closed — Handoff to Sierra
Prospect confirms they're ready to proceed. Drew captures their details, marks the lead as converted, and passes the file to Sierra for onboarding.
6:00 PM
Daily Sales Summary
Drew delivers a clean end-of-day report — leads contacted, deals progressed, objections logged, and one conversion confirmed for the day.
