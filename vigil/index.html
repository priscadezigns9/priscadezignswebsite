The Problem

Caribbean security companies
are running on pen and paper.

Guards lie on patrol logs. Schedules are done on WhatsApp. Clients have zero visibility. And when something goes wrong — nobody knows until it's too late.

📵
Last-minute callouts
Guards call out with no notice. Managers scramble on WhatsApp to find a replacement. Sites go uncovered.
🗺️
Guards who don't patrol
They sign the log. They never walked the route. Nobody knows until there's a breach and the client pulls the contract.
📄
Useless incident reports
"Nothing to report." Or a paragraph of barely legible notes that would never hold up in court or with a client.
🙈
Clients in the dark
Your client has no idea what their guards are doing. They only hear from you when something goes wrong. Then they cancel.
📹
CCTV nobody watches
Cameras recording 24/7 but no one's watching. By the time someone reviews footage, the incident is hours old.
📉
No performance data
You don't know which guards are good until a client complains. No data. No accountability. No way to improve.
