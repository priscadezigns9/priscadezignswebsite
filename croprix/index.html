The Problem

Caribbean farmers lose yield every season
to the same problems.

These aren't bad luck — they're predictable problems that data and AI can solve. Every season, they cost Caribbean farmers millions in lost crops and revenue.

📉
Poor Yield
No data on best planting times, wrong fertilizer schedules, and guesswork on quantities mean yields never reach their potential.
⛈️
Unpredictable Weather
No system to adjust plans when weather changes. Farmers apply fertilizer before rain or miss optimal planting windows.
🐛
Pests & Disease
Detected too late, entire crops are lost. By the time disease is visible to the eye, it's often already spread across the field.
💸
No Sales Tracking
No idea what's profitable and what's not. Farmers grow the same crops year after year without knowing their actual margins.
