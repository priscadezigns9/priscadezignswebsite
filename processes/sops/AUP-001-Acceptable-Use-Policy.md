## 1. PURPOSE

This Acceptable Use Policy ("Policy") defines the rules, responsibilities, and prohibited activities governing all clients ("Client") who engage Prisca Dezigns for AI automation services. By signing a Service Agreement with Prisca Dezigns, the Client agrees to be bound by this Policy.

This Policy exists to:
- Protect the integrity of Prisca Dezigns' infrastructure and AI systems
- Protect the end users (the Client's customers) who interact with AI agents deployed by Prisca Dezigns
- Ensure all usage complies with applicable laws in Trinidad and Tobago and internationally
- Maintain the reputation and ethical standards of Prisca Dezigns

---

## 2. SCOPE

This Policy applies to:
- All AI agents deployed by Prisca Dezigns on the Client's behalf (WhatsApp, Email, Website Chat)
- All automation pipelines configured for the Client
- All knowledge bases, data, and content provided by the Client for use in AI systems
- All staff, contractors, and third parties acting on behalf of the Client

---

## 3. PERMITTED USE

The Client is permitted to use Prisca Dezigns AI services exclusively for:

### 3.1 Legitimate Business Communication
- Responding to genuine customer inquiries about products and services
- Qualifying and routing sales leads
- Providing post-sale customer support
- Booking appointments and managing scheduling
- Sending service notifications and updates to opted-in subscribers

### 3.2 Marketing Communication
- Sending promotional messages to individuals who have **explicitly opted in** to receive them
- Following up on sales leads who have initiated contact
- Re-engaging past customers within applicable consent windows (GDPR: 12 months; TT DPA: reasonable period)

### 3.3 Internal Process Automation
- Automating internal notifications (not involving customer personal data)
- Data entry and document processing where authorised by data subjects

---

## 4. PROHIBITED USE

The following uses are **strictly prohibited** and will result in immediate suspension of services without refund:

### 4.1 Unlawful Communications
- Sending messages to individuals who have not consented to receive them (spam)
- Sending messages that violate the TT Electronic Transactions Act, CAN-SPAM Act, CASL, or GDPR
- Using AI agents for phishing, fraud, or impersonation of any person or organisation
- Sending unsolicited commercial messages (cold outreach) without a lawful basis

### 4.2 Harmful or Deceptive Content
- Instructing AI agents to make false or misleading statements about products, services, pricing, or availability
- Using AI agents to manipulate, coerce, or psychologically pressure individuals
- Deploying AI agents in a manner designed to deceive users into believing they are interacting with a human when directly and sincerely asked
- Generating or distributing disinformation, propaganda, or deliberately misleading content

### 4.3 Illegal Activities
- Using AI services to facilitate any activity that is illegal under the laws of Trinidad and Tobago or any other applicable jurisdiction
- Processing or transmitting data related to illegal goods or services
- Circumventing any security control, access restriction, or data protection measure
- Money laundering, fraud, or financial crime of any kind

### 4.4 Sensitive Data Violations
- Instructing Prisca Dezigns to process the following data types without a signed data amendment and appropriate safeguards:
  - Health or medical information
  - Financial account or payment card data
  - Government-issued identification numbers
  - Biometric data
  - Data relating to children under the age of 18
- Storing sensitive personal data in any system not specifically approved for that purpose

### 4.5 Harassment and Abuse
- Using AI agents to harass, threaten, intimidate, or discriminate against any individual
- Deploying AI at volumes or frequencies designed to overwhelm or harass recipients
- Ignoring or overriding opt-out requests from any individual
- Targeting vulnerable populations (elderly, minors, individuals with disabilities) in an exploitative manner

### 4.6 Platform Policy Violations
- Violating Meta's WhatsApp Business Policy or any applicable platform terms of service
- Violating Google's or any email provider's policies regarding commercial communications
- Using AI services to bypass or circumvent platform rate limits, spam filters, or security systems

### 4.7 Intellectual Property
- Providing Prisca Dezigns with content, data, or materials that infringe the intellectual property rights of any third party
- Using AI-generated outputs to infringe copyright, trademark, or other intellectual property rights

### 4.8 System Integrity
- Attempting to access, reverse engineer, or exploit any Prisca Dezigns system, code, or infrastructure
- Introducing malicious code, scripts, or data into any system
- Sharing or reselling Prisca Dezigns AI services, pipelines, or infrastructure to third parties without prior written consent

---

## 5. CONTENT STANDARDS

All content provided by the Client for use in AI knowledge bases, messaging templates, or automation workflows must:

- Be accurate and up to date at the time of submission
- Not contain defamatory, discriminatory, or hateful language
- Comply with all applicable advertising and consumer protection standards
- Not make unsubstantiated health, financial, or performance claims
- Be approved in writing by the Client's authorised representative before deployment

Prisca Dezigns reserves the right to refuse to deploy or to remove any content that, in its reasonable judgement, violates this Policy or applicable law.

---

## 6. OPT-OUT AND CONSENT OBLIGATIONS

The Client bears full responsibility for:

### 6.1 Obtaining Consent
- Collecting valid, informed, and documented consent from all individuals before initiating AI-driven communications
- Maintaining records of consent that can be produced on request
- Ensuring consent language clearly describes the use of AI automation

### 6.2 Honouring Opt-Outs
- Immediately honouring opt-out requests — no further messages may be sent after an opt-out is received
- Processing opt-outs within a maximum of **24 hours** of receipt
- Maintaining a suppression list of opted-out contacts and sharing it with Prisca Dezigns for exclusion

### 6.3 Consent Records
- Retaining consent records for the duration of the service relationship plus **3 years**
- Making consent records available to Prisca Dezigns within **48 hours** upon request

---

## 7. KNOWLEDGE BASE AND DATA QUALITY

The Client is responsible for:
- Providing accurate, complete, and current information for the AI knowledge base
- Reviewing and updating the knowledge base at minimum every **90 days**
- Promptly notifying Prisca Dezigns of any changes to products, pricing, policies, or services that may affect AI responses
- Not including confidential third-party information in the knowledge base without authorisation

Prisca Dezigns is not liable for AI responses based on inaccurate, outdated, or incomplete information provided by the Client.

---

## 8. INCIDENT REPORTING

The Client must notify Prisca Dezigns immediately (within 24 hours) if the Client becomes aware of:
- Any misuse of the AI service by a third party
- Any complaint from a Data Subject regarding AI interactions
- Any regulatory inquiry or investigation related to the AI service
- Any suspected security incident affecting data processed through the service

---

## 9. MONITORING AND ENFORCEMENT

### 9.1 Monitoring
Prisca Dezigns reserves the right to monitor AI interactions and system usage to:
- Ensure compliance with this Policy
- Maintain service quality and security
- Detect and respond to incidents

### 9.2 Consequences of Violation
Violations of this Policy may result in:

| Severity | Example | Consequence |
|---|---|---|
| Minor | Outdated knowledge base, single formatting violation | Written warning, required remediation within 7 days |
| Moderate | Repeated minor violations, unintentional policy breach | Temporary suspension of service pending remediation |
| Severe | Spam campaign, data breach, illegal activity | Immediate termination without refund, legal referral |

### 9.3 Suspension and Termination
Prisca Dezigns may suspend or terminate services immediately and without prior notice if:
- The Client's use poses an immediate legal risk to Prisca Dezigns or any third party
- The Client breaches any Prohibited Use provision (Section 4)
- The Client fails to cooperate with an incident investigation
- A regulatory authority directs Prisca Dezigns to cease processing
