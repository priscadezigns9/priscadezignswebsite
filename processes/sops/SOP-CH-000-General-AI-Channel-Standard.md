## 1. PURPOSE

This SOP establishes the universal governance framework for all AI Channel Automation services delivered by Prisca Dezigns. It applies across every communication channel — WhatsApp, Email, and Website Chat — and governs conduct, legal compliance, data handling, pricing authority, delivery standards, and escalation protocols. All channel-specific SOPs (SOP-CH-001, SOP-CH-002, SOP-CH-003) are subordinate to this document.

---

## 2. SCOPE

This SOP applies to:
- All AI Channel Automation engagements across WhatsApp, Email, and Website Chat
- All agents (human or AI, including Drew and Sierra) handling channel automation sales or delivery
- All automation pipelines (Make.com, n8n, custom APIs)
- All AI brains used (Claude API, GPT-4o, Gemini)
- All clients regardless of industry, tier, or geography

## 3. LEGAL AND REGULATORY FRAMEWORK

### 3.1 Trinidad and Tobago Legislation

| Law | Obligation |
|---|---|
| **Data Protection Act, 2011 (TT)** | All personal data collected via AI channels (name, phone, email, enquiry content) requires a lawful basis — typically explicit consent. Data must not be retained beyond its necessary period. Violations carry fines up to TTD 100,000 and/or up to 5 years imprisonment. |
| **Electronic Transactions Act, Chap. 22:06** | AI-generated messages, automated replies, and digital agreements are legally binding electronic records under TT law. Every automated interaction must be traceable and auditable. |
| **Computer Misuse Act, Chap. 11:19** | Unauthorized interception, scraping, or manipulation of communication channels is a criminal offence. All channel integrations must be authorized by the channel platform and the client. |
| **Consumer Protection and Safety Act (TT)** | AI systems must not make false, misleading, or deceptive representations to consumers. Every AI agent must disclose its AI nature upon direct inquiry. |
| **Telecommunications Act, Chap. 47:31** | Commercial electronic messaging via any channel must be authorized. Unsolicited bulk messaging is prohibited. |
| **Miscellaneous Provisions (Companies) Act** | Prisca Dezigns must represent itself truthfully in all client agreements, invoices, and automated communications sent on a client's behalf. |

### 3.2 International Standards

| Standard | Obligation |
|---|---|
| **GDPR (EU) 2016/679** | Applies when serving EU-based clients or processing data of EU residents. Requires Data Processing Agreements (DPA-001). Consent must be explicit, specific, and withdrawable. Right to erasure applies. |
| **CASL (Canada)** | Express or implied consent required before any commercial electronic message is sent to Canadian recipients. Unsubscribe mechanism mandatory in every email. |
| **CAN-SPAM Act (USA)** | Applies to all email automation targeting US recipients. Sender identity must be clear, opt-out must be honoured within 10 business days, and subject lines must not deceive. |
| **WhatsApp Business Policy** | All WhatsApp automation must use officially approved channels (360dialog, WATI, Z-API). Spam, bulk unsolicited messaging, and impersonation are grounds for permanent account ban. |
| **ISO/IEC 27001:2022** | Information security controls must be applied to all client channel data, API credentials, and conversation logs. |
| **ISO/IEC 42001:2023** | AI systems must be governed with transparency, accountability, and documented risk assessment. |
| **WCAG 2.1 AA** | Any AI-facing web interface (chat widget, email template) must meet minimum accessibility standards. |
| **NIST AI RMF 1.0** | AI risk must be governed, mapped, measured, and managed across all channel deployments. |

## 4. CORE PRINCIPLES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

All AI Channel Automation services must comply with the following at all times:

1. **Transparency:** Every AI agent must clearly identify itself as AI upon direct inquiry. No impersonation of a human operator is permitted.
2. **Consent First:** No automated message may be sent to any end-user without a valid, documented consent basis.
3. **Data Minimisation:** Only data strictly necessary for the automation purpose may be collected and stored.
4. **Audit Trail:** Every conversation, lead capture, and escalation must be logged and retrievable for a minimum of 12 months.
5. **Escalation Ready:** Every AI channel must have a defined escalation path to a human operator for sensitive, legal, or complaint scenarios.
6. **No Deception:** AI agents must never make promises about pricing, timelines, or outcomes that have not been approved by the Lead Strategist.
7. **Client Ownership:** All data collected via the client's AI channel belongs to the client. Prisca Dezigns acts as Data Processor only.

## 5. SERVICE DEFINITION

### 5.1 What AI Channel Automation Is
AI Channel Automation is the deployment of intelligent, autonomous AI agents across a client's communication channels — WhatsApp, Email, and/or Website Chat. These agents handle:

- Instant FAQ responses (24/7)
- Lead capture and qualification
- Appointment booking and confirmations
- Customer support and complaint triage
- Sales qualification and objection handling (with Sales Logic add-on)

## 7. THE AUDIT-FIRST PROTOCOL

Before any package is recommended, every agent must complete a 5-question Business Process Audit:

1. What channels are you currently using to communicate with customers? (WhatsApp, email, website?)
2. What is your biggest communication pain point right now?
3. How many customer enquiries do you receive per day on average?
4. Do you have any existing tools — CRM, booking system, or helpdesk — we need to integrate with?
5. What does success look like for you 90 days after deployment?

> Answers determine which tier, which channel(s), and which add-ons are appropriate.

## 8. THE STARTER RULE

All new AI Channel clients must begin with the Starter Package (from US$750). No exceptions.

**Rationale:**
- The Starter provides a live AI solution immediately
- It provides Prisca Dezigns the operational insight to identify where AI can scale further
- It protects the client from over-investing before the AI is calibrated to their workflows
- It is mandated under SOP-AI-000 Section 5.1

> Upsell to Growth or Enterprise is permitted only after the Starter has been live for a minimum of 30 days, or if the Business Process Audit conclusively demonstrates that the client's operational complexity requires a higher tier from day one.

## 9. DELIVERY STANDARDS

### 9.1 Delivery Timeline
| Tier | Target Go-Live |
|---|---|
| Starter | 5–7 business days from signed agreement and payment |
| Growth | 10–14 business days |
| Enterprise | 21–30 business days (custom scoping required) |

### 9.2 Pre-Delivery Requirements (from client)
- Channel access credentials (WhatsApp Business API approval, email SMTP, or website embed access)
- Business information: FAQs, services list, pricing (if to be disclosed by AI), escalation contact
- Approved AI persona name and tone
- Signed Service Agreement and DPA-001

### 9.3 Quality Assurance
- All AI flows must be tested across 20+ real-world conversation scenarios before go-live
- Escalation paths must be verified — every dead-end must route to a human
- Response accuracy must meet a minimum 95% threshold on FAQ testing
- Lead capture must be validated end-to-end into the designated CRM or database

## 10. DATA GOVERNANCE

| Requirement | Standard |
|---|---|
| **Consent** | Collected at first interaction — opt-in language displayed before any data is captured |
| **Storage** | Client data stored in client's designated system (Supabase, CRM, Google Sheets) — not retained by Prisca Dezigns beyond the engagement |
| **Retention** | Conversation logs retained for 12 months minimum for audit purposes, then deleted unless otherwise agreed |
| **Access** | Only the client and Prisca Dezigns (as Data Processor) may access conversation data |
| **Breach Protocol** | Any suspected data breach must be reported to the Lead Strategist within 24 hours and to the TT Data Commissioner within 72 hours per DPA-001 |

## 11. ESCALATION PROTOCOL

### 11.1 When AI Must Escalate to Human
- Legal threats, complaints, or claims of harm
- Sensitive personal data disclosed (medical, financial, legal)
- Repeated failed attempts to resolve an enquiry (3 strikes)
- Request to speak to a human operator
- Any interaction outside the AI's defined knowledge scope

### 11.2 Escalation Script
> "I want to make sure this is handled at the highest level. Let me connect you with our team directly. Can I confirm the best WhatsApp number or email to reach you?"

### 11.3 Lead Capture on Escalation (Strike Protocol)
When escalation is triggered:
1. Capture: Name, business name, WhatsApp number, and nature of enquiry
2. Forward immediately to Lead Strategist: WhatsApp 18683424101
3. Log interaction in Supabase `client_leads` table

## 12. PROHIBITED CONDUCT

The following are strictly prohibited in all AI Channel engagements:

- Sending unsolicited bulk messages via any channel
- Collecting sensitive data (health, financial, legal) without explicit consent and a documented legal basis
- Impersonating a human operator when directly asked
- Quoting final prices without completing the Business Process Audit
- Deploying AI on a channel without the platform's official authorization
- Storing client conversation data outside the agreed system without the client's written consent
- Making guarantees of specific conversion rates, lead volumes, or revenue outcomes
