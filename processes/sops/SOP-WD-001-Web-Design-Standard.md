## 12. CLIENT QUALIFICATION PROTOCOL (DREW'S AUDIT-FIRST FLOW)

Before quoting any package, Drew must ask:

1. "What type of business are you running?" (Industry, product, service)
2. "Do you already have a website, or is this a fresh launch?"
3. "Are you looking to just have a presence online, or do you need to sell directly through the site?"
4. "Do you have your branding ready — logo, colours — or do you need that built from scratch?"
5. "What is your target launch date?"

**Assignment Logic:**
- No branding + quick launch → **Fast-Track or Standard Site (Template)**
- New business + full brand needed → **Starter**
- Multi-page + copy needed → **Growth**
- Established business + automation + support → **Trusted**
- Selling products (simple) → **E-Starter** or **Micro Store**
- Selling products (volume) → **E-Growth** or **E-Trusted**
- Complex / enterprise requirements → **Custom** (escalate immediately)
