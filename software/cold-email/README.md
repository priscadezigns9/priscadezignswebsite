# OUTREACH_AI

**AI-Powered Cold Email Sequencer**

A standalone Web2 SaaS product for automating and personalizing cold email outreach using artificial intelligence.

## Features
- **AI Lead Enrichment:** Automatically researches prospects via LinkedIn and company data.
- **Dynamic Personalization:** Generates unique icebreakers for every email.
- **Smart Sequences:** Multi-step follow-ups that stop when a lead replies.
- **A/B Testing:** Optimize subject lines and content with built-in analytics.
- **CRM Integration:** Syncs with HubSpot, Salesforce, and more.

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3 (Custom Grid), Vanilla JavaScript.
- **Design:** Black & Blue High-Fidelity Professional Aesthetic.
- **Font:** Inter

## Product Structure
- `/landing/index.html`: Marketing landing page with pricing and features.
- `/app/dashboard.html`: Main application interface for campaign management.
- `/app/dashboard.css`: Application styles and layout.
- `/app/dashboard.js`: Interactive components and UI logic.
