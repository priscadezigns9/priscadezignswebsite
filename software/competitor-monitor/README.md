# WATCHDOG_PRO

**AI-Powered Competitor Intelligence Monitor**

A standalone Web2 SaaS product designed to give businesses a competitive edge by monitoring market moves in real-time.

## Features
- **Price Intelligence:** Tracks every pricing change across competitor plans and tiers.
- **Website Monitoring:** Detects new landing pages, product features, and blog content.
- **Ad Spy:** Aggregates active ads from Facebook, Google, and LinkedIn into a single dashboard.
- **AI Strategic Insights:** Weekly reports that analyze data trends and suggest counter-moves.
- **Real-time Change Feed:** A live timeline of all detected market activity.

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript.
- **Design:** Black & Amber High-Fidelity Professional Aesthetic.
- **Font:** Inter

## Product Structure
- `/landing/index.html`: Marketing landing page with pricing and features.
- `/app/dashboard.html`: Main intelligence dashboard.
- `/app/dashboard.css`: Grid-based layout for data visualization.
- `/app/dashboard.js`: Interactive navigation and data simulation.
