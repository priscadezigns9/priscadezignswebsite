# MEETAI

**AI Meeting Recorder & Action Extractor**

A standalone Web2 SaaS product that automates meeting notes, action items, and follow-ups for remote teams.

## Features
- **Live Transcription:** Real-time speech-to-text with high accuracy and speaker labels.
- **AI Summary:** Intelligent condensation of long meetings into key takeaways.
- **Auto Action Items:** Automatically detects and assigns tasks discussed during calls.
- **Follow-up Drafts:** One-click emailRecap generation.
- **CRM & Tool Sync:** Integrates with Slack, Notion, HubSpot, and Salesforce.
- **Searchable Archive:** Search through your entire meeting history by keywords or topics.

## Tech Stack
