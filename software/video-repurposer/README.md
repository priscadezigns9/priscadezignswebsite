# CLIPFORGE - AI Video Repurposing Tool

ClipForge is an advanced Web2 SaaS platform that transforms long-form video content into viral short-form clips optimized for modern social media platforms.

## Key Capabilities
- **Intelligent Hook Detection:** Uses AI to identify segments with the highest engagement potential.
- **Viral Subtitles:** Generates high-fidelity, animated captions automatically.
- **Smart Framing:** Automatically crops 16:9 video to 9:16 portrait mode while keeping the subject in frame.
- **Content Expansion:** Generates blog posts, Twitter threads, and LinkedIn articles directly from the video transcript.

## Workflow
1. **Source:** Upload or link a video.
2. **Analyze:** AI processes audio, visual, and retention data.
3. **Curation:** Review and edit generated clips.
4. **Deploy:** Export to TikTok, Instagram, and more.
