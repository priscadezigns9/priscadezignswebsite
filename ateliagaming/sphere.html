HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE
Navigate the 3D void. Collect the spinning Data Cubes while dodging the static Corruption Nodes.
CONTROLS
DRAG your finger (mobile) or use your MOUSE (desktop) to steer the sphere through the 3D environment.
REWARDS
Every 5 cubes harvested secure +10 ATLR. Maintain high Link Strength for maximum rewards.
