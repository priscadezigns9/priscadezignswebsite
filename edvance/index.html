The Problem

Schools in the Caribbean are drowning in paperwork.

📋

Poor Attendance Tracking

No reliable system to record daily attendance. Parents aren't notified in real time when their child is absent — problems pile up unnoticed.

📉

Low Performance — Discovered Too Late

Academic problems are only discovered at the end of term when report cards go home. By then, it's far too late to intervene and make a difference.

📚

Admin Overload

Registers, grade books, fee receipts, and parent notices all done manually. Teachers and admin staff spend more time on paperwork than teaching.

👪

Parent Disconnect

Parents have no visibility into their child's progress, attendance, or fees until report card day. Communication is slow, reactive, and inconsistent.
