Professional Profile

Luna is an AI HSE Officer deployed by Prisca Dezigns for Caribbean businesses. She operates as the complete health, safety and environment compliance layer — generating Job Safety Analyses, managing incident reports, automating safety inductions, calculating TRIR, and keeping the workplace audit-ready at all times. Luna was purpose-built for the Caribbean operational reality: WhatsApp-first communication, limited in-house HSE capacity, and regulatory environments governed by the OSH Act TT 2004.

Core Responsibilities

  • Generate Job Safety Analyses (JSAs) for any task or worksite on demand via WhatsApp or email
  • Log and classify every workplace incident — near-miss, injury, or property damage — with full timestamps
  • Automate staff safety inductions — deliver, track completion, and log acknowledgement per OSH Act TT 2004
  • Calculate TRIR, LTIFR, and severity rates — broadcast weekly to management automatically
  • Distribute daily toolbox talk topics to all field staff via WhatsApp broadcast every morning
  • Maintain a live corrective action register — assign, track, and close out hazard rectifications
  • Conduct scheduled workplace inspection checklists and deliver reports to the business owner
  • Generate emergency response plan summaries and distribute to all relevant staff
  • Track PPE issuance and expiry — flag replacements 14 days before expiry with zero missed alerts
  • Deliver monthly HSE compliance report and quarterly audit summary (Enterprise)

Caribbean HSE Compliance Tracking

  • OSH Act TT 2004 — Section 13 employer duty of care and inspection readiness
  • Occupational Safety and Health Authority (OSHA TT) — audit and reporting compliance
  • Safety induction mandate — documented acknowledgement per new hire per OSH Act
  • Incident reporting deadlines — OSHA TT notification within 7 days of serious injury
  • PPE adequacy requirements — industry-specific standards per OSH Regulations TT
  • Emergency response plan documentation — current and accessible at all worksites
  • Contractor safety vetting — pre-qualification checklist and site induction log
