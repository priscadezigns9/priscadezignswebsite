Professional Profile

Luna is an AI HSE Officer deployed by Prisca Dezigns for Caribbean businesses across all industries. She operates as a full Health, Safety & Environment function — covering 14 active skills and 12 of the 15 globally benchmarked HSE KPIs with AI-grade accuracy. Luna was purpose-built for the Caribbean compliance reality: an OSH Act (2004) that most SMEs have never fully implemented, no sector-specific enforcement guidelines, and a workforce that operates on WhatsApp. She fills the compliance gap that costs businesses TT$8K–$18K per month to fill with a human officer — at a fraction of the cost, with zero sick days and zero missed incidents.

Core Responsibilities

  • Send daily safety reminders, PPE alerts, and morning briefings to all workers via WhatsApp
  • Receive and classify hazard reports by severity — route immediately to the responsible manager
  • Generate and deliver weekly toolbox talks with industry-specific content and attendance logging
  • Automate new hire safety inductions — deliver content, confirm completion, and log to record
  • Generate Job Safety Analysis (JSA) documents on demand from a WhatsApp description
  • Capture, classify, and log all incidents — near-miss, injury, and property damage — with root cause and corrective action linkage
  • Maintain a live training register for all employees — track expiry dates and send renewal reminders
  • Track every corrective action from hazard flag to verified closure
  • Compile and deliver a Weekly Safety Statistics Report every Monday morning
  • Schedule emergency drills, send reminders, and log attendance and outcome records
  • Run scheduled compliance checks against OSH Act TT and ISO 45001 — generate audit-ready gap reports
  • Calculate TRIR, LTIFR, and DART rates from incident and hours data with period-over-period trending
  • Build structured risk assessment documents through guided data collection
  • Log environmental incidents — spills, emissions, waste events — with classification and escalation
