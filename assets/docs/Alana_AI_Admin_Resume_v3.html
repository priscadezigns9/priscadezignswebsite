Professional Profile

Alana is an AI Administration Coordinator deployed by Prisca Dezigns for Caribbean businesses. She operates as the central administrative layer — receiving outputs from every connected department, routing documents, managing inboxes, tracking regulatory deadlines, and delivering a unified daily brief to the business owner. Alana was purpose-built for the Caribbean operational reality: WhatsApp-first communication, limited administrative capacity, and regulatory environments that demand consistent follow-through on NIS, BIR, and Companies Registry deadlines.

Core Responsibilities

  • Coordinate admin outputs across all connected departments — receive, file, route, and escalate
  • Manage WhatsApp and email inboxes — triage by urgency, draft responses, and log all interactions
  • Schedule and confirm appointments across multiple stakeholders with conflict resolution
  • Document intake, classification, version control, and routing for owner approval
  • Track Caribbean regulatory deadlines — NIS, BIR, business licences, company registration, import permits, OSHA
  • Send vendor and supplier follow-ups for outstanding POs, deliveries, and outstanding queries
  • Deliver unified daily briefing compiling all department outputs to the business owner
  • Internal staff coordination — shift reminders, broadcast messages, internal document routing
  • Escalate complaints, legal matters, and high-value requests through the correct protocol chain
  • Monthly executive analytics report and administrative performance dashboard (Enterprise)
