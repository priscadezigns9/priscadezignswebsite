Myth vs. Reality

Myth

"EVs are dangerous in flooding."

Reality

The Autodrome fleet is IP68-rated. Our vehicles are rigorously tested to operate in up to 2 feet of water for 30 minutes. The high-voltage system is hermetically sealed against water ingress.

Myth

"Charging takes too long for business."

Reality

Public DC Fast Chargers can reach 80% charge in just 30-45 minutes. For taxi and courier fleets, this fits perfectly into a standard lunch or rest break. Home charging handles the rest while you sleep.

Myth

"Electric cars are slow and heavy."

Reality

Electric motors deliver 100% of their torque instantly. The Hyptec HT launches from 0-100km/h in just 5.8 seconds, providing high-fidelity performance that gasoline engines cannot match.

Myth

"The battery will die in 2 years."

Reality

Modern EV batteries are designed for 3,000+ charge cycles—roughly 15-20 years of daily driving. We provide a dedicated 8-year battery warranty to ensure long-term peace of mind.
