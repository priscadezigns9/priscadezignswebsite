Privacy Policy

The Autodrome | Sovereign Automotive Engineering & Private Digital Vehicle Networks

Sovereign Data Mandate

At The Autodrome, we redefine the relationship between the driver, the vehicle, and the data they co-create. In an era of invasive automotive surveillance, we stand as a fortress for owner sovereignty. Our systems are engineered to ensure that your mobility remains private, secure, and under your absolute control.

EV Diagnostic Data Ownership

Your Data, Your Vehicle. We believe that the diagnostic data generated by your vehicle is your intellectual property. Unlike traditional manufacturers who monetize telemetry and performance logs, The Autodrome ensures that all data generated by our EV fleet remains the exclusive property of the vehicle owner.

We do not aggregate or sell diagnostic snapshots. Your vehicle's health, performance metrics, and usage patterns are stored locally or within your private cloud, accessible only by you or authorized technicians you explicitly designate.

Private Vehicle Telemetry

Non-Surveillance Protocol. Our telemetry systems are built on a foundation of 'Privacy by Design.' We utilize private digital vehicle networks to facilitate necessary communications between vehicle subsystems. This data is transmitted via encrypted channels and is never routed through public data brokers or third-party marketing platforms.

We do not track real-time location for the purpose of profiling or advertising. Any location-based services are opt-in and processed locally whenever possible to ensure your movements remain your own.

Identity-Gated Performance Updates

Secure Evolution. Performance enhancements, battery management optimizations, and software-defined vehicle updates are gated by your unique sovereign identity. This cryptographic handshake ensures that only the verified owner can initiate modifications to the vehicle's core engineering parameters.

This approach prevents unauthorized remote tampering and ensures that the integrity of your high-performance engineering is never compromised by external actors.

Disclosure & Transparency

The Autodrome will never disclose vehicle data to insurance companies, law enforcement, or government agencies without a valid warrant or cryptographically signed consent from the vehicle owner. We do not participate in industry-wide data-sharing consortiums that compromise driver anonymity.

Updates to this Policy

As the landscape of automotive engineering evolves, so will our privacy protocols. Any changes to this policy will be communicated through our private owner network and will always prioritize the sovereign rights of the driver.
