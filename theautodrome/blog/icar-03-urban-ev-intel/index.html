iCAR 03: Rugged Urban Mobility

Chery's new dedicated EV brand, iCAR, has launched its first flagship: the iCAR 03. This rugged, boxy SUV is designed for the "Urban Explorer"—the driver who navigates the concrete jungle by day but craves weekend adventures in the wild. With its aluminum multi-chamber cage body and intelligent electric four-wheel drive, the iCAR 03 is a high-fidelity tool for the modern lifestyle. At The Autodrome, we've dissected the 03 to see if its performance matches its bold, geometric aesthetic.

Technical Mastery: Off-Road Intel

The iCAR 03 is built on a high-strength aluminum frame, which is significantly lighter and more rigid than traditional steel bodies. This weight reduction directly translates to better range and handling. The vehicle features a 70 kWh LFP battery, providing a range of up to 501 km (CLTC). Its intelligent electric AWD system can adjust torque distribution in milliseconds, ensuring traction on everything from wet asphalt to loose gravel.

What sets the iCAR 03 apart is its approach to "Digital Intelligence." The vehicle is equipped with a Qualcomm Snapdragon 8155 chip, powering an ecosystem that includes L2+ autonomous driving and a high-resolution 15.6-inch touchscreen. For the Caribbean tech enthusiast, the iCAR 03 offers a level of connectivity and automation that was previously reserved for vehicles twice its price.

Pros vs. Cons: The Explorer's Log

Pros: Unique, boxy design that stands out from the "egg-shaped" EV crowd. High-strength aluminum construction provides superior safety and durability. Excellent value for an AWD electric SUV.

Cons: The boxy design results in higher wind resistance (drag), which can impact highway range compared to more aerodynamic competitors. The ride quality is tuned for "adventure," meaning it is firmer than a traditional luxury cruiser.

Cost Analysis: Caribbean Market Context

In Trinidad and Tobago, the iCAR 03 is a disruptor. Thanks to the tax exemptions for EVs, the 03 is priced competitively against entry-level gasoline crossovers. For the price of a standard petrol SUV, buyers can own an AWD electric vehicle with an aluminum chassis and cutting-edge AI. This makes the iCAR 03 one of the most compelling options for young professionals and adventurers in the Caribbean.
