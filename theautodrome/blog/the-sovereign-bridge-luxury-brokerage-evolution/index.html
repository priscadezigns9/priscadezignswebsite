The distance from primary manufacturing hubs in Europe and North America, combined with the lack of localized representation for brands like Lamborghini, Ferrari, and McLaren, has forced regional collectors to navigate a maze of secondary markets, unreliable importers, and logistical nightmares. The result was often a compromise—either in the specification of the vehicle or in the transparency of the transaction.

The Autodrome’s evolution is a direct response to this void. We are not a dealership in the traditional sense; we are a Brokerage. This distinction is critical. Instead, we represent the interests of the client, acting as their sovereign agent in the global market to secure exact allocations, negotiate bespoke specifications, and manage the complex chain of custody from the factory floor to the roads of South Trinidad.
