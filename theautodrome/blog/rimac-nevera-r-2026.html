Next-Generation R-AWTV & Handling

The core of the Nevera R's cornering dominance is the Rimac All-Wheel Torque Vectoring (R-AWTV) system. In the R, this system has been recalibrated to handle the increased downforce and mechanical grip. It calculates the optimal torque for each wheel over 100 times per second, ensuring that the vehicle rotates with surgical precision through high-speed apexes. Unlike traditional mechanical differentials, the R-AWTV can switch from neutral handling to aggressive oversteer in milliseconds, providing the driver with a high-fidelity connection to the asphalt.

The mechanical grip has been significantly enhanced through a new partnership with Michelin, featuring the Pilot Sport Cup 2 Connect tires. These tires are designed specifically to handle the immense lateral loads generated by the R's revised suspension geometry and increased negative camber. The result is a 13% improvement in cornering speeds compared to the standard Nevera, making the R a true track-day weapon.

Aero-Intelligence: The 400kg Advantage

Every element of the Nevera R's exterior is a function of aerodynamic intelligence. The most striking addition is the fixed large rear wing, which works in synergy with the revised front diffuser and active underbody flaps. This package generates over 400kg of downforce at top speed—a significant increase over the base model—ensuring that the vehicle remains sucked to the ground even as it approaches 430 km/h.

The front end has been redesigned with larger intakes and more aggressive air curtains to manage thermal loads for the quad-motor system. At The Autodrome, we've analyzed the cooling telemetry: the Nevera R features a 20% increase in cooling capacity, allowing it to sustain peak power delivery for multiple laps around the Nürburgring without thermal degradation. This is the new gold standard for electric endurance in the hyper-performance niche.

The Cabin: High-Fidelity Minimalism

Inside the Nevera R, the focus remains on the driver. The cabin is a blend of lightweight carbon fiber and Alcantara, designed to minimize weight while maintaining an elite aesthetic. The unified digital interface provides real-time telemetry, allowing the driver to monitor motor temps, battery state-of-charge, and G-loads through a high-resolution HUD. There are no generic components here; every dial and switch is custom-engineered to reflect the Rimac heritage of innovation and precision.

The Nevera R is more than just an evolution; it is a declaration of intent. It proves that the limits of electric performance are still far from being reached.
