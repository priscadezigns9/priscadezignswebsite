## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### 1.1 Overview
Hartaly is an AI-powered mental wellness platform designed to bridge the gap between expensive clinical therapy and passive meditation apps. Powered by GPT-4o, Hartaly provides 24/7, high-fidelity emotional coaching, guided CBT exercises, and personalized mood tracking. Unlike traditional apps, Hartaly offers persistent memory and nuanced conversation, making it feel like a human companion rather than a robotic assistant.

### 1.2 The Problem
*   **Accessibility:** Clinical therapy is prohibitively expensive ($60-$150/session).
*   **Availability:** Crises don't wait for business hours; therapists have waitlists.
*   **Engagement:** Passive apps (Calm/Headspace) are repetitive and lack interactive support.
*   **Privacy:** Recent data leaks in the therapy industry have eroded user trust.

### 1.3 The Vision
To become the global "First Responder" for mental wellness—providing immediate, affordable, and private emotional support to every human with a smartphone, ensuring no one has to face their dark moments alone.

## 4. PRODUCT FEATURES

### 4.1 MVP (Launch)
*   **Hartaly Chat:** 24/7 GPT-4o emotional coaching with long-term memory (Sovereign Memory).
*   **Mood Tracker:** Daily interactive check-ins with a heatmap calendar for trend analysis.
*   **Guided Journal:** AI-generated prompts based on yesterday’s mood, providing real-time feedback.
*   **CBT Toolkit:** 50+ evidence-based exercises (Thought challenging, behavioral activation).
*   **Breathing Room:** 6 interactive techniques (4-7-8, Box Breathing, Cyclic Sighing).
*   **Crisis Protocol:** Immediate detection of self-harm language with local hotline routing.

### 4.2 Phase 2 (Month 3)
*   **Voice Mode:** Real-time, low-latency vocal therapy using GPT-4o Audio.
*   **Sleep Insights:** Correlation analysis between sleep patterns and mood dips.
*   **Hartaly Circles:** Anonymous peer-to-peer support groups moderated by AI.
*   **Wearable Sync:** Apple Watch/Whoop integration to detect physiological stress spikes.

### 4.3 Phase 3 (Month 6)
*   **Family Plan:** Consent-based mood sharing for families and couples.
*   **Therapist Bridge:** Seamless referral to human therapists for clinical-grade needs.
*   **Hartaly Enterprise:** B2B wellness tier for employee burnout prevention.

## 10. LEGAL & ETHICAL FRAMEWORK

### 10.1 Disclaimers
"Hartaly is an AI wellness coach, not a licensed therapist or medical professional. It cannot diagnose or treat clinical disorders."

### 10.2 Crisis Protocol
1.  **Detection:** AI monitors for 200+ "red flag" phrases.
2.  **Intervention:** Immediate cessation of coaching; display of 988 (US), 111 (UK), or local equivalent based on IP.
3.  **Logging:** Crisis events are logged for liability protection (anonymized).

### 10.3 Data Privacy
*   **Zero-Knowledge Storage (Goal):** Encrypting chat history with user-derived keys.
*   **HIPAA-Adjacent Compliance:** SOC2 Type II standards for data at rest and in transit.
