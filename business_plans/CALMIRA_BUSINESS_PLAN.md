## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### 1.1 Product Overview
**AuraNode** is a high-fidelity, AI-powered mental wellness ecosystem designed for deep cognitive-behavioral intervention without human overhead. Leveraging GPT-4o, AuraNode provides 24/7 personalized coaching, guided journaling, and real-time mood diagnostics. It bridges the gap between passive meditation apps and expensive human therapy by offering emergent, empathetic conversation that evolves with the user.

### 1.2 Company Vision
To democratize high-fidelity mental health support, making "human-level" coaching accessible to every sovereign individual, ensuring that burnout, grief, and anxiety never remain untreated due to cost or availability.
