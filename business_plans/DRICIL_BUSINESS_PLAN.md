### 5. HOW IT WORKS (The Autonomous Flow)

1.  **Autonomous Onboarding:** Client visits dricil.com, selects a plan, and completes a "Brand DNA" intake form.
2.  **Brand Voice Generation:** AI analyzes the intake form, website, and existing social media to create a unique **Brand Voice Profile** (stored in Supabase).
3.  **Calendar Orchestration:** AI generates a 30-day content calendar tailored to the client's industry and goals.
4.  **Autonomous Production:** GPT-4o generates captions/copy; DALL-E 3 generates high-fidelity images.
5.  **Direct Scheduling:** Content is pushed via the **Buffer API** to the client's social channels.
6.  **Cycle Completion:** AI monitors engagement, generates a PDF report at month-end, and triggers the renewal billing via PayPal/Crypto.
