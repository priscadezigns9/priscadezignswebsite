# Les Amour Business Plan — Orcinos Product

## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Les Amour** is a premium, marriage-first intimacy brand designed to bridge the gap between clinical marriage counseling and explicit adult retailers. Rooted in faith and the theology of covenant, Les Amour serves married couples who desire to deepen their emotional, spiritual, and physical connection in a tasteful, empowering environment.

### Vision Statement
To become the global standard for sacred marital intimacy, empowering ten million couples to reclaim the joy and holiness of their physical and emotional union.

### The Problem
Christian and conservative married couples often face a binary: sterile, clinical therapy that lacks passion, or explicit, pornographic "adult stores" that contradict their values. There is a profound lack of high-fidelity, faith-rooted resources that address physical intimacy with both technical excellence and spiritual reverence.

### Why Now?
The "Marriage Wellness" market is surging as couples shift from reactive therapy to proactive relationship enrichment. With the rise of AI, personalized coaching is now scalable and accessible 24/7, allowing for private, sensitive conversations that many couples are hesitant to have with human counselors.
