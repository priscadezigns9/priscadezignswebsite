Common Questions

What people ask us

What is Prisca Dezigns?
Prisca Dezigns is a full-service creative and digital agency that helps entrepreneurs, solo traders, and small businesses build their brand from the ground up. From logo design and web development to app building, copywriting, and business management tools — we cover everything a growing business needs.
What is Velloq?
Velloq is Prisca Dezigns' web design and development tool. It creates custom, professional websites for any business type — portfolio, e-commerce, service pages, landing pages — designed to reflect the brand's personality and built to perform.
What is Moblync?
Moblync is Prisca Dezigns' mobile app design and development service. It builds iOS and Android applications for brands ready to put their business directly in their customers' hands.
What is Cupyx?
Cupyx is an AI-powered copywriting tool by Prisca Dezigns. It generates captions, content ideas, ad copy, email text, and website copy — written in your brand voice so it actually sounds like you, not a robot.
What is Rowcell?
Rowcell is Prisca Dezigns' bookkeeping and business finance tool. It helps entrepreneurs and solo traders track receipts, manage expenses, and keep their books in order — without needing an accountant.
What is Karjov?
Karjov is Prisca Dezigns' business management platform. It brings together client management, project tracking, tasks, and operations into one clean dashboard — built for busy entrepreneurs who need to stay on top of everything.
Who is Prisca Dezigns for?
Prisca Dezigns is built for entrepreneurs, solo traders, and startups — especially those who are just starting out, those who have a business but not yet a brand, and those who know their idea has potential but aren't sure how to bring it to life. We take every business seriously, no matter how early the stage.
Can I get all services in one package?
Yes. Prisca Dezigns offers full-service packages that combine branding, web design, app development, content creation, and business tools — customised to your specific needs and budget. Contact us via WhatsApp or email to discuss your package.
