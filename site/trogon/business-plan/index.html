01Executive Summary

Mission: Grow your brand daily — without lifting a finger. Trogon is a done-for-you social media management and content growth service. You connect your pages once; Trogon's expert team handles everything else.

Trogon is a managed social media service that handles content creation, scheduling, and daily posting across Facebook, Instagram, and other platforms for businesses and personal brands. The name comes from the Trogon bird — a vibrant, striking species with brilliant colours and precision, reflecting the brand's commitment to standing out in crowded social feeds.

A live product already exists at priscadezigns.org/trogon/, serving clients across multiple niches. Trogon is uniquely positioned as both a standalone service and an anchor product within the Prisca Dezigns ecosystem.

07Go-to-Market Strategy
1
Phase 1 — Foundation (Months 1–3)

Serve existing Prisca Dezigns clients first. Bundle Trogon with all new web design and branding packages. Target: 10 paying clients. Focus on quality delivery and gathering testimonials.

2
Phase 2 — Outbound & Content Marketing (Months 4–6)

Run Facebook and Instagram ads targeting small business owners. Showcase before/after page growth. Use Trogon's own pages as proof of concept — the 18 managed pages ARE the portfolio. Target: 30 clients.

3
Phase 3 — Agency Partnerships (Months 7–12)

White-label Trogon to other marketing agencies and freelancers who need a content production backend. Position as a B2B backend, not just a direct service. Target: 5 agency partners + 75 total clients.

4
Phase 4 — Platform Automation (Months 13–18)

Build a client dashboard — clients can review scheduled posts, request edits, and track page analytics. Reduce manual coordination. Increase capacity without adding headcount. Target: 150+ clients.
