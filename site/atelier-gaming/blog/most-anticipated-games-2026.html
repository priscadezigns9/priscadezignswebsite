Editorial

Top 10 Most Anticipated Games of 2026

📅 May 2026 ⏱ 9 min read 🎮 Upcoming Releases

2026 is shaping up to be one of the most stacked gaming years in recent memory. After years of delays, studio reorganizations, and post-pandemic production ripples, the pipeline is finally catching up — and the games sitting at the top of that pipeline are genuinely historic. From franchise revivals to long-awaited sequels, here are the ten games everyone in the gaming community has circled on their calendar.

01
GTA VI
Rockstar Games · Action/Open World · PS5, Xbox Series X|S, PC

The most anticipated game in history — and that title has held for three years now. GTA VI returns to Vice City with a female protagonist for the first time in the main series. From what Rockstar has shown, the world density, economy simulation, and storytelling ambition appear generational. The PC release window remains uncertain, but console players will finally have their answer. Every benchmark for open-world games will be reset when this ships.

02
Battlefield 6
EA/DICE · Multiplayer FPS · PS5, Xbox Series X|S, PC

After the chaos of Battlefield 2042, EA and DICE are making a very deliberate return to form. Battlefield 6 goes back to a modern military setting with emphasis on large-scale combined-arms warfare, destruction systems that put BF4 to shame, and a live-service model designed to sustain the community rather than monetize it into exhaustion. Early footage shows 128-player servers with real-time terrain deformation. If DICE delivers on what's been promised, this is a serious contender for game of the year.

03
Hollow Knight: Silksong
Team Cherry · Action/Metroidvania · PC, Switch, PS5, Xbox

Silksong has become something of a meme for its extended development — but Team Cherry's meticulous approach to the original Hollow Knight produced one of the greatest games ever made. Hornet's adventure promises a larger world, a completely new moveset with 40+ unique abilities, and the same handcrafted precision that made the first game a masterpiece. When this finally drops, expect it to dominate the conversation for months.

04
Fable
Playground Games · RPG · Xbox Series X|S, PC

Playground Games — the studio behind the Forza Horizon series — is rebooting Fable, one of the most beloved and under-served RPG franchises in gaming history. The new Fable promises the humor, moral consequence system, and whimsical British aesthetic of the originals, rebuilt for modern hardware. After years of teasers, the game appears to finally be close. It's one of Xbox's biggest exclusive bets of this generation.

05
Death Stranding 2: On the Beach
Kojima Productions · Action/Adventure · PS5

Hideo Kojima returns with the sequel to his most divisive — and most fascinating — game. Death Stranding 2 expands the world, deepens the social strand system, and appears to have a more immediate action focus alongside the meditative delivery gameplay. Norman Reedus returns alongside an ensemble of new and returning faces. Whatever Kojima is building, it will be unlike anything else released in 2026.

06
Monster Hunter Wilds (Year 1 Content)
Capcom · Action RPG · PS5, Xbox Series X|S, PC

Monster Hunter Wilds launched in late 2025 to enormous acclaim, and its major Year 1 content expansion — adding new biomes, flagship monsters, and crossover events — is positioned as one of 2026's biggest releases. For those who haven't entered the franchise yet, Wilds is the most accessible entry point ever created, and the expanded content makes 2026 the year to start.

07
The Elder Scrolls VI
Bethesda Game Studios · RPG · Xbox Series X|S, PC (TBC)

Still in early development by most accounts, TES VI makes this list because any concrete reveal in 2026 — a teaser, a name, a setting announcement — would be a seismic gaming moment. Bethesda has said the game will redefine their Creation Engine work. Whether we see meaningful gameplay in 2026 or not, any news moves markets and dominates headlines. It's the wildcard that belongs on every list.

08
Marathon
Bungie · Extraction Shooter · PS5, PC

Bungie's extraction shooter reboot of their 1990s Marathon franchise enters live service in 2026 after considerable community feedback reshaping its design. A PvP-focused extraction game with Bungie's legendary gunfeel is an exciting proposition. The studio's track record with Destiny suggests they understand long-term player retention better than almost anyone in the industry. Marathon is a dark horse candidate for the year's most played game.

09
Mafia: The Old Country
Hangar 13 · Narrative Action · PS5, Xbox Series X|S, PC

The Mafia series returns to its roots — Sicily, the early 20th century, and a story about the origins of organized crime in America. After the mixed reception of Mafia III and the solid Mafia II remaster, The Old Country appears to be a genuine creative reset. The early footage showed stunning production value and a grounded narrative tone that recalls the original trilogy's best moments.

10
Pragmata
Capcom · Action/Sci-Fi · PS5, Xbox Series X|S, PC

Capcom's mysterious sci-fi action title set in a future-Earth gone wrong. Originally teased in 2020 and delayed repeatedly, Pragmata appears to finally be approaching release. The premise — a father-daughter survival story in a compromised orbital city — and the visual aesthetic suggest something ambitious and emotionally resonant. It's the most unknown quantity on this list, which makes it one of the most intriguing.
