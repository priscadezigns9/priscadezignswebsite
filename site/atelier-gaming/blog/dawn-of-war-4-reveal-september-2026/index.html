For the Emperor! Dawn of War IV Officially Revealed for September 2026

Published: May 23, 2026

Strategic command is returning to the 41st Millennium. This week, the gaming world received the news many had whispered about for years: Warhammer 40,000: Dawn of War IV is officially in development and set to debut in September 2026.

The Return to Strategic Roots

After the experimental direction of the third installment, leaks and early reports suggest that Dawn of War IV is returning to the beloved mechanics that made the original series a staple of the RTS genre. Expect massive scale, deep base building, and the brutal squad-level combat that defines the Warhammer 40,000 experience. The debut trailer showcases stunning next-gen visuals, featuring a desperate clash between the Space Marines and a massive Tyranid swarm.

What to Expect in September

The September release window is strategic, aiming for the core holiday season rush. Developers have promised a "comprehensive campaign" at launch, along with a robust competitive multiplayer suite. For fans of the franchise, the promise of modernized graphics combined with classic strategic depth is the dream scenario.
