Rare Air Returns: First Look at the 2026 Air Jordan 4 'Tour Yellow' Retro

Published: May 23, 2026

Sneakerheads, mark your calendars. This week, we got our first official "first look" at one of the most anticipated retros of the year: the Air Jordan 4 'Tour Yellow'. Set to return as part of the "Rare Air" series, this release marks the 20th anniversary of the original 2006 debut.

A Vibrant Legacy Reimagined

The 2026 version stays faithful to the high-energy color blocking that made the 'Tour Yellow' a grail for many collectors. Featuring that iconic vibrant yellow nubuck upper, dark grey accents on the wings and eyelets, and the classic white midsole, this pair is a masterclass in mid-2000s sneaker design. Early leaks suggest the quality of the materials has been elevated to meet modern 'Retro' standards, ensuring a premium feel on-foot.

Release Details: September 5, 2026

The official release date is currently set for September 5, 2026. Sources indicate it will arrive in full-family sizing, meaning everyone from toddlers to adults can get in on the action. Given the "Rare Air" branding, expect these to be a limited drop with significant hype surrounding the launch.
