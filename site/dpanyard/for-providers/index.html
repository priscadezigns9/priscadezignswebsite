Getting Started
From sign-up to
first inquiry in minutes

Three simple steps to get your business listed and start connecting with local customers.

📝

1. Create Your Profile

Fill in your business name, service category, location, contact info, and a description of what you do. Add photos of your work to stand out. The more complete your profile, the higher you rank.

  • Add your service specialties — be specific about what you offer
  • Upload photos — before/after work, your workspace, your team
  • Set your service area — neighborhoods or radius you cover
  • Add pricing info — optional but increases conversion
✅

2. Get Verified

Our team reviews your submission to confirm your identity and the services you provide. The verification badge tells customers you're legitimate — it's the biggest trust driver on the platform.

  • Identity verification — government-issued ID
  • Business confirmation — for registered businesses
  • Credential check — for licensed trades (electricians, etc.)
📞

3. Start Getting Contacted

Your profile is live. Customers searching in your area find you, read your reviews, browse your photos, and reach out directly. From there, it's just you and the customer — no platform interference.

  • Respond quickly — providers with fast response times rank higher
  • Ask for reviews — every happy client is a 5-star opportunity
  • Upgrade to Featured or Pro — to appear at the top of search results
