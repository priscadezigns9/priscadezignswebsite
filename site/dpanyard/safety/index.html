Provider Verification
How we verify
every provider

Before any provider goes live on D' PanYard, they pass our verification process. A listing without the verified badge has not yet cleared this process.

Step 1
📋

Application Review

Every provider submits a detailed profile including their full name, service description, contact number, and service area. Our team manually reviews each submission.

Step 2
🪪

Identity Confirmation

We verify the provider's identity using government-issued ID. This confirms the real person behind the listing and ties accountability to the profile.

Step 3
📜

Credential Check

For regulated services (electricians, plumbers, etc.), providers must submit proof of licensure or certification. Uncertified providers are not listed in regulated categories.

Step 4
🏅

Badge Awarded

Once verified, the provider's profile displays the D' PanYard Verified badge — a visible trust signal for every customer browsing the platform.

Ongoing
🔄

Continuous Monitoring

Verified status is not permanent. Providers who receive repeated complaints, low reviews, or violations have their badges reviewed and can be delisted.

Review System
Reviews you can
actually trust

Fake reviews damage trust. Every review on D' PanYard follows strict authenticity rules so the ratings you see reflect real customer experiences.

🧾

Verified Customers Only

Only customers who have confirmed contact with a provider can leave a review. No anonymous ratings, no self-reviews.

📝

Detailed Review Fields

Reviewers rate quality, punctuality, and value separately — giving you a nuanced picture beyond a single star score.

🚫

Anti-Manipulation Controls

Providers cannot delete negative reviews. Attempts to manipulate ratings result in immediate account review and possible delisting.

🏳️

Review Flagging

Both customers and providers can flag reviews that appear inaccurate or violate guidelines. Our team investigates all flags within 48 hours.

💬

Provider Responses

Providers can respond to any review — a positive sign of professionalism and customer care that boosts trust with future clients.

⏱️

Recency Weighting

Older reviews carry less weight in the overall rating — ensuring a provider's current performance is accurately reflected.

Safety FAQ
Common safety
questions answered

Everything you need to know about staying safe on D' PanYard.

What does the Verified badge actually mean? ▾
The Verified badge means D' PanYard has confirmed the provider's identity using a government-issued ID, and for regulated trades, has verified their professional certification. It does not guarantee the quality of work but confirms the person is who they claim to be.
Can anyone leave a review for a provider? ▾
No. Only customers who have made confirmed contact with a provider through D' PanYard can leave a review. This prevents anonymous negative reviews and review manipulation by the provider themselves.
What happens when I report a provider? ▾
Our safety team reviews the report, reaches out to both parties if needed, and takes appropriate action — which can range from issuing a warning to immediate account suspension. Safety-critical reports are prioritized and reviewed within 24 hours.
Is my personal information shared with providers? ▾
D' PanYard does not sell or share your personal data with providers. When you contact a provider, you share only what you choose to — such as your phone number or location for the service. We never share your payment information or account data.
What should I do if a provider asks for payment before work starts? ▾
Small deposits for materials are sometimes standard practice, but be cautious of large upfront payments — especially in cash. Always get a written agreement before paying anything. If a provider pressures you for upfront payment, report it through the platform.
Can a provider remove negative reviews? ▾
No. Providers cannot delete reviews. They can respond to any review and flag reviews they believe violate guidelines — but the decision to remove a review rests solely with our moderation team, based on policy.
