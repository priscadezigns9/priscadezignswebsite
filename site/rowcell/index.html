Common Questions

What people ask about Rowcell

What is Rowcell?
Rowcell is Prisca Dezigns' business finance and bookkeeping tool. It helps entrepreneurs and solo traders track receipts, manage expenses, and keep their books in order — without needing an accountant or accounting experience.
Do I need accounting knowledge to use Rowcell?
None at all. Rowcell is specifically designed for people who are not accountants. The interface is simple, the language is plain, and the process is straightforward.
Can Rowcell replace my accountant?
For day-to-day tracking, expense management, and basic bookkeeping — yes. For complex tax filings or advanced financial planning, you may still want a professional for those specific tasks.
What size business is Rowcell for?
Rowcell is built for solo traders, freelancers, and small businesses. If you are running a business where you are managing the finances yourself, Rowcell was built for you.
