Our Story

About Verdant Co

“Sustainability isn’t a sacrifice. It’s a standard we chose to set — for ourselves, our homes, and the planet we share.”

Who We Are

Verdant Co is a curated editorial platform dedicated to eco-friendly and sustainable living. We are not a brand that manufactures products — we are editors, researchers, and advocates who find the best sustainable products available on the market and bring them directly to you.

We are a property of Prisca Dezigns, a Caribbean-born digital brand empire founded with the belief that high-quality, purposeful online spaces can change how people shop, think, and live.

Why We Started

Sustainable living can feel overwhelming. Between greenwashing, confusing certifications, and products that promise more than they deliver, it’s hard to know what actually makes a difference. Verdant Co exists to cut through the noise.

Our editorial team researches every product category — from zero-waste kitchen essentials to organic bedroom linens — so you don’t have to. We only feature products we genuinely believe in, backed by real certifications and verified environmental claims.

Our Values

Radical Transparency

We disclose all affiliate relationships. We earn commissions when you shop through our links — at no extra cost to you. We will never recommend a product purely for commission.

Real Impact

We focus on changes that actually move the needle — not guilt over straws, but real swaps that reduce plastic, emissions, and waste over years.

Accessible Sustainability

Eco-friendly shouldn’t mean expensive. We curate products at every price point so sustainable living is available to everyone.

Community First

We are building a community of conscious shoppers who share tips, discoveries, and wins on the journey toward greener living.
