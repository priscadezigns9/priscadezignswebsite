Affiliate Disclosure

We believe in full transparency. Here is exactly how affiliate links work on Peak Fit — and why it costs you nothing extra.

The Short Version

When you click a product link on Peak Fit and make a purchase on Amazon, we may earn a small commission. This costs you nothing extra. You pay the same price you would pay going to Amazon directly.

Our recommendations are honest and independent. We curate premium fitness gear and activewear because we genuinely believe in what we recommend — not because of commission rates.

What Is Affiliate Marketing?

Affiliate marketing is a way for content publishers — like Peak Fit — to earn a small fee for recommending products. When you click a product link on Peak Fit, that link contains a unique identifier that tells Amazon you arrived from our site. If you go on to make a purchase, Amazon pays us a small percentage of the sale as a referral fee.

This commission comes from Amazon's marketing budget — not from your pocket. The price you pay on Amazon is identical whether you click our link or go to Amazon directly. There are no hidden fees, no markups, and no catches.

Affiliate marketing is how independent publishers like us are able to create free, high-quality editorial content without charging readers or running traditional display advertising.

Honest & Independent Recommendations

We want you to trust our recommendations — and that means being honest about how we operate. Here is our editorial commitment to you:

  • We select products based on quality, relevance, and value — not commission rates.
  • We do not accept payment from brands or manufacturers to feature their products.
  • We do not inflate reviews or hide negative aspects of products we feature.
  • Commission rates on Amazon vary by product category but have no influence on which products we choose to recommend.
  • If we link to a product, it is because we believe it is genuinely worth your consideration.
  • We are not employees, contractors, or agents of Amazon — we are an independent publisher.

No Extra Cost to You

When you use our affiliate links, you will never pay more than the standard Amazon price for any product. The commission we earn is paid entirely by Amazon out of their marketing budget — it is not added to your purchase price in any way.

You are free to purchase any product we recommend by going directly to Amazon.com if you prefer not to use our affiliate link. We appreciate your support either way.
