Cardi B x Fashionphile: The 2026 'Get Your Bag' Campaign Redefining Luxury Resale

Luxury resale has a new face, and it's none other than Grammy-winning artist **Cardi B**. In May 2026, **Fashionphile** officially launched its 'Get Your Bag' campaign featuring Cardi B, pushing the narrative of designer bags as investment pieces and celebrating the culture of luxury resale. Cardi B, known for her extensive and rare Hermès Birkin collection, is the perfect ambassador for a brand that specializes in pre-owned ultra-luxury. The campaign highlights how savvy collectors can 'trade up' their bags, treating high-end accessories like a revolving door of style and finance. The viral campaign has already sparked a massive increase in searches for 'investment bags,' with the Chanel Classic Flap and Hermès Birkin topping the charts. In a year where retail prices for new luxury goods continue to skyrocket, Fashionphile and Cardi B are making a compelling case for the value and sustainability of the secondary market. Whether you're looking to start your collection or find that one-of-a-kind piece, the 'Get Your Bag' era is here to stay. Luxury is no longer just about the initial purchase; it's about the legacy and the resale value of the pieces you love.
