🧵 Seamrite Designs 📍 Fyzabad, Trinidad & Tobago Est. 2018

From word-of-mouth
to web presence.

A skilled seamstress. A vision. A name. But no logo, no website, no brand — just talent and word-of-mouth in Fyzabad. Prisca Dezigns built everything else from the ground up.

Industry Custom Clothing
Location South Trinidad
Services Full-Package Build
The Challenge

A craft without a canvas.

When Seamrite Designs first connected with us, the business existed almost entirely by word-of-mouth. The owner had been designing and sewing custom pieces since 2018 — dresses, uniforms, swimwear, costumes, bridal wear, alterations — all of it crafted by hand with real skill.

But outside of Fyzabad, nobody knew she existed. There was no logo. No website. No social media presence with consistent branding. No way for a potential customer in Port of Spain — let alone abroad — to find her, trust her, or book her.

  • No brand identity — no logo, no color palette, no defined visual style
  • No digital presence — not discoverable on Google, no website, no booking flow
  • No marketing materials — no flyers, banners, or business cards
  • No business infrastructure — no business plan, no email, no shop
  • Growth was limited to people who already knew her personally
What We Built

Everything. From scratch.

The Result

A brand that matches the craft.

Seamrite Designs has been creating since 2018. The skill was always there. What was missing was the infrastructure to let the world see it. That's what Prisca Dezigns built.

Today, Seamrite Designs isn't just a word-of-mouth operation in Fyzabad — it's a professional, discoverable, bookable business with a brand identity that communicates quality before a single stitch is made.

🌐
Zero to full digital presence From no online footprint to a live, professional website with services, about, and direct booking — all in one place.
🔍
Discoverable on Google Now findable for custom clothing, dressmaking, and alterations in Fyzabad and across south Trinidad — by people who never heard of her before.
💬
Direct WhatsApp bookings Customers can go from discovering the business online to booking directly — no friction, no middleman, just a tap to WhatsApp.
🎨
Consistent brand identity across all touchpoints Whether someone sees a flyer, a business card, the website, or an Instagram post — it all looks and feels like the same professional brand.
📱
Growing Instagram presence Branded content on Instagram (@seamritedesign2023) is building an audience that can now back up what the website promises.
✨
Brand credibility that opens doors A business plan, professional email, and complete brand kit means she can now approach schools, events, and corporate clients with confidence.
