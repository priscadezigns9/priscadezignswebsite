1. Introduction

Prisca Dezigns operates a network of curated brand websites under the priscadezigns.org domain. This Privacy Policy applies to all websites within the Prisca Dezigns network (collectively, "our sites," "we," "us," or "our"). By using any of our sites, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling any personal information we collect with care and respect. This policy explains clearly what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate our sites and provide you with a quality experience. We collect information in two ways:

Information you provide directly:

  • Contact forms: If you submit a contact or inquiry form on any of our sites, we collect your name and email address, along with any message content you provide.
  • Newsletter sign-ups: If you subscribe to a newsletter or email list, we collect your name and email address.
  • Style quizzes and interactive tools: Responses you provide to on-site quizzes are used solely to generate recommendations within your current session. We do not store quiz responses.

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data: We may collect standard web analytics data including page views, time spent on pages, browser type, device type, operating system, and referring URL. This information is aggregated and used to understand how visitors use our sites.
  • IP address: Your IP address may be logged automatically by our hosting infrastructure as part of standard server operations.
Important: We do not collect payment information of any kind. All purchases are completed directly on Amazon.com or other third-party retailer platforms, which handle all payment processing under their own privacy and security policies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To send newsletters, product recommendations, and other updates that you have opted into receiving.
  • To respond to inquiries and messages submitted through our contact forms.
  • To analyse site usage patterns and improve the quality, relevance, and usability of our curated content and recommendations.
  • To maintain the security and proper functioning of our sites.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. Your information is used solely as described in this policy.

4. Amazon Associates Disclosure

Prisca Dezigns participates in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

When you click a product link on any of our sites and subsequently make a purchase on Amazon.com, we may earn a small commission. This commission is paid by Amazon and comes at no additional cost to you — you pay the same price you would pay visiting Amazon directly.

Amazon operates its own platform with its own privacy policy and data practices, which govern all data collected during your activity on Amazon.com. We encourage you to review Amazon's Privacy Notice for full details on how Amazon collects and uses your data.

5. Cookies

Our sites use a minimal number of cookies to support basic functionality and improve your browsing experience. Specifically:

  • Session cookies: Used to remember your progress through interactive tools such as style quizzes, so that your experience is smooth within a single visit.
  • Preference cookies: Used to remember filter selections or display preferences you have set during a session.

We do not use tracking cookies, behavioural advertising cookies, or third-party advertising network cookies on our sites. We do not retarget you with advertisements based on your activity on our sites.

You can manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain interactive features on our sites. For guidance on managing cookies in your browser, refer to your browser's help documentation.

6. Third-Party Links

Our sites contain links to Amazon.com and, in some cases, other third-party websites and services. These links are provided for your convenience and to enable you to access products and services we recommend.

We have no control over the content, privacy practices, or data collection methods of external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of any third-party site, including Amazon.com. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any external site you visit before providing any personal information.

7. Email Communications

If you subscribe to a newsletter or mailing list through any of our sites, we will send you occasional emails featuring curated product recommendations, editorial content, and network updates relevant to that brand.

You can unsubscribe from any of our email lists at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link included in the footer of every email we send. Upon unsubscribing, your email address will be removed from that mailing list promptly.

We use FormSubmit for processing contact form submissions. FormSubmit forwards your form submissions to our designated email address. Please review FormSubmit's privacy policy if you wish to understand how form data is handled in transit.

8. Children's Privacy

Our sites are intended for a general adult audience and are not directed to, or designed for, children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly solicit or collect personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us at priscadezigns9@gmail.com and we will take prompt steps to delete such information from our records.
