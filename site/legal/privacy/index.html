Your privacy matters. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have over your data. We keep it plain and straightforward.

Prisca Dezigns is a digital agency and brand development company operating priscadezigns.org and a network of affiliated brand platforms, apps, and SaaS tools. When this policy refers to "Prisca Dezigns," "we," "us," or "our," it means this organisation and all platforms we operate.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Information you provide directly: name, email address, or other contact details submitted via forms, waitlists, or email inquiries.

  • Cookies and tracking: we use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience and analyse site performance. You may disable cookies in your browser settings.
  • Third-party data: if you interact with our content on platforms such as TikTok, Facebook, or Instagram, those platforms may collect data per their own privacy policies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our platforms and services

  • Analyse usage trends to improve user experience

  • Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorised access

When you click an affiliate link, you may be directed to a third-party site. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We may earn a commission on qualifying purchases made through our affiliate links. No personal data is shared with retailers beyond what is standard for the referral transaction.

5. TikTok Integration

We operate TikTok accounts and use the TikTok for Developers platform in connection with our Dreaming Anime and Prisca Dezigns brands. Through this integration:

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@priscadezigns.org.
