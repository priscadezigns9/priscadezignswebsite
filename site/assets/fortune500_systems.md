## 1. 🎮 Gaming Gear — Razer · Logitech · SteelSeries

### 1. Content Cadence
- **Razer:** Daily social posts across Instagram, X, TikTok, YouTube. Blog/news section updated 3–5×/week with product launches, esports partnerships, and "For Gamers By Gamers" editorial. YouTube publishes 2–4 videos/week (product reveals, reviews, tutorials).
- **Logitech/G:** Weekly blog posts on setup guides, gaming tips, product comparisons. Heavy YouTube presence (1–2 videos/week). Active Discord community feeds UGC.
- **SteelSeries:** 2–3 blog posts/week. Strong focus on esports news and pro-player gear features.

### 2. SEO Strategy
- **Keyword targets:** "best gaming mouse 2024," "gaming headset review," "mechanical keyboard under $100," "DPI settings explained," brand + "vs" comparisons (Razer DeathAdder vs Logitech G502).
- **Content structure:** Pillar pages for product categories (mice, keyboards, headsets) with supporting "best of" comparison articles. Heavy use of schema markup (Product, Review, FAQ).
- **Technical SEO:** Faceted navigation optimized for category pages. Hreflang for 30+ country storefronts.

### 3. Link-Earning Tactics
- **Esports sponsorships** generate massive press coverage and brand mentions (Team Razer, LCS, IEM).
- **Product review seeding** to IGN, PCMag, Tom's Guide, Digital Trends — these sites link back with "where to buy" CTAs.
- **In-game collaborations** (Fortnite, VALORANT) create news cycles that earn editorial links from gaming press.
- **Open-source/developer tools** (Razer Chroma SDK) earn links from developer communities.

### 4. Social Content System
- **Dominant formats:** Short-form setup tours (TikTok/Reels), RGB aesthetic clips, pro-player endorsement content, unboxing videos on YouTube.
- **UGC engine:** Hashtag campaigns (#RazerSetup, #LogitechG) aggregate user setups — reposted constantly.
- **Platform split:** TikTok for awareness (viral clips), YouTube for consideration (long reviews), Instagram for aesthetics, X/Twitter for esports commentary.

### 5. Tactic to Implement TODAY
> **"Best [Product] Under $X" comparison blog post targeting mid-funnel buyers.** Example: "Best Gaming Mice Under $50 in 2026." Structure it with a comparison table, affiliate links, schema markup. Wirecutter-style format. This type of post earns backlinks from Reddit, forums, and YouTube description links — and converts at 8–12%.
