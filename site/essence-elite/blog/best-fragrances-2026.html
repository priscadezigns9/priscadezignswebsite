The Best Fragrances to Wear in 2026

Published May 12, 2026 · By the Essence Elite Editorial Team

From niche houses making their boldest statements to beloved designer classics finally getting their due — these are the fragrances our editors wore obsessively in 2026, across every family, every season, and every mood.

2026 has been a remarkable year for fragrance.
