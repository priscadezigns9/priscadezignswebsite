Luxury Reimagined: The 2026 Fall and Winter Collection Debut

The maison has officially unveiled its Fall and Winter 2026 collection, and the focus is on archival textures and architectural silhouettes. In a cinematic campaign that has already gone viral, the collection showcases a return to structured luxury, moving away from the fluid minimalism of early 2026.

Handbags are taking center stage this season with oversized hardware and artisanal leather treatments. This debut sets the tone for high-end fashion as we move into the latter half of the year.
