Best Noise-Cancelling Headphones for Travel

📅 2026 📖 5 min read ⭐ The Escapist

The difference between a draining long-haul flight and a genuinely restful one often comes down to one thing: noise-cancelling headphones. Removing the constant drone of jet engines, crying children, and ambient cabin noise transforms the experience entirely — whether you're sleeping, working or watching content. Here are the best options for travellers in 2026.

What Makes Headphones Good for Travel?

Travel-specific requirements go beyond audio quality. You need strong Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) tuned for low-frequency drone (the primary aircraft noise frequency), good battery life (ideally 30-plus hours), a comfortable fit for extended wear, a compact folding design that fits in your personal item, and ideally multipoint connection to pair with both your phone and laptop simultaneously.

Sony WH-1000XM6

Sony's flagship continues to set the benchmark for consumer ANC headphones. The latest generation improves on the already excellent noise cancellation of the XM5, with better performance in the mid-frequency range that covers voices and ambient chatter. The 30-hour battery, Speak-to-Chat automatic pause feature, and superb carrying case make it the most complete travel headphone package available.

Bose QuietComfort Ultra

The Bose QuietComfort line has always been the gold standard for comfort, and the Ultra builds on that legacy with spatial audio and Bose's CustomTune technology, which personalises the ANC and sound profile to your ear shape. The over-ear cushions are exceptionally soft for long flights.

Apple AirPods Max (2nd Gen)

For Apple ecosystem users, the second generation AirPods Max deliver outstanding ANC, seamless device switching, and the best microphone array in any over-ear headphone. They're a strong choice if you're already deep in Apple's ecosystem and value audio quality highly.

Budget Option: Anker Soundcore Q45

If you need solid ANC without flagship pricing, the Anker Soundcore Q45 consistently tops budget ANC lists. For under $80, you get genuinely effective noise cancellation, 50-hour battery life, and a comfortable over-ear design. Audio quality won't rival Sony or Bose, but for blocking out flight noise and watching content, it performs well beyond its price point.

In-Ear Alternative: Sony WF-1000XM5

Some travellers prefer the compactness of in-ear ANC earbuds. The Sony WF-1000XM5 are the benchmark here: exceptional ANC for in-ear form factor, 8 hours per charge with additional hours from the case, and outstanding call quality. They pack to almost nothing — ideal if over-ear headphones feel too bulky.

Practical Travel Tips

Always bring a 3.5mm adapter — some aircraft entertainment systems still use wired connections, and even the best ANC headphones benefit from being wired to reduce battery drain on long flights. Keep the carrying case accessible at the top of your bag during boarding. Use flight mode and connect via Bluetooth only to reduce device interference.

Noise-cancelling headphones are one of the most impactful travel gear upgrades you can make. Once you've crossed an ocean in effective silence, travelling without them feels like a significant downgrade.
