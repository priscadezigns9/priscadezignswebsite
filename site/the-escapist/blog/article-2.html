Best Noise-Cancelling Headphones for Travel in 2026

Travel headphones are one of the best investments a frequent traveller can make. The right pair transforms a long-haul flight from an ordeal into genuine downtime. Here's what to prioritise.

Active Noise Cancellation (ANC)

ANC uses microphones to sample ambient noise and generate inverse sound waves that cancel it out. On a plane, this eliminates the constant engine drone that causes fatigue. The difference between good and great ANC is substantial — test before buying if possible.

Battery Life

Minimum 30 hours for travel use. You want to survive a long-haul flight, layover, and onward journey without charging. Quick-charge (10 minutes = 3+ hours) is a useful backup feature.

Comfort for Long Sessions

Memory foam ear cushions, adjustable headbands, and lightweight frames matter on a 12-hour flight more than they do in product photos. Clamping pressure (how hard the cups press against your head) is the most common complaint — look for reviews that specifically address this.

Transparency Mode

Lets you hear your environment without removing the headphones. Essential for airports — hearing announcements without pulling your headphones off repeatedly.

Foldability

Travel headphones must fold flat and come with a hard case. Anything else is a liability.

Key Specs to Compare

  • ANC rating (dB of attenuation)
  • Battery life (ANC on)
  • Weight
  • Case size
  • Multipoint connection (connect to two devices simultaneously)
