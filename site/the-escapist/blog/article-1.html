The Ultimate Travel Packing List for 2026

Overpacking is the most common travel mistake. The perfect packing list is ruthlessly edited — everything earns its weight. Here's the list frequent travellers actually use.

Documents and Security

  • Passport (check expiry — many countries require 6 months validity beyond travel dates)
  • RFID-blocking passport holder
  • Travel insurance documents
  • Copies of key documents stored in cloud

Clothing: The 5-4-3-2-1 Rule

For a week-long trip: 5 tops, 4 bottoms, 3 pairs of shoes (wear the heaviest), 2 bags, 1 jacket. Everything must mix and match. Dark colours hide travel wear better than light colours.

Tech Essentials

  • Universal travel adapter
  • 20,000mAh power bank
  • Noise-cancelling headphones
  • Cable organiser pouch
  • Laptop (if needed) in carry-on only

Toiletries

Use solid toiletries (shampoo bars, solid moisturisers) — they don't count toward liquid limits. Decant liquids into 30ml bottles only. A TSA-approved clear bag saves time at security.

Packing Method

Compression packing cubes change everything. Roll clothes tightly, compress, and suddenly a week's worth of clothes fits in a carry-on. No checked luggage = no fees, no waiting, no lost bags.

What to Leave Behind

  • More than one pair of jeans (heavy, slow to dry)
  • Full-size toiletries
  • "Just in case" items you've never needed before
  • Anything that requires ironing
