Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best travel destinations in 2026?

Top emerging destinations: Tbilisi (Georgia), Medellín (Colombia), Plovdiv (Bulgaria), Luang Prabang (Laos), and Oaxaca (Mexico). Established favorites: Kyoto, Lisbon, and Marrakech continue to deliver.

How do I pack light for a 2-week trip?

The carry-on formula: 5 tops, 3 bottoms, 2 shoes, 1 layer, 7 days of undergarments (wash mid-trip). Use packing cubes. Wear your heaviest items on travel days.

What travel insurance do I need?

At minimum: medical evacuation ($500K+), trip cancellation, and baggage loss coverage. For adventure travel, add emergency sports coverage. Avoid policies that exclude 'pre-existing conditions' broadly.

Is travel hacking with credit card points still worth it?

Yes — Chase Sapphire Reserve and Amex Platinum still offer 2–5x points on travel with transferable miles. A single business class redemption can be worth $3,000–8,000 in value.

What are the most underrated carry-on travel accessories?

A compression packing cube set, a 65W GaN charger (replaces 4 adapters), a packable rain jacket, silk sleep mask, and a reusable water bottle with a filter. These 5 change every trip.

How far in advance should I book flights?

Domestic: 1–3 months. International: 2–6 months. Peak season (December, July–August): 6–12 months. Use Google Flights' price tracking — Tuesday morning is statistically the cheapest booking time.
