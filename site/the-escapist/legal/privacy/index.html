The Escapist is a curated affiliate content website operated under the priscadezigns.org domain, part of the Prisca Dezigns network of brand sites.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit The Escapist, why we collect it, and how we use it. By using this site, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling any personal information we collect with care and respect.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Contact forms: If you submit a contact or inquiry form, we collect your name and email address, along with any message content you provide.
  • Newsletter sign-ups: If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your name and email address.
  • Interactive tools: Responses you provide to on-site quizzes or style tools are used solely to generate recommendations within your current session. We do not store these responses.

Information collected automatically:

  • Usage data: We may collect standard web analytics data including page views, time on page, browser type, device type, and referring URL. This is aggregated and used to improve our site.
  • IP address: Your IP address may be logged automatically by our hosting infrastructure as part of standard server operations.
Important: We do not collect payment information of any kind. All purchases are completed directly on Amazon.com or other third-party platforms, which handle all payment processing under their own policies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To send newsletters and product recommendations that you have opted into receiving.
  • To respond to inquiries submitted through our contact forms.
  • To analyse site usage patterns and improve the quality and relevance of our curated content.
  • To maintain the security and proper functioning of our site.

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

4. Amazon Associates & Third Parties

The Escapist participates in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. When you click a product link on The Escapist and subsequently make a purchase on Amazon.com, we may earn a small commission. This commission is paid by Amazon and comes at no additional cost to you.

Amazon operates its own platform with its own privacy policy and data practices. When you click through to Amazon, you leave our site and Amazon's data practices apply. We encourage you to review Amazon's Privacy Notice for full details.

We may also use the following third-party services: analytics providers (for aggregated, anonymised usage statistics) and FormSubmit (for contact form processing). These services operate under their own privacy policies.

5. Cookies

Our site uses a minimal number of cookies to support basic functionality and improve your browsing experience:

We do not use tracking cookies, behavioural advertising cookies, or third-party advertising network cookies. We do not retarget you with advertisements based on your activity on our site.

You can manage or disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of certain interactive features.
