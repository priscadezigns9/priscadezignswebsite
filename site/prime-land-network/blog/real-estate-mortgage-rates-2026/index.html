Timing the Market vs. Time in the Market

The most enduring principle in real estate remains: time in the market beats timing the market for long-term wealth creation. The question "is now a good time to buy?" almost always resolves the same way: if your personal finances are ready, your strategy is clear, and the specific property makes sense on its own fundamentals — then 2026 is as good a time as any. Waiting for the "perfect" moment is a strategy that has disappointed more potential investors than almost any other.

Looking Ahead to the Second Half of 2026

The Seattle housing market, covered extensively this week by local experts, shows regional variations that remind investors of real estate's fundamentally local nature.
