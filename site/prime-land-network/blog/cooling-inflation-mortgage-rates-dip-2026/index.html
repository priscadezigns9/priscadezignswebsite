Cooling Inflation: Why 2026 is Finally the Year for New Home Buyers

After years of high interest rates and market volatility, there is finally a light at the end of the tunnel for prospective homeowners. This week in May 2026, major financial outlets and real estate experts are reporting a significant **cooling of inflation**, leading to the first major dip in mortgage rates in years. Zillow and Realtor.com are predicting that 30-year fixed mortgage rates will settle into the low-to-mid 6% range by the end of 2026. Buyers are encouraged to act now to beat the rush, as the drop in rates will likely lead to increased competition and potentially higher home prices in the long run. If you've been waiting on the sidelines of the real estate market, 2026 is the year to prepare.
