02

Problem & Solution

The Problem

Food content is one of the most consumed categories on the internet. Billions of recipe searches are made every month, and the number of people willing to cook diverse, multicultural dishes at home has never been higher. Yet a critical gap exists in the journey from "I want to cook this" to "I am cooking this."

The research problem: 68% of home cooks report abandoning a recipe they wanted to make because they couldn't find one or more specialist ingredients at their usual store. (Source: Food Marketing Institute, 2023)

Existing recipe apps solve the discovery problem. They surface beautiful content, offer vast catalogues, and create genuine excitement about cooking. But when users close the app and head to the store, they're on their own. They may:

  • Not know what "dashi" is called on the shelf at their local store
  • Not know which nearby store even stocks sumac or lemongrass
  • Waste time visiting multiple stores only to fail to complete the recipe
  • Give up and cook something familiar instead — losing the app's engagement entirely

For immigrant communities, food enthusiasts, and curious cooks, this gap is deeply frustrating. Inspiration without execution is just content — and the best recipe apps in the world are currently just that.

The Solution

Callaloo is the first recipe platform to natively close the gap between inspiration and execution. We build an ingredient-finding layer on top of recipe discovery that does the following:

  • Ingredient normalisation: Our AI maps every ingredient in a recipe to its real-world product name, including regional synonyms, brand equivalents, and common substitutions
  • Local store matching: We query live inventory data from nearby stores to find exactly where each ingredient is available right now
  • Completeness scoring: Stores are ranked by how many of a recipe's ingredients they carry — so users can find the single shop with everything they need
  • One-tap cart integration: Ingredients are added directly to grocery delivery apps or a shareable shopping list
Value Proposition

Callaloo doesn't just show you what to cook — it makes cooking it actually possible, regardless of where you live, what cuisine you love, or how unfamiliar the ingredients are.

Product Overview

Core Features

  • Recipe Search Engine: 50,000+ recipes spanning every major world cuisine. Semantic search handles typos, alternate names, and related queries. New recipes added daily via cook community submissions and editorial curation.
  • AI Ingredient Engine: Parses recipe ingredient lists, normalises ingredient names to real-world product labels, identifies regional synonyms (e.g. cilantro/coriander), and flags alternatives for hard-to-find items.
  • Real-Time Store Matching: Live queries to nearby grocery store inventory APIs. Results ranked by completeness, distance, and price. Integrations with Tesco, Sainsbury's, Whole Foods, Kroger, and specialty grocers.
  • Serving Size Scaling: Ingredient quantities scale automatically based on number of servings. Pack size calculations prevent overbuying.
  • One-Tap Cart: Add all ingredients directly to Instacart, Deliveroo Groceries, Ocado, or other connected delivery services. Or save as a shareable list.
  • Cook Profiles: Community cooks share their recipes, build followings, and earn from their content. Verified profiles for professional chefs.
  • Save & Collections: Users save favourite recipes and organise them into personal collections (e.g. "Date Night", "Quick Weekdays").
  • Dietary Filters: Automatic vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, and allergen detection across the recipe database.

Technology Stack

NLP Ingredient Parser: Custom-trained transformer model for ingredient extraction and normalisation. Store Integration Layer: Unified API wrapper normalising responses from 15+ grocery APIs. Location Engine: Geospatial queries optimised for sub-200ms response times. Mobile: React Native (iOS + Android). Web: Next.js. Backend: Node.js + PostgreSQL + Redis.

Roadmap

  • Q3 2025 — Beta Launch: Web app, 2 markets (UK + US), 15,000 recipes, 5 grocery integrations
  • Q4 2025 — Mobile Apps: iOS and Android apps, push notifications, saved lists
  • Q1 2026 — Cook Community: Cook profiles, recipe submissions, community monetisation
  • Q2 2026 — Store Partnerships: Direct commercial relationships with grocery chains, featured product slots
  • Q4 2026 — Market Expansion: Australia, Canada, Germany (multilingual support)
  • 2027 — Callaloo Pro: AI meal planning, budget optimisation, nutrition tracking, bulk cook prep tools

Revenue Model

Callaloo is designed for multiple, complementary revenue streams that grow as the platform scales. The model is built to be free-first, with monetisation layered in as value is demonstrated.

🆓

Freemium Subscription CORE

Free tier: 10 recipe searches/month, basic store matching, saved lists. Premium: $6.99/month or $49.99/year — unlimited searches, real-time inventory, full cart integration, dietary filters, meal planning. Target: 8–12% free-to-paid conversion.

🏪

Store Partnership Revenue HIGH VALUE

Grocery chains pay for premium placement in store rankings, "ingredient of the week" features, and exclusive product integrations. Modelled on how Google Maps allows businesses to advertise. Projected: $50–500K/year per major partner.

🔗

Affiliate Ingredient Links SCALABLE

When users click through to buy ingredients from connected grocery platforms, Callaloo earns an affiliate commission (typically 1–4% of basket value). At scale with 500K users making 4 purchases/month averaging $60, this represents ~$7.2M/year.

👨‍🍳

Premium Cook Profiles COMMUNITY

Cooks pay $19.99/month for verified profiles, enhanced analytics, promotional placement, brand partnership facilitation, and tip jar access. Target: 5,000 premium cook profiles by Year 2 = $1.2M ARR.

📣

Sponsored Recipes & Content BRAND

Food brands sponsor recipes featuring their products. Integration is native and transparent — users see "Sponsored by [Brand]" labels. CPM-based pricing. High demand from specialty food, spice, and condiment brands. $5,000–50,000 per campaign.

📊

Grocery Insights API B2B

Sell anonymised, aggregated data to grocery chains: what people are searching to cook in specific areas, which ingredients are hardest to find, demand signals by neighbourhood. Positioned as a premium B2B data product in Year 2+.

Go-to-Market Strategy

Our GTM strategy is built around community, content, and commerce — with each phase unlocking the next layer of growth and revenue.

1

Phase 1 — Waitlist & Community Building

Now → Q3 2025

Build pre-launch demand through early access waitlist, cook community recruitment, and content marketing. Goal: 15,000 waitlist signups, 500 contributing cooks. Channels: social media (food communities on TikTok, Instagram, Reddit), food blogger partnerships, SEO-optimised recipe content.

2

Phase 2 — Beta Launch (UK + US)

Q3 2025 → Q4 2025

Soft launch to waitlist members in the UK and US. Focus on product-market fit, retention metrics, and NPS. Iterate weekly. Begin influencer marketing partnerships with food content creators. Target: 10,000 MAU, 8% premium conversion, 4.2+ App Store rating.

3

Phase 3 — Store Partnerships & Revenue

Q1 2026 → Q3 2026

Formalise commercial relationships with 2–3 major grocery chains per market. Launch sponsored recipe product. Activate affiliate links across all store connections. Media push: food press, tech press, national coverage. Target: 100,000 MAU, first profitable quarter.

4

Phase 4 — Global Expansion

Q4 2026 → 2027

Expand to Australia, Canada, and one non-English market (Germany or UAE). Multilingual support, localised recipe databases, regional store integrations. Series A fundraise to fuel international growth. Target: 500,000 MAU globally, $50M+ ARR run-rate.

Growth Channels

  • Content SEO: Recipe pages optimised for search — "how to make [dish]" queries drive top-of-funnel traffic at near-zero CAC
  • Food Creator Partnerships: Partner with food influencers to feature "find ingredients on Callaloo" CTAs in their recipe content
  • Community-Led Growth: Every cook who joins Callaloo brings their audience with them — organic distribution through cook promotion
  • App Store Optimisation: Targeting high-intent search terms: "recipe ingredient finder", "where to buy [ingredient]", "cook [cuisine] at home"
  • Word of Mouth: Shareable ingredient lists and recipe cards create organic viral loops every time someone shares a recipe with friends
