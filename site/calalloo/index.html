How It Works

Three steps. That's it.

No manual logging. No googling ingredients. Just snap and go.

📷
1

Snap your meal

Take a photo of anything you ate — a home-cooked plate, street food, a restaurant dish. Our AI identifies it instantly, even if it's a Caribbean dish most apps have never seen.

🧮
2

Get real nutrition data

Calories, protein, carbs, fat — built on Caribbean food data, not US databases that don't know what doubles or bake & shark actually contain.

📍
3

Find every ingredient near you

Every dish shows the full ingredient list and exactly which stores near you carry them — whether you're in Trinidad, London, Toronto, or New York.

Built Different

Three things no other food app can do.

📷

AI that knows Caribbean food

Scan a plate of pelau, bake & shark, or oil down and get accurate results — not "unknown dish" or wrong calories from a US database that's never heard of shadow beni.

📍

Ingredients, located

Every recipe and every scan links each ingredient to stores near you — whether you're back home or in the diaspora looking for green seasoning in Toronto.

👩‍🍳

Built by the community

Home cooks upload their own recipes, tag local ingredients, and build the database together. The more people use it, the smarter it gets for everyone.

NEW — AI Scanner
