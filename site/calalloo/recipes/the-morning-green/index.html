The Morning Green

The ultimate Caribbean green smoothie. Packed with iron-rich spinach, local cucumber, and coconut water for natural electrolyte replacement.

Verified Sourcing

Greens sourced from NAMDEVCO Macoya. Coconut water provided by Local Roadside Artisans.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Fresh Spinach or Chadon BeniNAMDEVCO
  • 1 Local Cucumber (peeled)Market Fresh
  • 1 Green ApplePantry
  • 500ml Fresh Coconut WaterRoadside Sourced
  • 1 tsp Spirulina (optional)Supplement
  • Squeeze of LemonLocal

Instructions

  1. Roughly chop the cucumber and green apple.
  2. Add the coconut water to your blender base.
  3. Add the greens, cucumber, and apple.
  4. Blend on high until completely smooth.
  5. Add lemon juice and spirulina, pulse for 5 seconds.
  6. Pour into a chilled glass and enjoy immediately for maximum nutrient absorption.
