Sahina.

A crispy, savory spinach pastry that delivers iron-rich micronutrients in a light, crunchy vessel. A staple snack with deep nutritional merit.

Verified Sourcing Nodes

Fresh Spinach: NAMDEVCO Macoya. Spices: Local Wholesaler. Pastry Flour: Premium Import Node.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups Fresh Spinach (Chopped)Iron & Magnesium
  • 2 cups All-Purpose FlourCarb Base
  • 4 tbsp Coconut OilLipid Profile
  • 1 tsp TurmericAnti-Inflammatory
  • Minced Garlic & GeeraAromatic Spices
  • Salt & Hot PepperMineral & Heat

Laboratory Instructions

  1. Mix flour with salt and turmeric. Gradually add water and coconut oil to form a soft dough.
  2. Divide dough into small balls and rest for 15 minutes.
  3. Roll each ball thin and fry on a tawa (griddle) until light and puffed.
  4. Heat fresh spinach with garlic, geera, and hot pepper in a separate pan until wilted.
  5. Stuff each pastry with the spinach mixture.
  6. Roll up tightly and fry until golden and crispy.
  7. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
