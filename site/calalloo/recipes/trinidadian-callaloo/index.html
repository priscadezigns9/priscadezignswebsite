Trinidadian Callaloo.

The crown jewel of Caribbean cuisine. A velvety, nutrient-dense blend of dasheen bush, okra, and coconut milk. This is performance nutrition in its most traditional form.

Verified Sourcing Nodes

Dasheen Bush: NAMDEVCO Macoya. Okra: Northern Wholesale Market. Coconut Milk: Roadside Artisanal Extraction.

Ingredients

  • 1 Bundle Dasheen Bush (Chopped)Primary Source
  • 12 Fresh Okras (Sliced)Local Node
  • 1/4 lb Pumpkin (Cubed)High-Fiber
  • 2 cups Fresh Coconut MilkActive Fat
  • 1 Onion & 4 Cloves GarlicAromatics
  • 3 Pimento PeppersZest
  • Shadow Beni & ThymeEnzymatic Herbs
  • Salt Meat or Crab (Optional)Protein Boost

Laboratory Instructions

  1. Wash dasheen bush leaves and stems thoroughly. Remove any tough outer skins from the stems and chop finely.
  2. Place dasheen bush, okra, pumpkin, onion, garlic, pimento, and herbs into a large heavy-bottomed pot.
  3. Pour in the fresh coconut milk and enough water to just cover the ingredients.
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 45-60 minutes until everything is completely tender.
  5. Using a traditional "swizzle stick" or a hand blender, pulse until the mixture reaches a smooth, velvety consistency.
  6. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper. Serve as a side or a main nutrient hub.
