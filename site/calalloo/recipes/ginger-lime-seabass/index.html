Ginger Lime Seabass

Light, zesty, and high in lean protein. This recipe uses the freshest local catch paired with punchy Caribbean ginger and lime for a quick, performance-focused dinner.

Verified Sourcing

Fish sourced from the San Fernando Fish Market (The Morning Catch). Produce sourced via Crafted Crates.

Ingredients

  • 2 Fresh Seabass Fillets (6oz each)San Fernando Market
  • 2 tbsp Freshly Grated GingerCrafted Crates
  • 3 Fresh Caribbean LimesLocal Sourced
  • 1 tbsp Coconut OilArtisanal
  • 1 tsp Sea Salt & Cracked Black PepperPantry
  • Handful of Fresh CilantroNAMDEVCO

Instructions

  1. Pat the seabass fillets dry with a paper towel. Season both sides with sea salt and black pepper.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the grated ginger with the juice of 2 limes and a drizzle of coconut oil.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat with the remaining coconut oil.
  4. Place fish in the pan, skin-side down. Sear for 3-4 minutes until skin is crispy.
  5. Flip the fish and pour the ginger-lime mixture over the fillets. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  6. Serve immediately garnished with fresh cilantro and extra lime wedges.
