How It Works

From kitchen to global platform
in four steps

01

Apply to Join

Fill in a short application telling us about your cooking style, what cuisines you specialise in, and what you're excited to share. We review every application personally.

02

Build Your Profile

Set up your cook profile — your story, your specialty cuisines, your social links. This becomes your home on Calalloo, visible to every user who loves your food.

03

Submit Your Recipes

Upload recipes in our simple format — ingredients, method, serving sizes, photos. Our AI automatically maps ingredients to stores near your audience. Zero extra work.

Watch your audience build, track your recipe performance, and earn from every ingredient sale your recipes drive. Your cooking, now working for you 24/7.
