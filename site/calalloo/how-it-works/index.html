Technology

What powers the engine

A combination of AI, real-time store data, and ingredient intelligence that no other recipe app offers.

🧠

Natural Language Ingredient Parsing

AI reads recipe text the way a human chef would — understanding context, quantities, preparation methods, and regional ingredient names to extract precisely what you need.

🔄

Ingredient Synonym Database

Over 40,000 ingredient aliases mapped. "Coriander" and "cilantro" are the same. "Cornflour" and "cornstarch" are the same. Our database knows this, globally.

📡

Real-Time Store Inventory API

Live connections to major supermarket chains and grocery platforms. Inventory checked at the moment you search — not yesterday's data.

📍

Hyperlocal Store Matching

Not just "near you" — we rank stores by how many of your specific ingredients they carry, saving you from splitting your shop across five locations.

⚖️

Serving Size Scaling

Cooking for 2 or 20? Ingredient quantities scale automatically, and we calculate the exact pack sizes you need so nothing goes to waste.

🛒

One-Tap Shopping Cart

Ingredients land directly in your grocery delivery app or as a shareable shopping list. No copy-pasting. No retyping. Just cook.

Behind the Scenes

What happens in milliseconds

When you search a recipe, here's what our engine does before you even look up.

🔍

Query Received

Your search is received and matched against our recipe database using semantic search — catching typos, alternate spellings, and related dishes.

📋

Ingredient Extraction

The AI reads the recipe's ingredient list and extracts every item, quantity, preparation note, and optional substitution.

🌍

Localisation & Synonyms

Each ingredient is checked against our global synonym database — matching to how it's labelled on store shelves in your region.

📡

Live Store Query

Real-time queries fire to nearby stores' inventory APIs, checking stock availability for each ingredient.

🏆

Store Ranking

Stores are scored by completeness (how many items they carry), distance, price, and user ratings — then ranked for you.

✅

Results Delivered

You see the full ingredient list, which stores carry what, prices where available, and a one-tap path to your shopping cart.
