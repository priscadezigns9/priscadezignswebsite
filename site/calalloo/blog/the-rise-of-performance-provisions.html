The Rise of Performance Provisions.

Why elite island athletes are ditching processed supplements for heritage starches.

For decades, the "gold standard" of athletic fueling was dominated by imported gels, powders, and bars. But in 2026, a quiet revolution is taking place in the kitchens of the Caribbean's most elite performers. We’re calling it the rise of Performance Provisions.

Rooted in Power

Local staples like Dasheen, Cassava, and Breadfruit are no longer just "side dishes." They are being recognized as high-fidelity complex carbohydrates. Unlike processed fuels that cause insulin spikes and crashes, these heritage starches provide a steady, controlled release of energy—critical for endurance and cognitive focus.

Did you know? Breadfruit contains 4.9g of fiber per 100g, making it one of the most efficient slow-burn fuels available in nature. It’s the perfect pre-training meal for sustained output.

The Sourcing Advantage

The "Pinterest of Food" isn't just about aesthetics; it's about transparency. When you source a Heritage Grain Bowl ingredients from the NAMDEVCO markets, you aren't just getting fresh produce—you're getting nutrient density that hasn't been lost in a long-haul shipping container.

Freshness equals bioavailability. When an ingredient goes from a farm in Macoya to your plate in under 24 hours, your body absorbs the vitamins and minerals at a significantly higher rate than "fresh" imports that have been chilled for weeks.
