HALF-LIFE 3?

A 15-second teaser on Steam has just broken the internet. No title. No release date. Just the iconic lambda symbol and the date of the Summer Game Fest 2026 Premiere.

The teaser, which appeared for exactly three minutes on the Steam front page before being pulled, features a brief ambient soundscape that fans are already mapping to the research facilities of Aperture and Black Mesa. While Valve has said nothing officially, the metadata of the video file allegedly contains the project name "HL3_REVEAL_2026".

THE STEAM MACHINE REBIRTH

Alongside the lambda teaser, leaks from the supply chain suggest Valve is preparing a "Steam Machine 2"—a dedicated high-performance console designed specifically to run Source 2 at 4K/120fps. Sources say the hardware features a proprietary APU developed in collaboration with AMD, aiming to bring the full Steam deck experience to the living room with desktop-class power.
