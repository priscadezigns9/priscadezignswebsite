Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best anime for beginners?

Attack on Titan (action/thriller), Demon Slayer (accessible shonen), Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood (masterclass storytelling), and Your Lie in April (emotional/music). These four convert non-anime fans consistently.

What is the difference between sub and dub?

Sub (subtitled) preserves the original Japanese voice performances and timing — generally preferred by longtime fans. Dub (English dubbed) is more accessible for casual viewing and multitasking. Quality of modern dubs has improved significantly.

How do I find anime figures that are worth buying?

Prioritize: scale (1/7 or 1/8 are standard), sculptor reputation, and manufacturer (Good Smile, Kotobukiya, Aniplex). Check MFC (MyFigureCollection) for community ratings. Buy from Amazon or official retailers to avoid bootlegs.

What anime is coming out in 2026?

Highly anticipated 2026 releases include new seasons of One Piece, Bleach: Thousand-Year Blood War, Jujutsu Kaisen, and the final arc of My Hero Academia. Check Crunchyroll's seasonal calendar for exact dates.

What is isekai anime?

Isekai ('another world' in Japanese) is a genre where a protagonist is transported to a fantasy world. Popular examples: Re:Zero, Sword Art Online, That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime, and Overlord.

Are anime figures worth collecting?

Limited edition figures from Good Smile Company and Aniplex have appreciated 2–5x over 5 years. Popular characters from flagship series (Demon Slayer, JJK) are the safest collector plays. Store sealed in stable temperature.
