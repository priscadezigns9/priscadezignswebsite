Best Anime of 2026 So Far: Ranked by Genre

2026 has been a monster year for anime. With major continuations, stunning originals, and at least three serious GOAT contenders, here's every genre ranked with our top picks so far.

Action/Shonen

1. Bleach: Thousand-Year Blood War Finale — the arc concluded this year and delivered arguably the best animation in the franchise's history. Ichigo vs the Soul King is a standout moment in anime cinema. 2. Elden Ring: The Animation — the FromSoftware adaptation exceeded all expectations, nailing the lore-heavy storytelling fans demanded. The Tarnished's journey is hypnotic.

Romance/Slice of Life

1. Frieren: Beyond Journey's End Season 2 — continued to deliver the most emotionally intelligent storytelling in years. The magic exams arc deepened every character magnificently. 2. Dungeon Meshi (Delicious in Dungeon) — still running and still perfect. The food-adventure formula never gets old.

Horror/Psychological

1. Chainsaw Man Season 3 — MAPPA outdid themselves this year. The pacing issues of Season 2 are gone; this is Chainsaw Man firing on all cylinders. 2. Junji Ito Maniac Season 2 — another anthology of terror from the master. The Tomie arc adaptation is genuinely disturbing.

Isekai

1. Re:Zero Season 4 — Subaru's trials reached a new level of complexity and heartbreak. The new arc with Roswaal's history recontextualizes the entire series. 2. That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime: The Movie — theatrical quality on a TV budget. Rimuru's diplomatic arc translates beautifully to the big screen format.
