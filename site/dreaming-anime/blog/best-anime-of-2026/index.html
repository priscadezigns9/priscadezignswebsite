Best Anime of 2026 So Far: The Definitive Mid-Year Rankings

May 15, 2026  |  Dreaming Anime Editorial

We are nearly halfway through 2026 and the year has already delivered some all-time great episodes. From long-running shonen finally hitting their peaks to surprise originals stealing the season — here is our definitive mid-year ranking of the best anime airing and streaming in 2026.

S-Tier: Unmissable

1. Witch Hat Atelier Season 2

The long-awaited continuation finally arrived and it did not disappoint. The animation from Production I.G. is arguably the most visually refined of any TV anime ever made — every frame looks like a watercolour painting. The story has deepened significantly, exploring the ethics of magic in a world that bars most people from it. If you only watch one anime in 2026, make it this one.

2. Blue Lock Season 2

Blue Lock took the sports anime genre and rebuilt it from scratch around ego, psychological warfare, and the question of what makes a true striker. Season 2 has escalated every element — the football sequences are cinematic, the character writing is ruthless, and Isagi Yoichi has become one of the most compelling protagonists in recent memory.

A-Tier: Excellent

3. Dandadan Season 2

The chaotic supernatural romance-action hybrid from Science SARU continues to be one of the most stylistically adventurous anime on television. The direction is unhinged in the best way — quick cuts, expressive character acting, and a colour palette that feels genuinely alien.

4. Frieren: Beyond Journey's End (Finale Arc)

The first mage exam arc concluded this year and it remains the emotional standard for all fantasy anime. Frieren barely moves her face and yet conveys more feeling than most characters who scream through every episode.

B-Tier: Worth Your Time

5. One Piece (Egghead Island Arc Conclusion)

Egghead wrapped and it was one of the most action-packed arcs in the entire series. The animation quality fluctuated but the lore drops were extraordinary. For longtime fans the payoff has been worth the wait.

6. Spy x Family Season 3

Warm, funny, and consistently charming. Spy x Family does not try to reinvent itself — it just keeps delivering the comfort-watch energy that made it a global hit.
