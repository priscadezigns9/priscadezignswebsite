Best JJK Gifts Under $25

Not everyone wants to spend $50+ on a figure, and that's completely fine — there's a fantastic range of JJK merch under $25 in 2026. Our top picks in this category: the Gojo plush (30cm, licensed, incredibly soft), the Enamel Pin Set (8 character pins from the main cast), and the Itadori / Fushiguro reversible tote bag (100% cotton canvas, excellent print quality).

If you only buy one JJK piece in 2026, make it the Gojo Six Eyes Statue — nothing else in the lineup comes close to its display impact and collectibility. For budget shoppers, the 30cm Gojo Plush is exceptional value. The Yuji S3 figure is the best new release of the year if you want to commemorate this specific season.
