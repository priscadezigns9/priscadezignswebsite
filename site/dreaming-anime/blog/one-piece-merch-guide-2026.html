The Ultimate One Piece Merch Guide 2026

One Piece is the best-selling manga of all time, and in 2026 — with the live-action Netflix series Season 2 and the manga reaching its final saga — the merch scene has never been bigger. Here's how to navigate it without overspending.

Essential One Piece Merch Tiers

Tier 1 — Official Figures: Good Smile Company, Bandai, and Ichiban Kuji produce the highest-quality One Piece figures. The Portrait of Pirates series from MegaHouse is the gold standard — expect $100-$300 for premium pieces like Gear 5 Luffy. Tier 2 — Clothing: The Crunchyroll store and official Toei collabs produce quality tees and hoodies. For streetwear crossovers, check Hypland's One Piece collections. Tier 3 — Accessories: Den Den Mushi phone cases, Jolly Roger pendants, and Haki-themed jewelry are popular entry points.

Best 2026 One Piece Releases to Watch

The Gear 5 Luffy figure lineup from multiple manufacturers dropped this year and multiple editions have already sold out. The Wano Arc tribute art books are incredible for art fans. The Netflix collaboration merch (for Season 2) produced limited-edition prints and prop replicas worth collecting. The Monkey D. Luffy Kizuna Arts figure from Bandai is the sleeper pick of 2026 — beautiful and still available at retail.

Avoiding Bootlegs

One Piece is one of the most bootlegged properties in anime merch. Red flags: prices too low to believe, unclear manufacturer names, pixelated product images, no certificate of authenticity for premium figures. Safe sources: CDJapan, AmiAmi, Crunchyroll Store, authorized Amazon sellers with manufacturer branding.

Building a One Piece Collection on a Budget

Start with Ichiban Kuji prize figures — available on eBay and Mandarake for $20-$50, official quality, great variety. The Grandista series by Banpresto offers large-format figures at $30-$60. Manga volumes give you the story in 106 books — Viz Media editions are the licensed translation. For apparel, the official Toei x Hot Topic collabs balance quality and price well.
