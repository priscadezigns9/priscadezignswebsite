Top 10 Anime to Watch in Spring 2026

Spring 2026 has delivered one of the most stacked anime seasons in years. Here is everything worth watching right now.

Jujutsu Kaisen - Final Arc

The final arc continues to dominate globally. With stakes higher than ever, this is unmissable viewing. Merchandise from figures to limited-edition apparel is flying off shelves.

One Piece - Elbaf Arc

The Elbaf arc is everything fans have been waiting for. Animation quality from Toei has never looked better. If you have been sleeping on One Piece, 2026 is the year to catch up.

Dandadan Season 2

Science Saru returns with more supernatural chaos, hilarious characters, and absolutely stunning animation. Dandadan Season 2 picks up exactly where it left off.

Rising Manhwa Picks

Manhwa is having a massive 2026 moment. Solo Leveling Season 2, Omniscient Readers Viewpoint, and Tower of God are all getting major anime adaptations. The crossover between Korean webtoons and Japanese animation is reshaping the entire industry.

Whether you are watching for the story, the art, or the community, Spring 2026 is an incredible time to be an anime fan.
