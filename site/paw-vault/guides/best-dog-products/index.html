The Complete Dog Owner Essentials List

Day 1 Must-Haves

  • Collar + ID tag
  • Adjustable harness
  • Leash (standard + long line)
  • KONG Classic toy
  • Food + water bowls
  • Crate (appropriate size)
  • Dog bed/mat
  • High-value treats for training

First Month Additions

  • FURminator or appropriate brush
  • Enzyme odour cleaner
  • Tick/flea treatment
  • Poo bags (100-pack minimum)
  • Snuffle mat (mental enrichment)
  • Car seat cover
  • Reflective collar/lead for walks
The Enrichment Rule: A tired dog is a good dog. Physical exercise is essential, but mental enrichment (puzzle toys, sniff walks, training sessions) tires a dog out faster and reduces problem behaviours more effectively. The KONG, snuffle mats, and Licki Mats are worth far more than their price tags.
