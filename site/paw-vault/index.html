Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I bathe my dog?

Most dogs need bathing every 4–6 weeks. Dogs with oily coats (Basset Hound, Labrador) may need monthly baths; double-coated breeds (Husky, Malamute) every 6–8 weeks.

What is the best food for senior dogs?

Look for reduced calorie, higher fiber, and joint-supporting supplements (glucosamine, chondroitin). Brands like Hill's Science Diet, Royal Canin, and Purina Pro Plan offer dedicated senior lines.

How do I stop my dog from pulling on the leash?

Use a front-clip harness (not a collar), stop walking when tension appears, and reward loose-leash walking with treats. Consistency over 4–6 weeks builds the habit.

What vaccines does my dog need?

Core vaccines: Distemper, Parvovirus, Adenovirus (DHPP combo), and Rabies. Non-core (based on lifestyle): Bordetella, Leptospirosis, Lyme. Consult your vet annually.

How do I introduce a new pet to my home?

Keep new and existing pets separated for 1–2 weeks. Allow scent-swapping via bedding. First face-to-face meetings should be brief, positive, and supervised.

What are signs my pet is sick?

Watch for: loss of appetite, excessive thirst, lethargy, vomiting, diarrhea lasting >24 hours, difficulty breathing, or sudden behavioral changes. Any of these warrant a vet visit.
