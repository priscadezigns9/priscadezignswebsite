Platform Features

Everything your practice needs,
in one place.

Built specifically for healthcare providers who are tired of juggling a dozen different tools. One login, total clarity.

📅

Smart Scheduling

Online booking, automated appointment reminders, and intelligent calendar management that fills your schedule and cuts no-shows by up to 60%.

💳

Integrated Billing

Invoicing, insurance claims, and payment collection in one workflow. Get paid faster with automated payment reminders and one-click claim submission.

🖥️

Secure Client Portal

Give clients a branded, HIPAA-compliant portal to complete intake forms, view their records, communicate, and manage appointments — 24/7.

📹

Built-in Telehealth

HIPAA-compliant video sessions directly within the platform. No third-party apps. No extra logins. Just click and connect with your clients.

Clinical Documentation

Customizable intake forms, SOAP notes, treatment plans, and progress notes. Auto-populate from session data and sign with a single click.

Analytics & Reports

Real-time revenue tracking, appointment metrics, and client outcome data. Know exactly how your practice is performing at every moment.
