Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What are the best luxury watch brands to invest in?

Rolex, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet, and A. Lange and Sohne are consistently strong investment choices — all featured on The Watch List.

How do I buy an authentic luxury watch online?

The Watch List verifies every timepiece through serial number checks, brand authentication records, and physical inspection before listing.

What makes a watch a good investment?

Limited production, mechanical complexity, brand heritage, and original documentation all contribute to long-term value appreciation.

What is the difference between Swiss-made and Japanese watches?

Swiss movements are renowned for precision engineering and brand prestige; Japanese movements (Seiko, Grand Seiko) offer exceptional value with near-perfect accuracy.
