ELLE Decor's 2026 Rising Stars: The New Faces of Quiet Luxury

The world of interior design gathered in Tribeca this week to celebrate the **ELLE Decor 2026 Rising Stars** list. This year's honorees represent the evolution of the 'Quiet Luxury' trend, moving toward a more personal, artisan-driven approach to home decor. The 2026 aesthetic is characterized by 'lived-in luxury'—spaces that feel both high-end and deeply comfortable. Key elements include handcrafted textiles, natural materials like Travertine and Reclaimed Oak, and a neutral color palette enriched with 'earthy' textures. One of the standout trends mentioned by the Rising Stars is the integration of 'smart-invisible' tech, where functionality is hidden behind elegant design. Additionally, the 'IKEA PS 2026' collection was cited as a major influence, proving that high-end design principles are becoming more democratic. As we move further into 2026, the focus for luxury homes is on quality over quantity. It's about finding pieces that have a story and creating an environment that fosters wellness and tranquility. Quiet luxury is no longer just a trend; it's a way of life.
