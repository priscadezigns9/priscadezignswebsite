Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best smart home device to start with?

A smart speaker (Echo Dot or Google Nest Mini) is the lowest-friction entry point. Add a smart plug next — it makes any lamp or appliance voice-controlled instantly. These two cover 80% of smart home use cases.

Is a 4K monitor worth it for everyday use?

For photo/video editing and content creation: absolutely. For coding or general office work: the jump from 1440p to 4K is marginal. Prioritize refresh rate (144Hz+) and color accuracy over resolution for most use cases.

What is the difference between Wi-Fi 6 and Wi-Fi 6E?

Wi-Fi 6 operates on 2.4GHz and 5GHz bands. Wi-Fi 6E adds the 6GHz band — faster speeds, dramatically lower congestion, and better performance in dense environments (apartments, offices).

How long should a laptop last?

Budget laptops: 3–4 years. Mid-range: 5–6 years. Premium (MacBook Pro, ThinkPad X1): 7–10 years with a battery replacement at year 4–5. The biggest longevity factors: RAM (16GB minimum), SSD, and build quality.

What should I look for in wireless earbuds?

Prioritize: Active Noise Cancellation quality, codec support (aptX or AAC for quality), fit/seal consistency, battery life, and call microphone quality. The Sony WF-1000XM5 and Apple AirPods Pro 2 remain the benchmarks.

When is the best time to buy tech?

Black Friday/Cyber Monday (November) for the deepest discounts. Amazon Prime Day (July) for Amazon-ecosystem products. Post-launch (3–6 months after new model release) for previous generation deals — often 20–30% below launch price.
