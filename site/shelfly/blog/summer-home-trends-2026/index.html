The Summer 2026 Home Essentials Moment

Three categories are driving the summer 2026 home refresh wave:

  • Wallpapering: Statement walls, maximalist patterns, and textured wallcoverings are replacing the decade-long reign of painted accent walls. The boldness of 2026's home aesthetic is expressed most dramatically through wallpaper choices
  • Curtain installation: Thoughtful window dressing — floor-to-ceiling drapery, layered sheers, and custom curtain solutions — is one of the highest-impact, relatively accessible home transformations available in 2026
  • Custom upholstery: Rather than replacing furniture, 2026 homeowners are investing in recovering beloved pieces — a more sustainable, more personal approach to furniture renewal
