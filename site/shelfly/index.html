Style Guide

3 Ways to Style
Any Shelf

Steal these techniques from professional interior stylists and transform any shelf in your home.

01

Layer Height & Depth

Start with your tallest item at the back, then work forward in descending height. Mix a tall vase with mid-height books and a small candle at the front. The variation creates visual interest and draws the eye across the shelf naturally.

02

Mix Textures & Materials

Combine hard (ceramics, metal) with soft (baskets, linen boxes) and natural (wood, plants, dried stems). The contrast between textures prevents the shelf from feeling flat or one-dimensional — and it photographs beautifully too.

03

Apply the Colour Rule of Three

Choose three colours max — one neutral (white, cream, tan), one warm accent (terracotta, amber), and one fresh note (sage, eucalyptus green). Repeat each colour at least twice across the shelf to create cohesion without matching everything perfectly.
