Executive Summary

RIDDIM is a music discovery, identification, and creation platform built for the next generation of music lovers. Where Shazam identifies songs but cannot tell you the culture behind them, and Spotify recommends but does not teach — RIDDIM bridges the gap between hearing music, finding its roots, and creating your own.

🎧
HEAR IT
AI-powered music identification with deep cultural context — artist bio, genre history, where to stream, similar artists.
🔍
FIND IT
Curated discovery engine by mood, genre, era, and culture — reggae, soca, afrobeats, dancehall, hip hop, jazz, and beyond.
🎻
CREATE IT
Mobile beat-making tools, sample libraries, loop packs, and producer resources from beginner to pro.
Target Market

18–35 music enthusiasts, independent artists, producers, and culture-first listeners globally. Primary focus: Caribbean, African, and diaspora markets — 700M+ people underserved by existing platforms.

02

The Problem

  • Shazam identifies songs but gives no cultural context — you know the name, not the story.
  • Spotify recommends algorithmically but ignores niche and regional genres that define entire cultures.
  • Caribbean, African, and diaspora music is systematically underrepresented in mainstream discovery platforms.
  • Beginner producers have no affordable, mobile-first tool to go from idea to beat.
  • No single platform connects the listener-to-creator journey — you need 4 apps to do what RIDDIM does in one.
03

The Solution

🎧 HEAR IT — Identify & Understand

Tap to identify any song. Get an instant deep-dive: artist story, genre history, cultural origin, lyrics, where to stream or buy.

"Music DNA" — our signature feature traces a genre's roots. Example: how soca evolved from calypso, how dancehall split from reggae, how amapiano rose from Johannesburg's house scene.

🔍 FIND IT — Discover & Explore
  • Mood-based discovery: Hype, Chill, Romantic, Spiritual, Workout
  • Genre-first browsing: Reggae, Dancehall, Soca, Afrobeats, Amapiano, Hip Hop, R&B, Jazz, Gospel
  • Curated playlists by culture, region, and decade
  • Local artist spotlight — surface undiscovered artists by location
🎻 CREATE IT — Build & Sell
  • Mobile beat maker with drag-and-drop pads, tempo control, export to WAV/MP3
  • Sample library: 500+ royalty-free loops across genres
  • Producer marketplace: buy/sell beats, loops, and vocal samples
  • Tutorial library: how to produce soca, trap, afrobeats from scratch

The Vision

RIDDIM is not just an app. It is the first platform to give Caribbean and African music culture its rightful place in the global music tech ecosystem. Every genre has a story. Every artist has a journey. Every listener has a creator inside them. RIDDIM exists to unlock all three.
Long-Term Vision

Become the go-to music platform for the Global South — the Spotify + Shazam + GarageBand built by and for the cultures that created the rhythms the world dances to. From Kingston to Lagos, Port of Spain to London, Accra to Brooklyn — RIDDIM is where the culture lives.
