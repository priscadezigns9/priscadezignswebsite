Spatial Computing and AI: The Martech Trends Shaping the Future in 2026

The 'State of Martech 2026' report has highlighted two key technologies that are fundamentally reshaping the creative tech landscape: **Spatial Computing** and **AI-Driven Transformation**. At Karjov, we're seeing these trends converge to create entirely new kinds of brand experiences. Spatial computing—the integration of digital content into the physical world—has moved beyond a gimmick into a mainstream marketing tool. Brands are now using AR and VR to create 'Spatial Advertisements' that users can interact with in their own environments. Combined with AI that can generate these experiences in real-time based on user preferences, the potential for engagement is limitless. This 'Creative Tech' revolution is not just about the technology itself, but how it's used to tell stories. AI is being used to create high-fidelity footage and design details that were previously the domain of multi-million dollar studios. This 'democratization of high-fidelity' is allowing smaller, agile teams to compete on a global stage. As we look toward the second half of 2026, the focus for Karjov is on pushing the boundaries of what's possible at the intersection of the physical and digital worlds. The future is spatial, it's intelligent, and it's incredibly exciting.
