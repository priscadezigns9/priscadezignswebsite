How It Works

From recipe to table —
no guesswork.

Every step of the cooking journey, sorted — with ingredients and stores relevant to where you actually live.

1

Find a Recipe

Browse healthy local recipes — traditional dishes, modern takes, and everything in between. Filter by dietary needs, prep time, or cuisine.

2

See Every Ingredient

Each recipe lists exact quantities — "2 cups dasheen leaves", "1 tbsp chadon beni" — so you know precisely what to buy and how much.

3

Find It Near You

Every ingredient links to local stores that carry it — supermarkets, markets, specialty shops near your location. No more dead ends.

4

Watch How to Cook It

Step-by-step instructions with how-to videos for every recipe. See exactly how it's prepared before you start.

5

Track Your Calories

Full calorie breakdown per ingredient and per serving — using local food data, not generic foreign databases. Log your meals and track your nutrition goals.

6

Your Location, Your Recipes

The app adapts to wherever you are. Local stores, local produce, local dishes — the experience is built around your world, not someone else's.
