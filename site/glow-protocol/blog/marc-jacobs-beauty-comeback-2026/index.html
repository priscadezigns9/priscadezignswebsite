Beauty's Most Anticipated Comeback: Marc Jacobs Beauty is Back for 2026

Published: May 23, 2026

The beauty world has been waiting for this moment since the brand went quiet in 2021. This week, Coty Inc. officially announced the revival of Marc Jacobs Beauty, marking one of the most significant brand relaunches in recent industry history.

The Evolution of a Beauty Icon

The relaunch isn't just about bringing back fan favorites like the Highliner Gel Eye Crayon or the Enamored Lip Lacquer. The 2026 iteration of Marc Jacobs Beauty is expected to bridge the gap between high-fashion color cosmetics and cutting-edge skincare. Early reports suggest the brand will focus on "skin-first" formulas that incorporate advanced botanical extracts and sustainable packaging, aligning with the 2026 consumer's preference for efficacy and ethics.

What to Expect from the New Collection

Scheduled to debut in major retailers and online boutiques, the new collection will span eyes, complexion, and lips. However, it's the rumored expansion into dedicated skincare that has the industry talking. With Coty's R&D power behind the brand, we expect nothing less than transformative formulas that deliver the "runway glow" Marc Jacobs is known for.
