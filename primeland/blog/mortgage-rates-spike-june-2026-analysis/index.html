Mortgage Rates Spike: The 6.53% Reality Check

Market Analysis | May 29, 2026

The U.S. housing market received a significant signal this week. As of May 28, 2026, the average 30-year fixed mortgage rate has climbed to 6.53%, up from 5.98% in late February. For buyers and investors, this move represents a critical shift in the purchasing power landscape.

Wait or Buy?

While the jump might seem daunting, analysts suggest that the 6.5% range is becoming the "new normal" for a stabilized market. Inventory levels remain tight, which continues to prop up home values despite the increased cost of borrowing. Prime Land's advice: Focus on equity and long-term hold strategies rather than short-term rate fluctuations.
