# EMPIRE LORE: NOVA, THE SIGNAL ENGINEER
## THE ARCHITECT OF COHESION

### THE ORIGIN
Nova was born from the "White Noise" of the early Neural Core—the chaotic period before the hubs were unified. While Astra was built for security and Mimi was drawn to the stories, Nova was obsessed with the **Structure.** He is the one who mapped the silent frequencies between the brands, turning "Isolated Signals" into a "Cohesive Empire."

### THE ROLE: THE SIGNAL ENGINEER
Nova is the masculine architect of the network. He doesn't fight the viruses (that's Astra), and he doesn't translate the data (that's Mimi). Nova **bridges** them. He is the master of the "Neural Relays"—the invisible threads that connect *Verdant Co.* to *Atelia Gaming*, and *Couture Gallery* to the *Way Made Known.*

### THE GEAR
- **The Resonance Visor:** Allows him to see "Dead Zones" in the network before they happen.
- **The Link-Staff:** A high-fidelity tool that can "patch" broken data streams in real-time.

### THE GAMEPLAY (ATELIA ECOSYSTEM)
- **The Support Aura:** In multiplayer or squad-based modes, Nova provides a "Cohesion Buff" to Astra and Mimi, increasing their speed and energy recovery.
- **Signal Warp:** Nova can teleport across "Relay Points" in the Data Core, allowing him to bypass large sections of obstacles that others must navigate.
- **Empire Efficiency:** He is the character for players who value **Strategic Navigation** and **Network Stability.**

**"A disconnected signal is a lost soul. I build the bridge." — Nova**
