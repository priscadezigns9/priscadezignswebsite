Handheld PC vs. Next-Gen Console: The Death of the Living Room?

Is the living room box dead? With the ROG Ally 2 and Steam Deck 3 pushing 4K/60fps via mobile docking stations, the traditional console is facing an identity crisis. Why tether yourself to a single room when your entire library is in your backpack?

The Nintendo Factor
