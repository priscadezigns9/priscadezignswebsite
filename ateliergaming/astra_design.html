The final synthesis is manifest. Astra has returned to her **Slender Fox Anatomy**, but with the high-fidelity depth of a jointed bone, muscle, and skin manifest. Design Philosophy

To capture the "fox soul," we moved away from the stocky wolf frame and returned to the slender, agile proportions of the Highland Fox. The anatomy remains layered—bone and muscle provide the pivot points—but the surface is now a high-fidelity orange fur with a white-tipped tail and muzzle. The inclusion of individual fingers allows for clear human-like signaling (Thumbs Up, Peace, Wave) that is now unmistakable.
