Neural Unit // ASTRA-01

The Obsidian
Fox

Constructed in the deep-void laboratories of Atelier World, Astra is not merely a character—she is a sentient neural bridge. Her chassis is composed of softFur (matte obsidian-composite) that absorbs 99.9% of ambient light, while her internals pulse with raw data-flow.

Core Architecture

  • Species Sub-Void Neural Fox
  • Optics Crimson Specular (X-Ray)
  • Chassis Material Synthetic Reactive Skin
  • Tactical Apparel Nano-Crimson Tunic
  • Neural Sync Rate 99.8% Perfect

Master Abilities

Void Shift

Short-range neural displacement. Astra can bypass physical and digital firewalls by temporarily phasing into the sub-void.

Sync-Link

Direct mind-to-machine interface. Allows Astra to control the ship’s weapons and engines with zero latency, purely through thought.

Phantom Pulse

A wide-range sensory wave. Emits a localized crimson pulse that reveals hidden enemies or rare network loot caches.

Void Barrier

The ultimate defense. Astra creates a localized field of negative energy that deletes incoming projectiles from existence.

Design Philosophy

The design goal for Astra was "Tactile Futurism." Unlike Mario’s playful, rounded shapes, Astra is defined by sharp angles, high-contrast lighting, and a feeling of dangerous precision. She is built for speed and intelligence, representing the peak of Atelier's RPG evolution.
