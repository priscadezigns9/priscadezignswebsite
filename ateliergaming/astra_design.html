Manifest

Astra has evolved. No longer a silhouette in the void, she is now manifest in high-fidelity charcoal charcoal fur with neural-teal highlights. She is built for multidimensional performance—capable of running, leaping, and expressing human-like sentience through a complex rig of jointed limbs and facial optics.

Performance Architecture

  • Visual Status High-Contrast Charcoal
  • Animations 10+ Action States
  • Facial Rig Jointed Brows & Jaw
  • Limb Rig IK Jointed Humanoid

Design Philosophy

The goal for this reveal was "Visual Visibility." By lightening the base obsidian to a charcoal grey and adding neural-teal highlights to the hands and tail, every movement—from a wave to a run—is now unmistakable. She is the first Atelier character to possess a fully jointed facial rig for lip-sync and emotional expression.
