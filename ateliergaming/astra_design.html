The reveal has entered its final anatomical phase. Astra is no longer just a model—she is manifest as a **Layered Anatomical Entity**. Anatomical Specs

Design Philosophy

To move beyond "muscles on a fox," we implemented a true four-layer manifest. The **Skeleton** provides the pivot points, the **Muscles** provide the volume and recoil, the **Skin** provides the visibility charcoal base, and the **Fur** provides the organic cinematic finish. This allows her broad wolf muzzle and powerful limbs to move with unmistakable definition.
