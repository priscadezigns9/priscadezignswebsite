Questions & Evidence

Is there historical evidence for the life of Jesus?

Beyond the Gospels, first-century historians like Tacitus (Roman) and Flavius Josephus (Jewish) recorded His existence and execution under Pilate. Archaeology consistently affirms Biblical geography and figures.

Historical Context: The Tel Dan Stele and the Pilate Stone provide archaeological confirmation of the Davidic line and the Roman Prefect's authority.
"That which was from the beginning, which we have heard, which we have seen with our eyes..." — 1 John 1:1

Can science and faith coexist?

Analytical faith views the universe as a cohesive unit of truth. The complexity of DNA and the fine-tuning of the universe point to an Intelligent Designer. Science explores the 'how,' while faith reveals the 'Who.'

Scientific Signal: The Anthropic Principle suggests that the physical constants of the universe are precisely tuned for life.
"The heavens declare the glory of God; and the firmament sheweth his handywork." — Psalm 19:1

What is "Analytical Faith"?

It is the commitment to follow Truth wherever it leads. It is not blind belief, but belief based on the evidence of the Creator's fingerprint in history, science, and the human heart.

Mission Philosophy: Like Paul at the Areopagus, we proclaim the 'Unknown' through the lens of reason and revelation.
"Come now, and let us reason together, saith the Lord." — Isaiah 1:18
