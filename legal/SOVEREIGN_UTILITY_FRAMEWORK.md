## 3. The Sovereign Shield (Compliance Pillars)

The Sovereign Wallet is 100% non-custodial. Priscion never holds user funds, private keys, or personal assets. Users maintain absolute sovereignty over their property. This removes the project from "Money Transmitter" or "Custodian" regulatory categories.

### B. Utility-First Distribution
Tokens are distributed through network utility, earned participation ($ATLR), and commercial transactions. There is no public speculative "ICO" or investment-driven fundraising.

### C. The TWMK Mission Anchor
The **10% TWMK Tithe** is hardcoded into the ledger. This ensures that a portion of all network economic activity is routed to "The Way Made Known" endowment. This establishes a clear social and charitable purpose, separating the ecosystem's mission from purely speculative commercial interests.

### D. Digital Identity (DID)
Sovereign Handles ($name.pri) are issued as Utility NFTs. They provide a decentralized identity (DID) solution that complies with global privacy standards (GDPR) while enabling secure, peer-to-peer commerce.
