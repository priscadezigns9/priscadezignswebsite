Crunchyroll Anime Awards 2026: The Tokyo Winners Circle

Dreaming Anime | May 23, 2026

The 10th Annual Crunchyroll Anime Awards have concluded live in Tokyo, Japan, and the results have solidified 2026 as a year of both new legends and returning titans. The ceremony, held at the Grand Prince Hotel Takanawa, featured a star-studded lineup of presenters including The Weeknd, RZA, and Winston Duke.

The Big Winners

Anime of the Year: In a highly competitive category, DAN DA DAN Season 2 has taken home the top honor, edging out The Apothecary Diaries and Solo Leveling. The series' blend of supernatural chaos and high-fidelity animation from Science SARU proved unbeatable.

Best Animation: To no one's surprise, DAN DA DAN also secured Best Animation, marking a massive night for the studio. The visual ingenuity and fluid action sequences set a new industry benchmark for 2026.

Best Main Character: Maomao (The Apothecary Diaries Season 2) has officially been crowned Best Main Character. Her sharp wit and deductive brilliance have clearly resonated with the global fan base.

Best Action Anime: Solo Leveling Season 2 -Arise from the Shadow- continues its dominance in the action genre, securing the win. The series' choreography and high-stakes tension remain unparalleled.

Full Category Breakdown

  • Best Continuing Series: One Piece
  • Best New Series: GACHIAKUTA
  • Best Score: Hiroyuki Sawano (Solo Leveling Season 2)
  • Best Director: Fuga Yamashiro (DAN DA DAN)
  • Best Background Art: GACHIAKUTA

Dreaming Anime celebrates this historic night for the industry. The 2026 winners reflect a shift toward bold, creator-driven stories that push the boundaries of the medium.
