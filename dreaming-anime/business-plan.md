## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Dreaming Anime is a standalone anime lifestyle brand and digital commerce hub — the go-to destination for anime fans who want curated merch, editorial content, community, and culture, all in one place. Where Crunchyroll owns streaming, Dreaming Anime owns everything else: the merch, the blog, the community, the vibe.

**Market:** Global anime merch market = $22.89B by 2033 | 9.36% CAGR
**Model:** Affiliate commerce (Amazon) + blog ad revenue + newsletter + future owned merch
**Phase 1 Revenue Target:** $500–2,000/month passive (affiliate + AdSense) within 6 months
**Phase 2 Revenue Target:** $5,000–15,000/month (owned merch drops + sponsorships) within 18 months

---

## THE PROBLEM

1. **Amazon is overwhelming** — 10,000+ anime products, no curation, no trust
2. **Crunchyroll has no merch culture** — streaming-only, basic store, no community
3. **Anime fans have no lifestyle hub** — scattered across Reddit, Pinterest, YouTube, Amazon, Discord
4. **Female anime fans are ignored** — merch sites skew male; Shojo/romance/slice-of-life underrepresented
5. **Anime music is orphaned** — OPs/EDs/OSTs have no dedicated discovery home

---

## THE SOLUTION — DREAMING ANIME

A Crunchyroll-inspired web destination that serves the anime fan's full lifestyle:
- **Shop** — curated Amazon affiliate merch (best products by series + vibe)
- **Blog** — rankings, news, reviews, recommendations
- **Explore** — browse by genre, mood, and series
- **Game** — anime quiz (already live)
- **Music** — featured OPs/EDs/OSTs (YouTube embeds)
- **Newsletter** — weekly anime drop digest

## BUSINESS MODEL

### Revenue Stream 1 — Amazon Affiliate (Immediate)
- Commission: 3–8% per sale via Amazon Associates (tag: priscadezigns-20)
- 20+ products live from day one
- Target: 100 clicks/day → ~$300–800/month at avg order value $35
- Scale: SEO-optimised blog posts drive organic traffic to product pages

### Revenue Stream 2 — Display Advertising (Month 3+)
- Google AdSense on blog articles
- Target: 5,000 monthly page views → $150–400/month
- Scale to Mediavine at 50K monthly sessions ($1,500–5,000/month)

### Revenue Stream 3 — Newsletter Sponsorships (Month 6+)
- Weekly anime digest to subscribers
- Sponsorship rate: $50–200 per issue at 1,000+ subscribers
- Target sponsors: anime figure shops, gaming brands, other anime channels

### Revenue Stream 4 — Owned Merch Drops (Year 2)
- Print-on-demand: Dreaming Anime branded hoodies, posters, accessories
- Printful/Printify integration with Shopify
- Target margin: 35–50% per item

### Revenue Stream 5 — Digital Products (Year 2)
- "Ultimate Anime Gift Guide" PDF — $9.99
- Printable anime rankings/bingo cards — $4.99
- Anime watch trackers/journals

### Typography

| Role | Font | Weight | Notes |
|------|------|--------|-------|
| Display/Hero | Space Grotesk | 800–900 | All major headings |
| Subheadings | Space Grotesk | 600–700 | Section titles |
| Body | Inter | 400–500 | Paragraphs, descriptions |
| Labels/Tags | Space Grotesk | 700 | Category tags, badges |
| Accent/Italic | Inter Italic | 400 | Pull quotes |

**Font scale:**
- Hero H1: clamp(2.5rem, 7vw, 5rem)
- Section H2: clamp(1.8rem, 4vw, 2.8rem)
- Card H3: 1.1–1.3rem
- Body: 0.95–1rem
- Labels: 0.75rem uppercase, 1.5px letter-spacing

---

### Iconography & Visual Style
- **Icons:** Line icons (Heroicons/Lucide style) — never filled, never chunky
- **Borders:** Subtle orange glow borders (1px, rgba orange)
- **Shadows:** Orange-tinted drop shadows on cards
- **Imagery:** Anime artwork, character art — always dark-background compatible
- **Badges:** Pill-shaped, orange bg or orange border
- **Buttons:** Solid orange (primary), orange border ghost (secondary)

---

### Animation Principles (Prisca Dezigns standard)
- **Entrance:** fadeUp (0.6s, cubic-bezier(.22,1,.36,1)) on scroll
- **Hover cards:** translateY(-6px) + orange border glow
- **Hero:** Subtle float animation on logo/art
- **CTAs:** Scale 1.04 on hover, 0.2s ease
- **Glow pulse:** keyframe pulse on accent elements (subtle, 3s loop)
- NO flashy or distracting animations — purposeful only

---

### Voice & Tone
- **Personality:** Passionate fan, knowledgeable friend, hype but never cringe
- **Tone:** Energetic, warm, direct — talks like an anime fan, not a corporation
- **Headlines:** Bold, punchy — "The Best Demon Slayer Merch. Full Stop."
- **Body:** Conversational, confident — "We tested 40 products so you don't have to"
- **CTAs:** Action-forward — "Shop the Drop", "Read the List", "Play Now"
- **Never:** Corporate speak, jargon, overly formal language

---

### Social Media Brand Voice (Facebook — already managed)
- Post style: Hype + value. "This [product] is sending anime fans wild 🔥"
- Always include product link or blog link
- Image-first posts — product shots or anime art
- No excessive hashtags — max 5, relevant only
