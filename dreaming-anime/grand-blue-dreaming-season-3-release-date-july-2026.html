Grand Blue Dreaming Season 3: Official July 2026 Premiere Confirmed

The long-awaited return of the Peek-a-Boo club is finally official. Grand Blue Dreaming Season 3 has been confirmed for a July 2026 premiere, bringing the chaotic energy of the diving-but-mostly-drinking club back to the screen.

Animation studio J.C. Staff is returning to handle the production, with a new key visual released today featuring Iori and Kohei in their signature state of undress. Fans can expect the series to adapt the much-anticipated 'Island Trip' arc, which promises peak comedy and underwater visuals.
