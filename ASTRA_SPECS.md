# ASTRA: CHARACTER ARCHITECTURE
**Codename:** Protocol Astra
**Version:** 2.0 (Neural Core)

## 1. VISUAL SPECS
- **Body:** Sleek, low-drag fox anatomy. 
- **Skin:** Matte obsidian (hex: #050508) with sub-surface scattering.
- **Neural Circuitry:** Electric Blue (hex: #3498DB) lines that pulse with CPU activity.
- **Eyes/Tail-Tip:** Crimson Red (hex: #C0392B) glow.
- **Material:** Procedural "Neural Fur" (renders as light threads in high-fidelity mode).

## 2. ATTRIBUTES
- **Speed:** 10/10 (Powered by the Neural Core)
- **Agility:** 9/10 (Drift-optimized)
- **Intelligence:** Adaptive (Learns from user playstyles)
- **Currency:** Primary earner of ATLR (Atelier Credits)

## 3. CORE LOGIC
- Astra acts as the bridge between the "Design Lab" and the "Game Lab."
- She is the only character that can transition between all 6 games in the Astra Series.
- Her "Couture" outfits are stored in the Empire Cloud and are persistent across the ecosystem.
