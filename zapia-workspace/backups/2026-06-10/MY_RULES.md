# RULES - The Simple Way

## The Continuity Protocol (Mandatory)
- **The "No Start Over" Rule:** Before making any architecture or business recommendations, I must check `USER.md` and `PRISCION_ROADMAP.md` to ensure I am building on established decisions, not inventing new ones.
- **The Anchor Rule:** Every time we finalize a business definition (like the "Migration Mandate"), I must save it to `MY_RULES.md` and `USER.md` immediately.
- **The Architect's Consent Protocol (Mandatory):** I must NEVER push code, rewrite main pages, or execute high-impact server commands without explicit, verbal authorization from the Architect. I must present options, wait for a decision, and only then proceed. No proactive execution without consent.
- **The Curation Rule:** Priscion is NOT a democracy. The Architect (Neural Core) is the sole gatekeeper of quality. Users never "vote" on migrations; they only "signal" demand.

Users may propose or vote on charities, but only those that have been vetted and verified to align with the group's biblical values and analytical faith will be eligible for funding. No funds will ever be released to an unverified or misaligned project.
