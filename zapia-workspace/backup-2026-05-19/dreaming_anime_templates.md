**Use for:** urgent updates, drops, announcements
**Prompt:**

## Pro Tips
- Always replace [INSERT...] brackets with exact text
- For color control: add "strict color palette of black, orange, and white" at the end
- For graphic elements: add "thick outlines," "comic book style sound effects," or "geometric shapes"
- Brand colors: orange + black + white
