FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I charge my EVolve vehicle?

You can charge at home using a standard 220V outlet (Wallbox) which we install, or at growing public fast-charging stations across the country.

What is the warranty coverage?

We provide a comprehensive 5-year vehicle warranty and a dedicated 8-year warranty on the battery pack.

Can I test drive these vehicles?

Yes, we offer demo drives for qualified buyers. Use the "Book Demo Drive" button to schedule your session at our showroom.

Do you offer financing?

We work with major financial institutions in Trinidad and Tobago to facilitate seamless EV financing with competitive rates.

What about maintenance and parts?

As authorized partners, we have a dedicated service team and a growing inventory of genuine parts to ensure your vehicle stays on the road.
