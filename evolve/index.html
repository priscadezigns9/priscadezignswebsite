FAQ

How long is the warranty?

All EVolve vehicles come with a comprehensive 8-year or 150,000km battery warranty and a 5-year general vehicle warranty.

Where can I charge my EV?

We provide a complimentary high-speed home charging station with every purchase. Additionally, the growing network of public chargers across Trinidad ensures you're never far from a boost.

Can I book a demo drive today?

Absolutely. Use the Concierge button to connect directly via WhatsApp and schedule your exclusive demo experience.

What is the maintenance schedule?
