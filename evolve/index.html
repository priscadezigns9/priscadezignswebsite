EV MYTHS VS. REALITY

Myth

"Electric cars are dangerous in heavy rain or floods."

Reality

EVolve vehicles are IP67-rated. The battery and electrical systems are fully sealed, making them safer than gas engines which can "hydro-lock" in water.

Myth

"The battery will need replacing in 3 years."

Reality

Modern EV batteries are designed to outlast the car. We provide an 8-year warranty, and many batteries still hold 90% charge after 10 years.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the warranty on the battery?

Our manufacturers provide an industry-leading 8-year or 150,000km warranty on the battery pack.

How long does a full charge take?

With a high-speed DC charger, you can reach 80% in approximately 30 minutes. A standard home AC charger typically takes 8-10 hours.

Are there charging stations in Trinidad?

Yes, the public charging network is expanding rapidly. Every EV purchase also includes a professional home-charger installation.

What are the maintenance costs?

Electric vehicles have fewer moving parts, resulting in maintenance costs up to 70% lower than traditional internal combustion engines.
