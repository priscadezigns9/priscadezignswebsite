EV MYTHS VS. REALITY

Myth

"Electric cars are dangerous in heavy rain or floods."

Reality

EVolve vehicles are IP67-rated. The battery and electrical systems are fully sealed, making them safer than gas engines which can "hydro-lock" in water.

Myth

"The battery will need replacing in 3 years."

Reality

Modern EV batteries are designed to outlast the car. We provide an 8-year warranty, and many batteries still hold 90% charge after 10 years.
