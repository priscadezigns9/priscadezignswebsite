FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is the warranty on the battery?

Our manufacturers provide an industry-leading 8-year or 150,000km warranty on the battery pack, ensuring long-term sovereign performance.

How long does a full charge take?

With a high-speed DC charger, you can reach 80% in approximately 30 minutes. A standard home AC charger typically takes 8-10 hours for a full cycle.

Are there charging stations in Trinidad?

Yes, the public charging network is expanding rapidly. Every EV purchase also includes a professional home-charger installation.

What are the maintenance costs?

Electric vehicles have fewer moving parts, resulting in maintenance costs up to 70% lower than traditional internal combustion engines.
