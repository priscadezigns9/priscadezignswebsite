Paris SS26 Recap: Structured Etherealism & High-Street Leaks

Paris Fashion Week SS26 has officially redefined the silhouette for the upcoming year. The 'Structured Etherealism' trend dominated the runways, from Marc Jacobs to Chanel, merging rigid architectural forms with flowing, semi-translucent fabrics.

As street style becomes a major influence on high-street production, Couture Gallery is tracking the 'Leaked Designs' that are already hitting the digital marketplace. This season is about high contrast—combining the authority of structured couture with the vulnerability of ethereal draping.
