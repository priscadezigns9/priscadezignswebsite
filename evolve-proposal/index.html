Executive Summary

The Opportunity in Brief
EVOLVE is bringing the future of transportation to the Caribbean. Every unanswered inquiry is a sale that goes to someone else.

EVOLVE Mobility is the Caribbean's leading electric vehicle dealership — offering BYD, GAC, Leapmotor, Denza, Dongfeng, iCAUR, and Wuling vehicles to a market that is increasingly ready to make the switch. Buyers are researching online, messaging after hours, and expecting instant answers on pricing, range, charging, and financing.

Right now, when a potential buyer contacts EVOLVE at 8 PM with a question about the BYD Sealion 7 or wants to book a test drive for the weekend, they wait. That wait costs EVOLVE sales. A competitor — or simply the buyer's hesitation — fills the gap.

Prisca Dezigns proposes to close that gap permanently. This proposal outlines a tiered AI system that answers vehicle inquiries, qualifies buyers, books test drives, handles WhatsApp messages, manages email, and answers inbound calls — all automatically, 24 hours a day. EVOLVE's sales team focuses on closing confirmed, qualified buyers. The AI handles everything before that moment.

Section 03

The Problem

EV buyers in the Caribbean are ready — but they need answers fast. Every hour of delay is a sale at risk.

Issue 01
After-hours inquiries go unanswered
A buyer researching the Leapmotor C10 at 9 PM sends a WhatsApp message or fills in the contact form. No one responds until the next morning. By then, they have either moved on or visited a petrol-vehicle dealership that answered immediately.
Issue 02
Test drive requests are manual
Booking a test drive currently requires a back-and-forth conversation to confirm availability, the model, and the date. An AI can handle the entire booking flow automatically — confirming the vehicle, selecting a date, and sending a reminder — in under two minutes, any time of day.
Issue 03
EV objections slow the sales cycle
Range anxiety, battery reliability, charging availability, and brand unfamiliarity are the four objections every EV buyer raises. Right now a salesperson must handle each one manually. The AI handles every objection instantly, with accurate data, before the buyer ever speaks to a human.
Issue 04
Unqualified leads consume sales team time
Not every inquiry is a ready buyer. Sales staff currently spend time on tyre-kickers, researchers, and people asking basic questions about charging costs. The AI filters and qualifies all inquiries, so the sales team only speaks to buyers who are genuinely ready to purchase or book.
The Business Cost
Every unanswered inquiry after 4 PM is a potential sale that never reaches EVOLVE's sales team.
In a high-consideration purchase like an EV — where buyers research for weeks before committing — the first dealership to respond with accurate, helpful information wins. The AI gives EVOLVE that first-response advantage, around the clock.
