Immediate Cost Reduction
Fuel is one of the largest recurring fleet costs. Switching to electric cuts that cost by up to 70% from day one. No oil changes, fewer brake replacements, no transmission servicing.
🏆
Brand Leadership
As a national broadcaster, TTT has an opportunity to lead the conversation on sustainability in T&T. An EV fleet is a visible, public signal of that commitment — on every road, every day.
🔧
Local Support Infrastructure
EVOLVE vehicles are serviced by SBCS University-trained technicians. Genuine parts are supplied directly by EVOLVE — and where parts are not locally stocked, EVOLVE imports them directly from the manufacturer. TTT is not dependent on grey market parts or unverified workshops.
⚡
Overnight Charging
Fleet vehicles charge overnight at TTT's compound — arriving every morning at 100%. No fuel runs, no petrol station queues, no idle time spent filling up during operational hours.
📈
Asset Value Retention
BYD and GAC vehicles maintain strong resale values globally. Battery degradation averages just 2.3% per year — a 5-year-old EV still has over 88% of its original range.
🌍
Caribbean-First Movement
EVOLVE is building the Caribbean's EV infrastructure from the ground up. Early adopters get access to preferred pricing, priority service slots, and first access to new model arrivals.
Section 03

The Case for TTT

TTT Limited operates a fleet that serves reporters, executives, and production teams across Trinidad & Tobago. Every vehicle on that fleet is a fuel cost, a maintenance liability, and a missed opportunity to lead.

Current Fleet Pain Points

  • Recurring petrol costs that scale with fuel price volatility
  • Unplanned maintenance pulling vehicles off rotation
  • No EV infrastructure = competitive gap vs. regional peers
  • Brand perception misaligned with a modern, forward-looking media house
  • Missed CSR positioning in an increasingly sustainability-conscious market

What an EV Fleet Delivers

  • Predictable, low fuel cost — electricity rates are stable vs. petrol
  • Fewer breakdowns — EVs have no combustion engine to fail
  • Overnight charging at compound — vehicles ready every morning
  • Visible CSR signal — every branded EV on the road tells a story
  • Long-term asset protection — battery degradation under 3%/year
