The Problem

Caribbean landlords lose thousands every year to the same problems.

The same issues repeat across Trinidad, Jamaica, Barbados, and beyond. Tenura was built specifically to solve them.

💸

Late Rent Payments

Chasing tenants for rent every month is exhausting. Manual tracking leads to missed payments and cash flow problems that compound over time.

🚨

Bad Tenants

Without proper screening tools, landlords end up with tenants who damage property, stop paying, and take months to evict legally.

🔧

Maintenance Chaos

Requests come in via WhatsApp, phone calls, sticky notes. Nothing is tracked. Repairs get lost and tenants get frustrated — then they leave.

🏚️

Property Damage

No inspection records means no proof of damage when tenants leave. Landlords lose deposit disputes and spend thousands on avoidable repairs.

AI Intelligence

AI that works before problems happen.

Tenura's AI doesn't just automate — it predicts, detects, and prevents the issues that cost Caribbean landlords the most.

AI CORE

AI Tenant Risk Scoring

Before a tenant moves in, our AI analyses their application for red flags — income inconsistencies, previous rental behaviour, payment history patterns — and gives you a clear risk score so you can make smarter decisions.

FRAUD DETECTION

Document Fraud Detection

Fake pay stubs, doctored bank statements, and forged references are common in rental markets. Tenura's AI scans uploaded documents for manipulation markers and suspicious patterns — flagging fakes before they cost you.
