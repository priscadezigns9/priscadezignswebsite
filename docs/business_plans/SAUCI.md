# SAUCI — Business Plan

**Type:** Consumer App / Marketplace  
**Category:** Paid Companion Marketplace  
**Status:** Concept — naming finalized 2026-05-07  
**Built by:** Prisca Dezigns  
**Formerly known as:** YORUVI  

---

## 1. Purpose & Vision
Sauci is a paid companion marketplace. It connects people who want a date or social companion for any occasion with verified individuals who get paid to show up. Think Airbnb for dates — the companion sets their rate and availability, the booker pays through the app. Fun, safe, and unapologetically direct.

**One sentence:** Who you showing up with?

---

## 2. The Problem It Solves
- Nobody wants to show up to a wedding, work event, fete, or dinner solo
- Dating apps are for romance — there's no marketplace for paid social companionship
- Companions (Saucis) have no platform to monetize their social skills and time
- Existing escort/companion services are underground, unsafe, and stigmatized

---

## 3. How It Works
- **Dater** — browses verified Sauci profiles, filters by occasion, location, vibe, rate. Books and pays through the app.
- **Sauci (companion)** — creates a verified profile, sets their own rate, availability, and occasion types. Gets paid per booking.
- **Occasions** — weddings, fetes, work events, dinner dates, nights out, travel companion, anything public
- **Safety** — all bookings are public occasions only. ID-verified profiles. In-app messaging. Rating system both ways.

---

## 4. Target Audience
**Daters:**
- Professionals attending work/social events solo
- People newly single who don't want to show up alone
- Caribbean diaspora navigating social scenes in new cities
- Anyone who just wants a fun, paid, no-strings companion for a night

**Saucis (Companions):**
- Social, outgoing individuals who want to monetize their time
- Models, entertainers, social butterflies
- People looking for flexible income
- Ages 21-35, verified, professionally presented

**Markets:**
- T&T and Caribbean (launch)
- Caribbean diaspora — London, New York, Toronto, Miami (phase 2)
- Global (phase 3)

---

## 5. Revenue Model
- **Platform commission:** 20% of every booking (Sauci keeps 80%)
- **Premium Sauci:** $9.99/month — featured placement, priority visibility, verified badge
- **Boost:** $4.99 — push your profile to top of search for 24hrs
- **Premium Dater:** $5.99/month — unlimited messaging before booking, advanced filters
- **ID Verification:** $9.99 one-time fee for Sauci profiles
- **Corporate packages:** Custom — company events, team outings

## 10. Legal Considerations
- Terms of service must clearly define: public occasions only, no sexual services
- Age verification mandatory (21+)
- Payment processing via Stripe or similar
- Privacy policy — booking details confidential
- Separate from escort/adult services in all legal filings
