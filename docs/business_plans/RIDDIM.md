# RIDDIM — Business Plan

**Type:** Consumer App / Music Super-Platform  
**Category:** Music Streaming + AI Creation + Artist Discovery + Community  
**Status:** Concept — ready for development  
**Built by:** Prisca Dezigns  

---

## 1. Purpose & Vision

Riddim is the world's first AI-powered music super-app. It combines music discovery, song detection, AI music creation, artist streaming, community, and direct monetisation — all in one platform. Born in the Caribbean, built for the world.

**One sentence:** Hear it. Find it. Create it. Share it. Get paid.

---

## 2. The Problem It Solves

The music experience is broken and fragmented:
- You hear a song → go to Shazam to identify it
- You want to stream it → go to Spotify
- You want to create music → go to Suno
- You want to follow the artist → go to Instagram
- You want to support them → go to Venmo or Cash App
- Emerging artists earn almost nothing on Spotify (~$0.003/stream)
- Caribbean and global south artists are underrepresented on major platforms
- There is NO single platform that does all of this — especially not with AI at the core

---

## 3. The Solution — Riddim's 6 Pillars

### 🎵 1. Stream
- Full music streaming library (licensed + independent artists)
- Personalised AI-powered playlists and discovery
- Offline listening on premium

### 🔍 2. Detect
- Shazam-style song identification using AI audio fingerprinting
- Hear a song anywhere → open Riddim → instant ID → stream it immediately
- Detected songs auto-saved to your library

### 🤖 3. Create
- Suno-style AI music generation (text-to-music)
- AI lyric writing and co-writing
- AI mastering — upload a rough track, get a polished version
- AI remixing — take any track, remix it in a new genre or style
- Stem separation — isolate vocals, bass, drums from any song

### 🎙️ 4. Discover
- Platform for emerging and independent artists to upload and distribute
- AI-powered artist matching — "if you like X, discover Y"
- Caribbean and global south artists given featured placement
- Weekly new artist spotlight — curated emerging talent

### 💬 5. Connect
- Artist profiles with direct fan messaging
- Community rooms — genre-based chat, listening parties
- Live audio rooms — artists go live, fans tune in
- Collaborative playlists — build one with friends

### 💰 6. Earn
- Artists earn 70% of streaming revenue (vs Spotify's ~15-30%)
- Fan tipping — direct tips to artists, Riddim takes 5%
- AI creation revenue — if your AI track gets streamed, you earn
- Brand sync licensing — AI matches brands with artists for commercial use
- Merch integration — sell merch directly from your artist profile

## 11. Go-To-Market Strategy

1. **Caribbean first** — launch in T&T, Jamaica, Barbados, Guyana with local artist partnerships
2. **Diaspora activation** — target Caribbean communities in London, New York, Toronto
3. **Artist-led growth** — give artists tools so good they promote the platform themselves
4. **Carnival activation** — major launch campaign around T&T Carnival (February)
5. **Creator virality** — AI music generation goes viral on TikTok and Instagram
6. **Press angle** — "The platform that actually pays artists fairly"
