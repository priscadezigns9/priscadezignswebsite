# TROGON — Business Plan

**Type:** SaaS Platform  
**Category:** Social Media Management  
**Status:** In Development (MVP live at trogon-app.vercel.app)  
**Built by:** Prisca Dezigns  

---

## 1. Purpose & Vision
Trogon is a social media management platform built for small businesses, creators, and agencies — especially in the Caribbean and emerging markets — who need a powerful, affordable alternative to tools like Hootsuite and Buffer. The goal is to make professional social media management accessible to businesses that can't afford enterprise tools or a full-time social media manager.

**One sentence:** Trogon handles your social media so you can focus on running your business.

---

## 2. The Problem It Solves
- Small businesses post inconsistently because managing multiple platforms is time-consuming
- Existing tools (Hootsuite, Buffer, Sprout Social) are expensive and complex
- Caribbean and emerging market businesses are underserved by global SaaS tools
- Agencies managing multiple clients need a clean, efficient dashboard

---

## 3. Target Audience
**Primary:**
- Small business owners (retail, food, beauty, fitness, lifestyle) — Trinidad & Tobago, Caribbean, Latin America
- Freelance social media managers
- Micro-agencies (1-5 person teams)

**Secondary:**
- Regional brands scaling internationally
- Content creators managing multiple brand pages

---

## 4. Value Proposition
- All-in-one dashboard: compose, schedule, post, inbox, analytics
- AI caption generator + AI image generator built in
- Crypto payments (Binance, MetaMask, Trust Wallet) — no bank account required
- Caribbean-first pricing in USD, accessible globally
- Clean, modern UI built for non-technical users

---

## 5. Revenue Model
**Subscription (Monthly):**
| Plan | Price | Pages | Posts/Month |
|------|-------|-------|-------------|
| Basic | $299/mo | 1 page | 20 posts |
| Growth | $599/mo | 3 pages | 90 posts |
| Empire | $999/mo | Unlimited | Daily posting + ads |

**Future:**
- Annual plans (2 months free)
- White-label licensing for agencies
- API access tier

---

## 6. Platforms Supported
- ✅ Facebook (live)
- ✅ Instagram (via Facebook)
- ⏳ TikTok (coming soon)
- ⏳ LinkedIn (coming soon)
- ⏳ YouTube (coming soon)
- ⏳ X/Twitter (coming soon)

---

## 7. Brand Identity
- **Colors:** Dark (`#0d0f1a`) + Green (`#00C040`) + Blue (`#3B82F6`) + Red (`#B01020`)
- **Logo:** Trogon bird — circular, green background
- **Font:** Inter
- **Tone:** Professional, modern, clean — built for business
- **Tagline:** *"Grow your brand daily."*

---

## 8. Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS (single page app)
- **Backend/Auth/DB:** Supabase
- **AI:** Groq (LLaMA 3.3) for captions + video scripts; Pollinations for images
