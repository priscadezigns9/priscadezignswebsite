What is Prisca Dezigns? The Agency Built for Entrepreneurs Who Are Just Getting Started

Most agencies want established clients with big budgets. Prisca Dezigns is different — it was built specifically for the entrepreneur who has an idea, a hustle, or a growing business, and needs a partner who takes them seriously from day one.

The short answer

Prisca Dezigns is a full-service creative and digital agency. It handles branding, web design, app development, copywriting, social media management, and business operations — everything a growing business needs, available in one place.

The agency also builds its own tools: Velloq (web design), Moblync (app development), Cupyx (AI copywriting), Rowcell (bookkeeping), and Karjov (business management). These are available as standalone products or bundled into agency packages.

The longer answer — why it exists

Most entrepreneurs starting out face the same wall: they know they need a professional brand, a proper website, and systems to manage their business — but they don't know where to start, can't afford the agencies that serve larger companies, and don't have time to piece together ten different tools from ten different providers.

Prisca Dezigns was built to remove that wall. The agency's positioning is intentional: it serves the businesses that need help the most but get ignored the most.

"Building a brand that suits the personality of the owner. Making business easier for entrepreneurs, solo traders, ones just starting out — the ones that need to get it together and don't know where to start."

Who is it for?

  • Entrepreneurs launching their first business and needing a brand from scratch
  • Solo traders who have been operating informally and want to look professional
  • Small businesses that have outgrown their DIY setup and need proper infrastructure
  • Founders with an app or platform idea who need a design and development partner
  • Anyone who has been putting off "sorting out the business side" and finally wants to do it properly

What does Prisca Dezigns actually do?

Branding & Identity

Logo design, colour palette, typography, brand guidelines — everything that makes a business look and feel consistent. The approach is personality-first: the brand should feel like the person or people behind it, not generic.

Web Design & Development (Velloq)

Custom websites for any type of business — portfolio, service page, e-commerce, landing page. Built to load fast, look good on mobile, and rank in search engines.

App Development (Moblync)

Mobile app design and development for brands ready to build a product. From early concept to a fully designed and developed application.

AI Copywriting (Cupyx)

Content that sounds like the brand. Captions, website copy, email campaigns, ad text — generated in the business's own voice so it doesn't sound robotic or generic.

Bookkeeping (Rowcell)

Simple financial management for non-accountants. Track receipts, manage expenses, stay on top of the books without needing to hire a dedicated finance person.

Business Management (Karjov)

One dashboard for clients, projects, tasks, and operations. For business owners who are managing everything manually and need a system that keeps them organised.

Social Media Management

Content planning, creation, scheduling, and posting across platforms — handled as part of a full-service package.

How does it work?

Clients can come in at any stage. Some come with nothing but an idea and need everything built. Some already have a brand but need a website. Some need one specific tool. Prisca Dezigns offers standalone services, individual tool access, and full packages that cover the whole journey — all priced to be accessible to businesses that are still growing.

The process starts with a conversation. No lengthy onboarding forms, no jargon-heavy proposals. Just a direct conversation about what the business needs and what the path forward looks like.
