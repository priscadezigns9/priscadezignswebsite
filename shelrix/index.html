The Problem

Caribbean retail stores lose money every single day to the same problems.

Without the right tools, you're running your store blind — guessing stock levels, missing theft, and throwing away expired products that should have sold.

🕵️
Theft
Staff and customer theft goes undetected for months. No system to flag unusual patterns, voided transactions, or shrinkage that quietly drains your margins every single week.
📦
Stockouts
Popular items run out before new orders arrive. Customers walk out empty-handed and go to your competitor. Without forecasting, you're always reacting — never prepared.
🗑️
Expired Items
Products expire on your shelves because no one tracked the dates. Perishables, dairy, bread — pure loss, every week. The damage adds up to thousands in wasted stock annually.
💸
Poor Pricing
Without real-time margin data, you don't actually know what you're making on each product. Supplier costs change, markups get outdated, and profits quietly disappear from your books.
📋
Manual Ordering
Guessing what to reorder and when. Walking the aisles with a pen and paper. Calling suppliers without knowing your real stock levels. It's 2026 — there's no reason to still do this.
